# Vael

*The mainland continent of [[Vaeloris]]. The heart of the Mage-Purge Wars. Where most of the campaign takes place.*

## Geography at a Glance

- **Westward Sands.** Desert expanse along the western coast. [[Lux Æterna]] is hidden here, in a slot canyon.
- **Central foothills.** Rocky, scrub, dotted with First Age ruins. The [[Mining Camp]] and [[The Sorath Scar]] sit in these foothills.
- **The Dragon's Tooth Mountains.** Northern range. Home to the Starfall Peaks Observatory (Act II destination).
- **Barren Hills.** Eastern. Ritual sites. Act III destination.
- **Coastal settlements.** South coast — ports like Port Farrick. [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]]'s birthplace.

## Political State (TA 412)

Forty years into the **Mage-Purge Wars.** The [[Legio Null]] controls most major cities and patrols the roads between them. Settlements that harbor mages either comply, hide, or burn. [[Lux Æterna]] is one of the hidden ones.

## The Hidden Network

Beneath the political surface, a loose alliance of subversive groups coordinates quietly:

- **[[Silent Network]]** — Lothari dream-mystics. Long-view.
- **[[Shadewalkers]]** — information brokers. Not idealists.
- **Waldkyn wanderers** — forest-dwelling elves. Old-world.
- **Star-Veiled Passage** — the [[Star-Veiled Passage|route-keepers]] who move mages between settlements.

These groups do not trust each other. They also do not fight each other. It is an arrangement of mutual utility.
