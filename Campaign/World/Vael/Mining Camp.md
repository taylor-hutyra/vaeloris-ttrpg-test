# The Mining Camp

*Three days east of [[Lux Æterna]]. The scarred half-circle in the lee of a rocky outcrop. Site of the first encounter of Session 1.*

## Location

Roughly a mile east of a low, dark hill that contains the First Age ruin of Sorath — and [[The Sorath Scar]] beneath it, which spawned the [[Scorch-Wraiths]] that killed the crew. Three days' ride from [[Lux Æterna]] along the standard Lothari desert routes.

## Layout

A half-circle of six tents in the lee of a rocky outcrop. Fire-pit in the center. Mining tools stacked neatly — [[Torvin Dast|Torvin]] was that kind of foreman. Everything has been done right. Everything is also on fire, or was, until the fire ran out of things to burn.

### Zones That Matter

| Zone | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Tent half-circle** | Cover everywhere. Collapsed tents, crates, the outcrop wall. The burn-marks on the bodies here are geometric. |
| **Fire-pit (center)** | Residual mana pools here. Wraiths are drawn to it. One is feeding at session start. |
| **Mining pit (NE)** | Scaffolding, winch. A shaft at the base drops fifteen feet into black crystalline stone. **[[Ulzammar Band|The Band]] is at the bottom**, half-embedded in the wall. |
| **Eastern outcrop crack** | In Scene 1, nothing. In [[02-scene-the-rift-opens|Scene 2]], this is where the fissure opens and [[Valeth]] drags [[Vorn]] through. |

## After Chapter 1

The camp is left behind. The [[Scorch-Wraiths]] are dead. The exposed aether-quartz vein is harvestable. The fissure is sealed. The crew is either buried or left under the stars.

The location becomes a minor waypoint for later sessions — if the party returns for the aether-quartz (200 Sparks of raw crystal) or to revisit the bodies. Otherwise, wind and sand will take the camp within a month.
