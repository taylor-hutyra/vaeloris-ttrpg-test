# Star-Veiled Passage

*The underground mage-evacuation route running north through [[Vael]] — operated by Wandering Kin caravans, supported by the [[Silent Network]].*

## What It Is

A network of **safe-houses, caravan-scheduling, and night-travel corridors** that moves mages and other persecuted persons from the Westward Sands (and [[Lux Æterna]] specifically) north, through the foothills, up toward the Dragon's Tooth Mountains and beyond. The name comes from the Wandering Kin practice of traveling only on nights when the stars are veiled by cloud or dust — harder to be seen by scouts, harder to be tracked.

## Who Runs It

- **Wandering Kin caravans** — itinerant Lothari and mixed-peoples groups who trade along the route and carry passengers alongside their goods. They are paid in small gold or in debts to be repaid later. They do not carry [[Legio Null]] informants, because they have lost two caravans in twelve years to informants and have since developed a screening protocol that works.
- **[[Silent Network]]** — provides intelligence, advance warning, and some safe-houses.
- **Local allies** — individual smallholders along the route who will hide a wagon under a tarp for a night and ask no questions.

## Session Relevance

- **Mentioned in a rumor** in [[03-scene-downtime|Chapter 2 Scene 3]] — a Wandering Kin caravan spotted on the mountain road, moving fast, no trade goods. That caravan was running the Passage.
- **Session 4+ relevance.** After [[Lux Æterna]] falls, the Passage is the route the party will most plausibly use to escape northward toward the Dragon's Tooth Observatory — the [[The Architect|Architect]]'s Act II destination.
