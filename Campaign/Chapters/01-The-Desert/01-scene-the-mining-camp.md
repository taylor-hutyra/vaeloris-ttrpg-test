# SCENE 1 — THE MINING CAMP

**Budget: 5–10 minutes** (pickup and orientation)
**Cast on stage:** [[Yldroth]], [[Norseman]], [[Arden]]
**Location:** [[Mining Camp]]

---

## Where We Pick Up

The party's mini-session ended mid-combat. Three **[[Scorch-Wraiths]]** are still standing. The party has taken some strain and little damage. One tent is burning. The smell of superheated glass is everywhere.

## Quick Scene-Setting (read aloud or paraphrase)

> *You've had about forty seconds to think since the wraiths came out of the fire-pit. Torvin Dast's crew — or what's left of them — is in the sand around you. Six tents, two collapsed. The burn-marks on the bodies are geometric, like someone drew the killing wound with a ruler and a protractor. Three of those things are still moving. One of them is feeding on the fire-pit. The other two are on you.*

## Running the Combat — Opening Round

- **Wraith A:** *Geometric Eyes* on highest-Defense PC ([[Yldroth]] likely). Coordination ◆◆, fail = 8 fire.
- **Wraith B:** engages nearest PC ([[Norseman]] or [[Yldroth]]). Glass claws.
- **Wraith C:** in the fire-pit, feeding. Recovers 2 strain. Obvious target.

Let the party act. One round, maybe two. **Do not resolve the fight here** — Scene 2 interrupts.

## PC Flavor Prompts (optional, if anyone wants a roleplay beat mid-combat)

- **[[Yldroth]]** — if he lays a healing strike on [[Norseman]] or [[Arden]], describe the ki briefly: *"Your fist connects, and the bruise underneath closes like a hand relaxing."*
- **[[Norseman]]** — he is a mage in a world that burns mages. Describe the weight of casting in the open, outside [[Lux Æterna]]'s wards, with nobody around to witness and therefore nobody to report.
- **[[Arden]]** — his mysticism is subtle by design. A flick of the hand, a whispered syllable, results that look almost like luck. He is not flashy. He has spent a lifetime learning not to be flashy.

## Scorch-Wraith Reference

Full stat block in [[Scorch-Wraiths]]. Inline summary:

**Rival · Wounds 8 · Strain 6 · Soak 3**
- Brawl 2, Perception 1
- Glass claws: Brawl / Dmg 6 / Crit 3 / Engaged / Burn 1
- Immune to fire · Vulnerable to cold (+2 dmg, disables beam 1 round) · Blindsight
- **Geometric Eyes** (maneuver, heat-beam line Short): Coord ◆◆, fail = 8 fire
- **Shatter** (on death): Coord ◆ or 3 piercing at Engaged

## Transition Out

After one or two rounds of combat, **cut to Scene 2 — The Rift Opens.** Do not wait for the party to kill a wraith first. The interrupt is more dramatic mid-combat.
