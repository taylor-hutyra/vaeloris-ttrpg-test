## Chapter Summary

The party was sent by [[Tajsh Thale]] to investigate a mining crew three days east of [[Lux Æterna]] that had stopped reporting in. They arrived at dusk to find the camp destroyed by **[[Scorch-Wraiths]]** — aberrations spawned from [[The Sorath Scar]] beneath the nearby First Age ruin of Sorath. The fight was already underway when the chapter opens.

Mid-combat, the wraiths will freeze and screech as a rift in reality tears open beneath the sand. Two figures will emerge — [[Valeth]] and [[Vorn]] — before the fissure seals. The party will finish the wraiths together, recover the **[[Ulzammar Band]]** from the bottom of the mining pit, and find **[[Torvin Dast|Torvin Dast's]] journal** describing the crew's fate and the three-ring symbol Tajsh will later recognize.

The chapter ends with the ride back to [[Lux Æterna]].

~**50–55 minutes** total table time. Scenes 1–3 are paced by combat; 4 and 5 are the emotional beats; 6 is a breather into [[Lux Æterna]].

## GM Reminders

- **Let [[Valeth]] lead the party to the [[Ulzammar Band|Band]].** He has heard the song for four days. He owns that beat.
- **Read the journal aloud.** The cadence is doing emotional work.
- **Hold Tajsh's revelation for Chapter 2.** Nobody learns the symbol's meaning here.
- **Do not heal strain between scenes 3 and 5.** The combat's cost should carry into the Band and the journal.
