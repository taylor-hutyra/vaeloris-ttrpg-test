# SCENE 3 — DOWNTIME

**Budget: 15–20 minutes** (can contract to 10 if the table is ready to move on)
**Cast:** all five PCs; shopkeepers and contacts as the PCs seek them out
**Location:** [[Lux Æterna]] — [[Veiled Market]], shops, contacts' homes

---

## How to Run

The next morning is gray. The settlement looks almost normal — lanterns stabilized, people in the [[Veiled Market|market]], dogs back inside. But you can tell by the way people aren't quite looking at each other that last night wasn't normal either.

[[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] has sent word: *"Early. But not too early."* The party has roughly **two in-game hours** before they need to be in his office for [Scene 4](04-scene-the-revelation-and-the-flicker.md).

Let them do what they want with the time. Below is the menu.

---

## THE SHOPS

Prices in [[Lux Æterna]] run **150%** of standard for common goods, **200%** for anything enchanted. **Mana-Crystals (Sparks)** are the local currency. 1 Spark ≈ 1 standard unit.

### Hask's Alchemy Workshop

See [Scene 2](02-scene-the-evening.md) for Hask himself.

| Good | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Healing draught (minor) | 15 Sparks | Restore 3 strain or 2 wounds. |
| Healing draught (proper) | 40 Sparks | Restore 5 strain or 4 wounds. Limit 2 in stock. |
| Ash-Root paste | 10 Sparks | Numbs pain for 1 hour. ◼ on Resilience checks against cold. |
| Smokebomb (clay jar) | 20 Sparks | Short-range cloud, lasts 3 rounds. |
| Wyrm's-tear oil | 25 Sparks | Coats a weapon; first attack next combat adds ◼◼. |
| Antidote (mundane) | 8 Sparks | Works on common desert venoms. |

**Hask may barter** if the PCs offer alchemy ingredients (aether-quartz dust is particularly valuable — 30 Sparks worth of credit per thumb-sized shard).

### Selin's Shop — Needle & Thread

[[Selin Voss|Selin]] is tired but functional today. The Stitching is stable but she is watching it.

| Good | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Desert cloak (standard) | 30 Sparks | ◼ on Stealth in sand terrain. |
| Mage-Weave cloak scrap | 80 Sparks | ◼◼ on Stealth in any terrain; fragile (ruined on any combat wound). |
| Boot repair / re-sole | 5 Sparks | 1-day turnaround. |
| Pack repair | 3 Sparks | While you wait. |
| Clan-marked insignia (Dwarven) | 10 Sparks | Useful for [[Vorn]] if he wants to wear his grandmother's clan mark openly. |

### The Veiled Market — Common Goods

For anything else, have the PCs roll **Streetwise (Cunning), Easy ◆** to find a stall. On success, they can buy:

- Rations (1 week) — 20 Sparks
- Waterskin — 5 Sparks
- Rope (50 ft) — 10 Sparks
- Lantern + oil (1 week) — 15 Sparks
- Simple weapon (club, dagger, sling) — 15–40 Sparks
- Leather armor (repaired) — 80 Sparks
- Camel (reliable) — 150 Sparks
- Maps (regional) — 20–60 Sparks

### Relic Vendors

[[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]]'s office deals relic-hunting work, but the [[Veiled Market|market]] also has **two independent fixers** who take small commissions. Mention them if a PC asks about selling loot. (For Session 1, the party shouldn't sell anything important. Save that for Session 2.)

## CONTACTS

Each PC has a starting contact from their background (see [[_meta/the-broken-harmonies|the campaign doc]] for the full list). Give each player a few minutes with their contact if they want. Below are flavor prompts — adapt to the PC's actual background.

### [[Valeth]] — Starting Contact

Needs a contact assigned. Options, GM's choice or player's pick:

- **A Waldkyn refugee in the Warrens** — recognizes his dialect and flinches at it, then invites him in for tea.
- **A Lothari trader named Elara** — dreamed of him last month. Says *"You were walking out of a door that wasn't there."* She will meet him again.
- **Nobody yet** — he has been wandering. His contact builds over the course of Act I.

### [[Vorn]] — Starting Contact

- **An old Thulkorûn stonemason named Dorran** in the Warrens. Looks at his clan-mark for a long time. Silently pours him a cup of something dark. Does not speak for the rest of the conversation. At the end, before [[Vorn]] leaves, Dorran says: *"Your grandmother. She stopped here once. Long ago. Before this was a settlement. I was a boy. She said your name. I remember your name."* Does not elaborate.

### [[Yldroth]], [[Norseman]], [[Arden]]

Their backgrounds have established contacts — adapt from [[_meta/the-broken-harmonies|the campaign doc]]. Spend 2–3 minutes with each if the player wants. Or skip.

## Special: [[Old Cira|Cira]]-Meditation for [[Valeth]]

If [[Valeth]] wants to sit with [[Old Cira|Cira]] — a private offering only. No check required, but the beat only triggers if he comes to her.

She accepts a long silence before speaking. When she does, she says:

> *"The song you were following was never yours. It was calling you. There is a difference. You know the difference now, because it stopped."*
>
> *"Listen to me. When a thing stops calling, it is about to arrive."*

She does not explain. She returns to her humming. Let the player make of it what he will.
