# SCENE 1 — ARRIVAL

**Budget: 10 minutes**
**Cast:** all five PCs, [[Tajsh Thale]], [[Old Cira]] (brief)
**Location:** [[Lux Æterna]] — slot canyon approach, [[Veiled Market]], [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh's office]], the Vault

---

## Read-Aloud — The Slot Canyon

> *The sun has been down an hour by the time you reach the slot canyon. Two sandstone pillars. A narrow slot. A quarter-mile of stone walls. And then the lanterns bloom out of the dark, and [[Lux Æterna]] is there, waiting for you.*
>
> *Except it isn't, exactly. The lanterns are flickering wrong. One steady, one steady, flicker, flicker-flicker, steady. The [[Veiled Market|market]] is quieter than it should be — not silent, just *careful*. People are not arguing tonight. That isn't normal.*
>
> *From the mouth of the Warrens, someone is humming a single sustained note. Low. Long. Not quite a pitch a human throat should be able to hold. The note has been going for a while.*

## What Everyone Sees, Tonight

The settlement is **holding its breath.** Drop any of these details into whatever the party does first:

- Lanterns asymmetrically stuttering.
- Well water, at the [[Veiled Market]] center, tastes metallic.
- [[Selin Voss|Selin]] is up a ladder against the canyon wall, retouching a wavering Stitching glyph with a chisel and a pot of enchanted ink, muttering to herself.
- Three of four working dogs refuse to come inside.
- [[Old Cira|Cira]] sits outside the Vault (below [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh's]] office), humming that sustained note, facing the Warrens.

## [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] Takes the Band

First stop — up to [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh's]] office above the Vault. He is in his chair behind the desk. He watches the new faces ([[Valeth]], [[Vorn]]) enter and does not speak until the door closes behind them.

### Read-Aloud — The Band on His Desk

> *[[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] gestures at the two chairs. There are only two, for five of you. He waits to see who sits down. He does not ask the new ones their names. He will, eventually. Not yet.*
>
> *You set the bundle on his desk. He unwraps the corner of the wool. He sees the dark crystal and the pale metal veining. He does not touch it.*
>
> *The candle on his desk — the one that's been burning all evening — stops flickering. The flame is still lit. It is not moving. He watches it for a long second. Then he picks up the candle and carries it across the room and sets it on a shelf near the door. He comes back. He sits down.*
>
> *He says: "Give me tonight with this. Come back in the morning."*

### If the Party Explains the New Arrivals

[[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] looks at [[Valeth]] and [[Vorn]]. He says, flatly:

> *"They're with you? Fine. They eat with you. They sleep where you sleep. If they're what they say, we're lucky. If they're not — you're my trap."*

**He does not yet ask about the journal.** He will read it tonight. The revelation is [Scene 4](04-scene-the-revelation-and-the-flicker.md).

### If the Party Asks About the Wrongness

[[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] doesn't know. He's unsettled too. *"Something's off tonight. Could be the Band. Could be [[Old Cira|Cira]] reading the wind wrong again. [[Warden Sabel|Sabel's]] on the outlook. If anything's coming, he'll see it."*

### Downtime Setup

As the PCs leave his office, [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] says: *"You all need food and a bed. Tell [[Marta Dustborn|Marta]] to put it on my tab. And — tomorrow morning. Early."*

## The Vault — [[Old Cira|Cira]]'s Line

As the party descends the stairs past the Vault entrance, [[Old Cira|Cira]] is sitting against the wall. Eyes closed. Humming the sustained note.

**If [[Valeth]] passes near her**, she stops humming. Opens her eyes. Looks up at him. Says, very softly:

> *"Two of you are walking. Only one came back."*

She returns to humming. Does not clarify.

Let the player sit with it. **This is a gift. Do not explain it.**

### If Another PC (Not [[Valeth]]) Asks Her What She Meant

> *(She does not look up. She says, without inflection:) "Ask me again in a year. I will remember by then. Or I will not. Either way."*

### [[Old Cira|Cira]] and [[Arden]]

If the [[Arden]] player wants to linger — there's a beat for him. [[Old Cira|Cira]] looks at him for a long moment. Not with recognition. With a kind of *politeness* — she is being careful not to name something. She says nothing to him. She does not need to. **Pass the [[Arden]] player a private note:**

> *She sees you. She knows. She is choosing not to say so. Out loud, to anyone.*

This is an [[Arden]]-only moment. Do not narrate it to the table.

## Transition

The party disperses into the evening — to eat, to rest, to meet contacts. Cut to [Scene 2 — The Evening](02-scene-the-evening.md).
