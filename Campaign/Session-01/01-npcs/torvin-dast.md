# Torvin Dast (deceased)

**Role this session:** The voice of the journal. The crew's conscience. A ghost the players never meet.
**Appears in:** Beat 3 (as a corpse + as a written voice)

---

## Thumbnail

- Human male, 50s. Veteran relic-hunter. Worked for Tajsh for 18 years.
- Foreman of the mining expedition the party was sent to find.
- Married, childless. His wife Esha lives in the Warrens. She does not yet know he is dead.
- Handwriting is cramped, practical, and unexpectedly tender when the subject allows.

## What He Carried

- Leather-bound journal (sand-stained, in his vest pocket — survives the wraith fire because he was shielding it with his body when he died)
- Half-burned mining report
- A small brass compass his wife gave him, engraved *"Come home."*
- A pouch of three hardtack biscuits — the crew's last food

## What the Journal Reveals

See [05-handouts/foremans-journal.md](../05-handouts/foremans-journal.md) for the full read-aloud version. Key plot beats:

- **Day 4:** the abandoned mine entrance, the chamber with the pedestal, the three-ring symbol, the book across the chasm, the giant bats.
- **Day 7:** the dwarf PC begins hearing the song. *"He never hums. He said he didn't realize he was doing it."*
- **Day 10:** the black stone under the crystal vein.
- **Day 11:** the dwarf's humming is louder. *"He said it is not his humming. He said that twice in a row and didn't notice he said it twice."*
- **Day 12:** the dwarf strikes the deep cut, the seam opens, he is gone. *"The hole is not a hole."*
- **Day 13 (fragment):** *"There is something in the dark near the stones. I don't think it followed us from—"*

## The Tenderness Beats (the entries that make the deaths hurt)

- **Day 1:** *"Six of us. Rozen is already complaining about his boots."*
- **Day 3:** *"Good crew. I like this crew."*
- **Day 9:** *"Tomas broke a tooth on hardtack. I owe him a drink. He says two. We are agreed on one."*
- **Day 12 late:** *"Rozen won't stop crying. Maret won't speak. Tomas is sitting on a crate with his knife out. We are not miners anymore. We are witnesses."*

**Do not skip these entries.** They are the emotional load-bearing beams of Beat 3. The dwarf PC hears these names. His people.

## How He Died

Scorch-Wraith attack, two nights before the party arrived. His body is one of the six — identifiable by the brass compass in his hand. He was standing over Rozen's body, trying to shield him, when the wraith's *Geometric Eyes* burn caught them both.

## If the Dwarf PC Asks About Torvin

He was a good foreman. Fair with pay. Hard on latecomers. Made the crew stop to bury a stranger they found in the sand once — said *"if that were me out here, someone would have to."* The dwarf remembers him.

## Post-Session

Someone has to tell **Esha Dast** that her husband is not coming back. Tajsh will ask the party to do it. This is Session 2 business. Remember it. Don't forget her name.
