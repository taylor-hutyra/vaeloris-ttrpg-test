# Tajsh Thale

**Role this session:** Employer. Journal catalyst. Mission-giver.
**Appears in:** Beat 3 (offstage — sent the crew), Beat 5 (receives Band), Beat 6 (reads the journal)

---

## Thumbnail

- Human male, mid-50s, weathered. Eyes that have read too many contracts.
- Former Shadewalker. Left the Guild on bad terms but voluntarily.
- Runs Lux Æterna's relic-hunting operation. Employs the party.
- Sharp, pragmatic, morally flexible. Genuinely cares about the settlement; will still cut a corner to keep it breathing.

## Voice

Short sentences. Dry. Says exactly what he means, except when he doesn't want you to know he's afraid — then he says a little more than he needs to.

> *"You want the long version or the short? I've got time for short."*
> *"That's either a god or the best con I've ever seen. Either way, I'd listen."*

## What He Knows

- **About the Band:** nothing, yet. Can feel it's wrong. Will feel worse once it sits on his desk for a night.
- **About the three-ring symbol:** two prior sightings in his life. Shadewalker archive, age 22 — a sealed crate labeled *"Ulzammar provenance — do not acquire, do not transport."* Second sighting: Barren Hills ruin, paid to forget. He never learned what the symbol *meant.* He has been curious for thirty years.
- **About the Architect:** will not learn of it until after the scene. Will be awed. Will also be suspicious. Good.
- **About the party's new arrivals:** nothing, but he is a Shadewalker. He reads faces.

## What He Won't Say Out Loud

- He is afraid. The Band on his desk is the first thing in his life that ever made him wish he hadn't been curious.
- He already suspects the book on the pedestal and the Band are matched pieces. He doesn't want the party to understand the urgency until they've already said yes.

## Key Scene Moments

**Beat 5 — Receiving the Band**
He takes it, sets it on his desk. A candle on the desk **freezes mid-flicker.** He sees it. Says nothing. Picks up the candle and carries it across the room. Returns. *"Give me tonight with this. Come back in the morning."*

**Beat 6 — The Revelation**
See [beat-6-tajsh-revelation.md](../03-scenes/beat-6-tajsh-revelation.md). The monologue beginning *"I've seen that symbol twice in my life…"* is his load-bearing line of the night. Deliver it slowly.

## How He Treats the Party

**Trusted** (4 on the 5-tier reputation scale). He has chosen them. He will not say so. He trusts them with the Band because he has no one better, which in his accounting is the same as trusting them completely.

## If a Player Asks About His Shadewalker Past

He answers, briefly, if pressed. *"I left. They preferred I hadn't. We came to an arrangement."* Will not elaborate further this session. Save that for a quiet scene in a later downtime.
