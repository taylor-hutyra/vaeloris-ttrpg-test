# Lux Aeterna

**A hidden mage-refuge in a box canyon in the Westward Sands of Vael. Population roughly 340. Cloaked from the sky by a network of mirror-charms and a layer of enchanted silk called the Mage-Weave.**

## The Approach

The canyon is invisible until you are inside it. The Lothari route the party knows threads between two identical-looking sandstone pillars and drops abruptly into a slot canyon that opens, after a quarter-mile of stone walls, into the settlement itself. Coming home feels like crossing a threshold. Lanterns bloom out of the dark.

Tonight, the lanterns are stuttering.

## Read-Aloud — Arrival (Beat 5 opener)

> *The sun has been down an hour by the time you ride in. The lanterns are lit — the strings of them that run from the tavern to the barracks, and the single standing lantern at the well, and the warm squares of light from Hask's windows. But they aren't steady. One flickers, two burn clean, one flickers, one goes almost out and then flares back up. You've lived here long enough to know this isn't normal.*
>
> *People in the market have stopped arguing with each other. Nobody is shouting. That isn't normal either.*
>
> *Someone — you can't see who, it's too dark — is humming. A single sustained note. It takes you a moment to realize it's coming from the mouth of the Warrens.*

## Key Locations (in Beat 5 order — the route the party will naturally walk)

### The Veiled Market

The commercial heart, built into the widest part of the canyon. Stalls cluster around a central well. Mana-Crystal haggling. Spiced flatbread. Argument that did not happen tonight.

**Tonight's detail:** the well's water tastes metallic. A crowd has gathered. See [Beat 5](../03-scenes/beat-4-5-return-and-city.md) for the confrontation over Petra.

### The Dusty Lantern

The tavern. Marta's place. Normally packed at this hour. Tonight: half-full. People drinking slowly. The lantern strings behind the bar are out of rhythm.

### Hask's Workshop

Low stone building at the canyon's north wall. Smells of herbs, sulfur, and tea. Hask is on a stool watching a potion that will not cool.

### Selin's Shop (Needle & Thread)

Cramped tailor's shop doubling as the Mage-Weave repair station. Selin is **not inside** — she's up a ladder in the canyon, patching a failing section of the Weave by lantern light.

### The Vault (Tajsh's strongbox annex, beneath his office)

Reinforced stone room where the Band was briefly stored before Tajsh moved it to his desk. Old Cira is sitting outside it. Humming.

### Tajsh's Office

Above the Vault, entered through a door off the upper market. Desk. Two chairs. Ledger. Kettle. Candle that tonight has stopped flickering.

### The Warrens

Pre-settlement tunnels carved into the canyon wall. Some residents live down here. Some storage. And, at the deepest, oldest bend, a chamber that the settlement has never had a reason to use.

In Beat 7, that chamber is where the Architect will appear. See [the-deep-chamber.md](deep-chamber.md).

## The Settlement's Tonight-State

Use this shortlist to lace into any scene the party walks into during Beat 5. These are all happening **simultaneously**:

- **Lanterns stutter.** Asymmetric, unpredictable.
- **Water tastes metallic.** Growing complaints from the well.
- **Mage-Weave stutters.** Selin can feel it. Nobody else can articulate what's wrong but they know.
- **Dogs are uneasy.** Three of the settlement's four working dogs have refused to come inside.
- **Candles near the Vault freeze** mid-flicker, then resume, unpredictably.
- **Cira is humming** one sustained note. She is facing the Warrens.

The players should feel the settlement is **holding its breath.** None of these effects is dangerous alone. Together, they are the room going wrong.

## GM Note — Geography in Play

You do not need to track exact distances tonight. Lux Aeterna in Session 1 is a **vibe**, not a map. If a player wants to be somewhere, they are there within a minute's walk. The only location that requires navigation is the Warrens descent in Beat 7, and that is narrated, not mapped.

Maps exist for later sessions. Tonight, trust the mood.
