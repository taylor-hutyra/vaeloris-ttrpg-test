# Legio Null

*The continent's mage-hunting military order. Forty years into the Mage-Purge Wars and still winning.*

## Summary

The Legio Null is the military and bureaucratic arm of the Decree of Prohibition — the law, passed 65 years ago, that made mortal magic punishable by death. They are not an informal militia. They are a professional, funded, long-established institution with uniforms, ranks, training academies, and an internal culture.

Their public mission: *eliminate mortal magic from Vaeloris.* Their actual function: power consolidation — every mage they burn is a competitor removed from the continent's political economy.

## Structure (brief)

- **High Arbiter** — supreme commander, based at Castellum.
- **Commanders** — field officers, each with a regional remit.
- **Sand-Riders / Storm-Riders / Woods-Riders** — terrain-specialized patrol squads.
- **Inquisitors** — interrogators and magic-detectors.
- **Ash-Wardens** — executioners.

## How They Operate

They are **methodical.** They are not cruel for cruelty's sake — they are cruel because cruelty produces compliance. They keep records. They track. They take notes. They are, in the specific sense of the word, professional.

This is what makes them dangerous. They do not make mistakes because of passion. They make mistakes because their model of the world is incomplete.
