# Silent Network

*Lothari dream-mystics. Underground. Ancient. Carry messages through sleep.*

## Summary

The Silent Network is the active limb of a Lothari mystical tradition stretching back before the First Age. Its members are **dream-walkers** — mystics who can share dreams with one another across any distance. This lets them coordinate without letters, couriers, or fixed meeting places. A Lothari dream-walker in [[Lux Æterna]] can speak to a Lothari dream-walker in Novaterra within a single night.

They use this capability quietly. They move refugees. They track [[Scars of Dissonance|Scars]]. They relay warnings to the [[Legio Null]]'s targets. They do not claim credit. They do not lead campaigns. They are, by design, very difficult for outsiders to prove exist.

## Leadership

The **Council of Dreamers** — five mystics, always five, whose identities are not public. Their decisions are made in shared sleep and transmitted to the network by those present in the dream. When a Council member dies, the other four select a replacement; the dream-network votes.

## Relevance to Session 1

- **[[Old Cira]]** is a ranking operative. She was placed in [[Lux Æterna]] in SA:1487, specifically to watch [[The Sorath Scar]]. Twenty-five years of observation. The party has no idea.
- The Network has been quietly **rerouting Lothari caravans around the foothills** for the last year, because the Scar has been showing increased activity. This shows up in the rumor table in [[03-scene-downtime|Chapter 2 Scene 3]].
- The Network will assist the party in modest ways if contacted — routes, intelligence, dream-warnings. They will not fight their wars.
