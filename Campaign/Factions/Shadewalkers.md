# Shadewalkers

*The continent's oldest information brokerage. Morally flexible. Institutionally patient. Not on any side but their own.*

## Summary

The Shadewalkers are the archivists and information brokers of Vaeloris. They predate most existing nations. Their membership is small, their reach is large, and their institutional memory is extraordinary.

They do not fight wars. They do not take political positions. They sell information — accurately, discreetly, and expensively — to whoever pays. Their reputation for neutrality is the basis of their business model, which means they guard it ruthlessly. A Shadewalker who tells a lie can be killed by their own guild within a week.

They are not friends. They are not enemies. They are a service.

## Relevance to Session 1

- **[[Tajsh Thale]]** is a former Shadewalker. He left eighteen years ago on bad terms but voluntarily. He still has contacts. He does not always know what they do with the information he passes them.
- The **three-ring symbol** on the pedestal, and on the [[Ulzammar Band|Band]], is classified in Shadewalker archives as *"Ulzammar provenance — do not acquire, do not transport, report to senior archivist immediately."* Tajsh saw this classification once, in a sealed crate, as a young man. He has spent thirty years curious about it.
- Once the party has the Band, the Shadewalker Inner Circle will eventually learn of it. Their response — probably dispatching the **Pale Ledger** — is a Session 3+ concern.
