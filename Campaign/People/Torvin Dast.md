# Torvin Dast (deceased)

*Foreman. Veteran relic-hunter. The voice of the journal.*

## Summary

Torvin worked [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh's]] relic-hunting operations for eighteen years. Fair with pay. Hard on latecomers. The kind of foreman who stopped his crew to bury a stranger they found in the sand, because *"if that were me out here, someone would have to."*

He led the six-person mining crew that discovered the aether-quartz vein east of [[Lux Æterna]]. He also led them past the abandoned mine, past the pedestal, past the three-ring symbol, back to the crystal. On Day 12 of the expedition, [[Vorn]] struck the deep cut and fell through a seam. On Day 13, [[Scorch-Wraiths]] came from [[The Sorath Scar]] beneath the ruin, drawn by the exposed [[Ulzammar Band]].

He died two nights before the party arrived, trying to shield Rozen from a Wraith's *Geometric Eyes* beam. His journal survived because he was lying on it.

## What He Left Behind

- **His journal** — see [[handout-the-journal]] (when compiled). The load-bearing narrative document of Session 1.
- **A brass pocket-compass** — engraved *"Come home"* on the back. Gift from Esha. Still functional. Grants ◼ on desert Survival checks to whoever carries it.
- **A wife who does not know yet.** Esha Dast lives in the Warrens. [[Tajsh Thale|Tajsh]] will ask the party to tell her, in Session 2. Remember her name.

## How the Party Meets Him

As a corpse, in [[04-scene-the-camp-aftermath|Chapter 1, Scene 4]]. Collapsed over Rozen. Vest partly burned. Journal pokes out. Brass compass clenched in his hand. [[Vorn]] will recognize him.
