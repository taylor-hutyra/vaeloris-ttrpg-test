# Valeth

*Aen'valar wanderer · survivor of a [[Scars of Dissonance|Scar]] · the one the song found first*

## Summary

Valeth joined a rift-walking crew roughly four years ago, tasked with entering a [[Scars of Dissonance|Scar of Dissonance]] and killing the fiend anchoring it. His crew was wiped out. He spent what felt like **six to ten years** inside the rift. Outside, only three to four years passed. He learned the rift's rules one companion at a time.

A Waldkyn Song-Ward apprentice in his crew taught him to hum a specific sustained note into an aether-quartz shard — creating a small field of tonal dissonance that lesser fiends cannot cross. He has been using this trick ever since. He has 7 uses remaining at the start of the campaign. After [[02-scene-the-rift-opens|Chapter 1 Scene 2]], he has 4.

He emerged from the rift into the desert two weeks ago. He has been following a single sustained song westward for the last four days. He does not know what the song is. He only knows it is the clearest shape in a life that has not had shape since before he entered the rift.

## The Anchor

A palm-sized shard of aether-quartz wrapped in sinew. Cracked. Warm.

**Use:** action, once per scene. Humming a sustained note creates a field that fiends/Scar-touched entities cannot cross at Short range for 3 rounds. Each use cracks the quartz further.

**Charges:** 7 at campaign start; 4 after Chapter 1 Scene 2 (he burned 3 tearing a seam back to the real world).

**Replacement:** a new anchor can be crafted from raw aether-quartz (such as the vein exposed in the [[Mining Camp]]) with a **Knowledge — Arcana or Mechanics ◆◆◆** and a week's work.

## The New Trick (GM-visible, player-aware)

During the rescue of [[Vorn]], Valeth discovered that a different tonal pattern — three sharp descending notes against the sustained tone — can **rip open a seam** between realities. He used it to escape the rift. The cost was three Anchor uses and a cracked quartz. He does not know:

- Whether he can do it again.
- Whether he can target where the seam opens.
- Whether doing so attracts the attention of what lives in Scars.

He is afraid of all three answers.
