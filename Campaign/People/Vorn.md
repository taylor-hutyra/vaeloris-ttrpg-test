# Vorn

*Dwarf tank · lost crew member · survivor of a seam*

## Summary

Vorn was the pick-man on [[Torvin Dast]]'s six-person mining crew, employed by [[Tajsh Thale]] to assess a promising aether-quartz seam three days east of [[Lux Æterna]]. On Day 12 of the expedition, Vorn struck the deep cut and his pick did not crack the stone — it *opened* it. He fell through a seam into [[The Sorath Scar]]. Three days later, [[Valeth]] pulled him out.

He emerged into the desert alongside Valeth on the night the party found what remained of his crew. He has spent the evening since then trying to understand what happened to him, and to bury — or at least witness — the people who were alive when he went down the shaft.

## What He Carries

- A pickaxe from the crew. Serviceable as a weapon.
- Clan-marked boots (Thulkorûn lineage by style; player may name the specific clan).
- A stone sigil carved with his clan mark, worn on a cord under his shirt.

## Grandmother's Memory

When Vorn was very small, his grandmother sketched a symbol in ash on the hearthstone — three interlocking rings with a dark point at the center. She looked at it for a long time. Then she wiped it clean with her hand and said, under her breath:

> *"The Measure, the Setting, the Stone. Do not speak the fourth."*

She would not explain. She died when he was twelve. He did not think of her phrase in forty years. He thinks of it now.

*(In play: confirmed by a **Knowledge — Lore ◆◆◆** check in Chapter 1, Scene 4. A Triumph adds the word ***"Ulzammar."***)*
