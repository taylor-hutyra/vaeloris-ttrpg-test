# Arden

*Drakona Mystic · descendant of the Sleeping Wings · hides his nature beneath his skin*

## Summary

Arden appears, to casual eyes, fully human. He is not. He is **Drakona** — a descendant of the line of the Sleeping Wings — and has spent his life concealing his scales, his draconic heritage, and his true capabilities. His mysticism, by long habit, is subtle: a flick of the hand, a whispered syllable, results that look like luck. He has a lifetime of practice in not being flashy.

## What Others Know

- **[[Old Cira]]** sees him. She recognizes what he is. She will not say so out loud, to him or to anyone. The restraint is the communication.
- **[[The Architect]]** also sees him. In [[05-scene-the-architect|Chapter 2 Scene 5]], the Architect addresses him by a lineage name — *"Son of the Sleeping Wings"* — and explicitly *declines* to speak the full truth in front of his companions. This is calculated: a gift of discretion that doubles as leverage. The player will have to decide what to do with it.

## Sensitivity

As a mystic with some attunement to scarred places, Arden can sense something on [[Valeth]]: not wrong exactly, but *older than he should be.* The elf has been in a place where time does not flow cleanly. Pass this to the [[Arden]] player as a private note in [[03-scene-the-fight-concludes|Chapter 1 Scene 3]].
