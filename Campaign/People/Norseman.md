# Norseman

*Human battle mage · northern origin · carries both sword and spell*

## Summary

Norseman is a mage in the Mage-Purge era — which means he is hunted. He has chosen the battle-mage path: not the scholar, not the hedge-wizard, not the fugitive. The sword and the spell in one hand, with the intent to use both.

He carries magic in a world that burns people for carrying it. He has made peace with being both the sword and the hand that wields it. Most mages never learn that distinction; Norseman has. The Architect will acknowledge it directly in [[05-scene-the-architect|Chapter 2 Scene 5]].

## Potential Beat — [[Petra]]

In [[02-scene-the-evening|Chapter 2, Scene 2]], if Norseman is at the Well when Petra is being accused, there is a beat: he recognizes her as a fellow mage. He was her, once. If he tells her so, she will find him the next morning and ask a single question. GM's choice; recommend: *"Have you ever burned someone by accident?"*
