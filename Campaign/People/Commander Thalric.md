# Commander Thalric

*Field commander of the [[Legio Null]] Sand-Rider unit. The hunter who finds what others miss.*

## Summary

Thalric is a professional. He is forty-two years old. He has been a Legio Null officer for fifteen years. He is unusually good at his job — not because he has magical aptitude (he does not), but because he has a **methodology**. He looks for patterns in mundane data: caravan routes, water usage, grain purchases, missing-person reports. He has found three hidden refuges in his career. [[Lux Æterna]] would be his fourth.

He does not hate mages. He does not love his work. He believes, with the full weight of a considered adult conviction, that the Decree is correct and that his role in enforcing it is necessary. He will change his mind only in the face of overwhelming personal evidence. The campaign may or may not give him that evidence.

## Appearance

Mid-40s. Lean. Tanned from years in the sands. A long scar under his left jaw — a mage set him on fire in SA 1494; he survived and killed her. He wears the Legio Null black-and-gray, meticulously maintained. He rides a gray desert horse named **Beda** that will outlast three of his mounts.

## Tactics

Thalric does not attack until he has enough information to be certain of a kill. He scouts. He waits. He prefers to arrive at dawn with a unit of twelve Sand-Riders, surround the target, and offer terms. If the terms are refused, he attacks efficiently. If they are accepted, he honors them — to the extent the Decree permits.

He is particularly dangerous because he is **not reckless.** The PCs cannot goad him. He will not pursue them into a trap.

## Session 1 Relevance

Not on stage. Arrives in Session 2 — or arrives in Session 3 if the party leaves [[Lux Æterna]] quickly.

The Stitching drop at the end of Chapter 2 is the intelligence he needed. His unit was already patrolling the region. Three of his scouts felt their gaze lock onto a specific canyon for one heartbeat and then snap away — a phenomenon they have been trained to report immediately. They will have the settlement located within 72 hours.
