# Yldroth

*Elf Monk · Aen'tir line · heals by striking*

## Summary

Yldroth channels ki through his fists. What he strikes, he mends. The effect is unsettling to watch — a blow that should bruise, closing the bruise underneath instead. He learned the practice young, or taught himself it, or inherited it; the PC's player will name which.

He is the other elf in the party. [[Valeth]] is Aen'valar. Yldroth is Aen'tir — a different lineage, different philosophy, same long memory. He sees in Valeth what the rift cost. He does not press him on it. He also does not leave him alone.

## Ki Mechanic (narrative note)

When Yldroth lays a healing strike on an ally, describe it: *"Your fist connects, not hard — exactly hard enough. The blow pushes back through flesh into something that is less flesh. The bruise closes like a hand relaxing."* The Architect's Naming (Chapter 2 Scene 5) acknowledges this pattern directly.
