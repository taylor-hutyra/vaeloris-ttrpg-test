# The Resonance Compass

*Gift of [[The Architect]]. Fragment locator. Harmonic device. Secretly: the Architect's conduit.*

## How the Party Receives It

Offered in [[05-scene-the-architect|Chapter 2 Scene 5]] — revealed on a plinth of folded light during the Architect's message. A PC may take it; it does not resist.

## Appearance

A palm-sized circle of **dark crystal wrapped in pale metal.** A slot in the center — thumb-tip sized — is empty. The slot has smooth, precise edges. It was made for a specific object.

It is warm to the touch. It hums almost imperceptibly.

## Known Properties (Session 1)

- **Hums** when the party is within roughly 10 miles of a Crown fragment. Pitch climbs as they get closer.
- **Heats** (gently) when a fragment is within Long range of the carrier personally.
- **Synchronizes** with [[Ulzammar Band|the Band]] when both are in the same room. [[Valeth]]'s anchor-quartz will ring in sympathy with both.

## Unknown Properties (revealed in later Acts)

- **Vector.** At close range to a fragment, the Compass begins to *pull* — physically — toward it.
- **Sings.** When all three fragments are present, it sings one clear triumphant note — the opening of the Fifth Harmony.
- **Architect conduit.** [[The Architect]] manifests THROUGH the Compass. Its attention reaches wherever the Compass is. **Perception ◆◆◆◆** during any Architect manifestation reveals that the geometric light originates FROM the Compass and expands outward.
- **The slot** fits a **Resonance Disc** — the object on the pedestal in the ruin [[Torvin Dast|Torvin]]'s crew described. Retrieving it is Session 2's A-plot.

## GM-Only

The Compass is not an instrument given by an ally. It is the Architect's **homing beacon** installed in the party. Perceptive players who decouple the Compass from the party (leaving it in a room, locking it away) will discover that the Architect cannot manifest to them. Whether they'll test this, and when, is up to them.
