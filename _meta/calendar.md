# World Calendar

## Eras

- **Pre-History (PH)**: The age of the divine, before mortal reckoning. Ends with the Paroxysm.
- **First Age (FA)**: The age of gods walking among mortals. Duration unknown — mythological time.
- **Second Age (SA)**: Years 1–3250. Begins with the Day of Silence (The Great Withdrawal).
- **Third Age (TA)**: Year 1–present (current year: 412 TA). Begins after the Mage Ascendancy epoch.
