# Rise of the Federation

The southern city-states, fearful of both the weakened [[Varnathi Republic]] and the powerful Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]], united to form the [[Federation of City-States]].

## Background

The [[Great Arcane Integration]] had reshaped the balance of power. The southern cities recognized that individually they could not compete with the major powers, but together they could become a force to be reckoned with.

## What Happened

The Federation adopted an innovative military doctrine: "Sword and Spell" — integrating mages directly into infantry squads rather than keeping them as a separate force. This approach combined conventional discipline with magical capability in a way neither the Republic nor the Technocracy had achieved.

## Consequences

A new superpower emerged on the mainland. The Federation's military doctrine would prove highly effective and eventually allow them to eclipse the aging Empire in military capability.
