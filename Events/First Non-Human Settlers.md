# First Non-Human Settlers

A Mirennian supply fleet, blown off course, was forced to hire local [[Thulkorun]] Dwarven mercenaries to repair their ships in a hidden cove. Seeing the potential of [[Novaterra]], the Dwarves negotiated passage for their families in exchange for their silence and their steel.

## Background

The [[Founding of Windsail]] had established Mirennian presence on Novaterra, but the colony was exclusively human. A chance encounter changed the continent's demographic trajectory.

## What Happened

The Thulkorun who repaired the Mirennian ships recognized the opportunity of a new land. They negotiated a deal: passage for their families to Novaterra in exchange for keeping the secret and providing their metallurgical skills. They established Iron-Beach, the first Dwarven enclave on the continent.

## Consequences

The secret of Novaterra began leaking into the Dwarven underworld. The Thulkorun settlement at Iron-Beach was the first crack in the League's monopoly on the new continent, foreshadowing the multi-racial colonization to come.
