# Stone-Road Projects

[[Vernath]], now a bustling city-state, began a massive infrastructure project to pave the trade routes through the dangerous highlands. Using Dwarven consulting engineers and human labor, they built elevated roads with fortified waystations every day's march to protect against beasts.

## Engineering Achievement

The roads represented the first major collaborative engineering project between [[Humans]] and [[Dwarves]] in the Second Age. Dwarven expertise in stone-cutting and Human labor combined to create a network of elevated, paved highways that transformed overland travel from a deadly gamble into a manageable journey.

## Economic Impact

The projects drastically reduced merchant mortality rates and fueled rapid economic expansion of [[Vernath]] as the trade hub of the north. These roads would later be expanded into a continental network under the [[Codex of the Road]] during the Imperial era.
