# Second Age of Flight

## Background

The [[Steam Revolution]] and influx of Dwarven steel from the [[Crack in the Door]] gave engineers the tools they needed. Gearhaven engineers collaborated with Mirennian architects to solve the problem of flight without magic.

## What Happened

The pivotal moment of the late epoch: engineers unveiled the Aether-Glider, using "Lift-Gas" refined from alchemical processes and steam propellers to achieve flight without Aeromancy. The [[Magocracy]] tried to ban them, but the [[First Decree]] specifically forbade "Flight Magic" -- the machines used no magic, creating an unassailable legal loophole.

## Consequences

The "Sky-Rush" began. The [[Mirennian League]] poured its fortune into building a massive fleet of commercial airships, reclaiming their title as masters of the sky. The [[Magocracy]], bound by its own rigid laws, could not stop them without inciting a global revolt -- leading to the [[Sky-Tax Crisis]].
