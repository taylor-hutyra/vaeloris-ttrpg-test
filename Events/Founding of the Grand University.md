# Founding of the Grand University

To standardize magical education and prevent another "Wild Years" scenario, the Magister's Council and the [[Aen'valar]] Elves co-founded the [[Grand University]] in [[Mrenr]].

## Background

The [[Arcane Hysteria]] of SA:1410 had demonstrated the catastrophic consequences of untrained magic users. With magic now legalized across the continent, the need for formal education was urgent.

## What Happened

The Grand University was established as a joint institution, combining human administrative structure with Elven magical expertise. It offered rigorous training in arcane theory, practical application, and safety protocols.

## Consequences

Magic became an academic science rather than a wild talent. The University marked the end of Epoch IV — the Arcane Revolution was complete, and the world had stabilized into a "High Magic" society. However, the rapid use of arcane energy would soon draw the attention of ancient, sleeping things in the sky.
