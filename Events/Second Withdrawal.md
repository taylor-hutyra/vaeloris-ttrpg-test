# Second Withdrawal

On the winter solstice of the 800th year, all divine magic ceased for one hour. When it returned, it was fainter, more distant. The [[The Twelve Minor Deities|Minor Deities]] withdrew their consciousness from the mortal plane entirely.

## Background

The [[Council of Divine Admonition]] had been the gods' final warning. When secular leaders dismissed it as the posturing of "powerless relics," the gods chose to depart completely.

## What Happened

Sacred fires in the temples dimmed. The winds no longer answered prayers, only physics. The world became truly secular. A period of nihilism and artistic melancholy -- the "God-Grief" -- swept through Human and Elven society, influencing poetry and music for a generation.

## Consequences

The [[Church of the Ordained]] lost political power as their miracles failed. The moral authority of the gods was gone, clearing the path for the unrestricted ambition of the coming ages. The [[Secular Decrees]] followed almost immediately, and Epoch III -- the Age of Expansion -- began.
