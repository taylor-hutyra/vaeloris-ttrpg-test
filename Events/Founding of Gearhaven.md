# Founding of Gearhaven

The "Great Collaboration" began at Rust-Water Bay on [[Novaterra]]. The [[Bronrn]] Dwarves laid the foundations, the [[Elm-korin]] Dwarves designed the power grid, human mages provided the arcane spark, and the [[Gnomes]] designed intricate clockwork control systems.

## Background

The refugees who arrived at the [[Landing at Rust-Water]] decided to build rather than wander. Their diverse skills proved complementary, enabling the creation of something none could have built alone.

## What Happened

[[Gearhaven]] rose from the sea cliffs — a city of brass, steam, and glowing runes built vertically into the rock face. The Binding Accord was drafted as its constitution, ensuring no single race would rule. Governance fell to the Cog-Council, representing the various Guilds.

## Consequences

Gearhaven gave birth to Technomancy — the revolutionary fusion of magic and machine that would reshape the balance of power across the world. The city became a beacon for exiles and innovators, growing rapidly into a superpower.
