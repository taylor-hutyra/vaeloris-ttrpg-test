# Primalkyn Convergences

The **Primalkyn Convergences** were eruptions of raw, untamed elemental energy that manifested across [[Vaeloris]] as [[Kael'Zorai]]'s corrupting influence strained the fabric of existence. These were the earliest warning signs that the world's fundamental balance was approaching a breaking point.

## Nature

Reality began to fray in unpredictable ways, giving rise to sentient or semi-sentient beings of pure nature. A river might spontaneously give birth to a creature of flowing water and unthinking rage, or a mountain might shed rock and soil to form a walking embodiment of geological fury.

## Mortal Response

The Convergences were terrifying and inexplicable to the mortal races, who had only known a world of deliberate, divine order. They were seen as omens, elemental hauntings, or divine punishments. These manifestations were distinct from the later, larger [[The Elemental Unraveling|Elemental Unraveling]] that followed the Binding, but they were precursors — splinters of the same instability.
