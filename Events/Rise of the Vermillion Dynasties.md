# Rise of the Vermillion Dynasties

While the north focused on the [[Rise of the Merchant Princes]], a line of Sorcerer-Kings arose in the deep southern oases. Using primitive "Blood-Binding" rituals found in First Age ruins, they united the scattered tribes into a terrifying military hegemony.

## Background

The southern desert tribes had been largely ignored by the northern powers. Discovery of First Age ruins containing Blood-Binding rituals gave ambitious leaders the power to unite the region.

## What Happened

The [[Vermillion Dynasties]] rapidly conquered the southern tip of the [[Varnathi Republic]] and seized the salt-flats of the [[Mirennian League]]. They severed overland trade routes in what became known as "The Trade Cut," forcing Mirennian merchants to sail further south into uncharted waters.

## Consequences

The Trade Cut directly accelerated Mirennian exploration of southern sea routes. The Dynasties collapsed around SA:1150 due to magical drought and inbreeding, leaving behind red-stone ziggurats and a fierce culture of independence that later birthed the [[Desertborn Confederacy Founded|Desertborn Confederacy]].
