# District of Scales

## Background

Following the [[Treaty of the Broken Peaks]], the surviving [[Drakona]] were stripped of flight and dignity. The [[Magocracy]] classified them as "Wards of the State" and sought to contain them.

## What Happened

The Drakona were relocated to the "District of Scales" in the ruins of their former capital, now grounded. They were used as living batteries to power the anti-Rot wards, their life force drained to fuel the magical containment efforts.

## Consequences

The simmering rage of the confined Drakona gave rise to the "[[Broken Wing]]" resistance movement, which would eventually launch the [[Broken Wing Insurgency]] -- a decades-long campaign of magical terrorism against the Magocracy.
