# Beast of Black Rock

A remote [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] border fort was annihilated overnight. Investigators found the stone walls melted into glass. The official report blamed a rogue fire-elemental.

## Background

The surface nations had no concept of the [[Drakona]] as an organized empire. Dragons were the stuff of legend, not a military threat to be catalogued and planned against.

## What Happened

Secret [[Shadewalkers|Shadewalker]] reports told a different story: "claw marks the size of siege engines" and "scales harder than diamond." This was the first confirmed contact with the organized military might of the Drakona — a reconnaissance probe testing surface defenses.

## Consequences

The Senate dismissed the incident as a localized anomaly. This willful blindness would cost the surface nations dearly when the Drakona invasion came centuries later.
