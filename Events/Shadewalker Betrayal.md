# Shadewalker Betrayal

## Background

The [[Shadewalkers]], a shadowy network of spies and information brokers, had been playing both sides of the war between the [[Allied Command]] and the [[Drakona Empire]] for profit.

## What Happened

The Shadewalkers sold the blueprints of the new Mk-IV Sky-Cruiser to the Drakona. The enemy began fielding "Mock-Ships" -- vessels that looked Allied but were filled with dragon-kin boarders, causing chaos and paranoia within the Allied fleet.

## Consequences

The Allied Command launched the Paranoia Purge, executing hundreds of suspected spies. The Shadewalkers went deep underground, becoming enemies of the state. Their reputation as untrustworthy double-dealers would persist for centuries.
