# Discovery of Novaterra

Mirennian Navigator [[Tynna Swift-Tide]], seeking a route around Republican blockades, sailed further east than any before. She discovered a massive, untamed continent.

## Background

The rivalry between the [[Varnathi Republic]] and the [[Mirennian League]] had driven blockades and trade restrictions. Mirennian navigators sought alternative routes to bypass Republican control.

## What Happened

Tynna Swift-Tide's voyage reached the shores of [[Novaterra]], a continent unknown to the peoples of the Old World. The [[Mirennian League]] immediately declared its existence a level-zero secret and established the "Ghost Fleet" -- ships officially listed as lost at sea, but actually ferrying supplies to the new land.

## Consequences

Novaterra would become the prize over which the Republic and League would fight for centuries. The secret colonization gave the League a massive strategic advantage until the [[The Secret is Sold|Ghost Charts were eventually sold]] to the Republic.
