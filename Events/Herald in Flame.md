# Herald in Flame

During a massive refugee crisis outside [[Luminas]], a bandit army attacked the helpless camp. As [[Iovan]] and the refugees prayed — not for salvation, but for the strength to endure — the sky ignited.

## Background

The [[Founding of the Church|Church of the Celestial Word]] had been growing through practical aid and spiritual teaching, but lacked the dramatic validation that would transform it from a local movement to a global faith.

## What Happened

A winged figure of pure light — an [[Aetheris]] — descended, placing itself between the bandits and the refugees. It did not attack; it simply stood. The bandits, overcome by a wave of unnatural dread and awe, fled. The entity dissolved after the danger passed.

## Consequences

The first Aetheris sighting validated the Church's core doctrine: the cosmos listens. Conversion rates skyrocketed as word spread of the miracle. The Church became an unstoppable spiritual force across the war-torn mainland.
