# Expansion of the Shadewalkers

The [[Shadewalkers]] espionage guild infiltrated the high courts of the [[Varnathi Republic]]. By blackmailing key Senators with proof of their illicit dealings with pirate captains, they effectively became the shadow government of the Republic's trade policy.

## Background

Since their [[Rise of the Shadewalkers|founding]] in Port Farrick, the Shadewalkers had accumulated vast stores of compromising information on the Republic's political class.

## What Happened

The guild leveraged blackmail material to control key Senators, directing trade policy to their benefit. They established the "Whisper-Market" in [[Port Farrick]], where secrets were bought and sold like grain.

## Consequences

Political corruption became systemic in [[Vernath]]. The Shadewalkers would continue to grow in influence, eventually playing a decisive role in events like the [[Blockade of Port Farrick]] and the manipulation of colonial policy.
