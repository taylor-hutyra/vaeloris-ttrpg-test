# Passing of the Giants

A [[Gearhaven]] survey team ascended the highest peak of the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]] to install a weather station. They found the massive cyclopean fortress there completely abandoned. Meals were left on tables; forges were cold but unweathered.

## Background

The Giants had been an enigmatic presence since their [[First Sighting of Giants|first sighting]] in SA:75 — colossal beings who tended cyclopean ruins and ignored the smaller races. Their territories had long been marked as forbidden.

## What Happened

No bodies were found. The Giants simply ceased to exist on the material plane. The mystery of their departure was never solved — they left no explanation, no warning, no trace beyond their abandoned works.

## Consequences

The "Scavenger Rush" began immediately as adventurers and nations scrambled to loot the Giant strongholds for "Titan-Steel" and ancient artifacts. The sudden absence of these ancient guardians left a power vacuum in the high places of the world.
