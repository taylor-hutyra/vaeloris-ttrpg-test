# First Sighting of Giants

A Dwarven mining expedition in the northern peaks of the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]] broke into a high-altitude valley and recorded the first official contact with the [[Giants]].

## The Encounter

These colossal beings were observed tending to cyclopean ruins and massive herds of woolly beasts. The [[Giants]] ignored the [[Dwarves]] until a prospector struck a rune-carved stone, prompting a Giant to casually crush the expedition's supply cart with a single finger gesture. The message was clear — this territory was not to be disturbed.

## The Wide Berth Decree

The High King of [[Kharnzarak]] issued the decree of "Wide Berth," marking Giant territory as strictly forbidden. Scholars theorized that the [[Giants]] were the "gardeners" of the [[First Hands]]' abandoned mountain sanctuaries — custodians of a power far older than the mortal civilizations emerging below.
