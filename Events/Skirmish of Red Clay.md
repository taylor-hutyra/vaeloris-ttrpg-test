# Skirmish of Red Clay

Republican colonists made landfall on [[Novaterra]]'s eastern coast, clashing with Mirennian outposts. The "Skirmish of Red Clay" was the first battle fought between the two powers on the new continent.

## Background

The [[The Secret is Sold|sale of the Ghost Charts]] had launched the Republic's Great Scramble to colonize Novaterra. Mirennian outposts, already established on the western coast, prepared for the inevitable confrontation.

## What Happened

Republican forces landed on the eastern coast and immediately clashed with Mirennian patrols. The fighting was inconclusive -- the continent was simply too vast for either side to dominate.

## Consequences

The stalemate led to the de facto division of Novaterra: the West Coast remained culturally Mirennian, while the East Coast became heavily Varnathi/Imperial. This division created the space for the later [[Rise of the Free Holds]] in the central highlands.
