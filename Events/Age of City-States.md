# Age of City-States

With central Imperial authority broken, the [[Varnathi Republic]] functioned as a loose federation. The major city-states developed distinct identities that would shape their cultures for millennia.

## The Four Powers

- **[[Vernath]]**: Philosophy and Law — the "Old Guard," seat of tradition and stoic governance.
- **[[Argentum]]**: Finance and Industry — the "New Money," driven by the silver wealth of the [[Silver Lords]].
- **[[Castellum]]**: Military and Discipline — the "Iron Fist," a garrison-state born of the southern frontier.
- **[[Port Farrick]]**: Trade and Diplomacy — the "Gateway," the crucial link between land and sea.

## Legacy

The Age of City-States created the political framework that would persist through the Republic era. While nominally united, each city pursued its own interests, creating a dynamic but often fractious political landscape.
