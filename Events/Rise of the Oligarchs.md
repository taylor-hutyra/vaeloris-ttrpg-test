# Rise of the Oligarchs

In the [[Varnathi Republic]], the Senate became a playground for the ultra-wealthy. Seats were openly bought and sold. The "Citizen" class was effectively disenfranchised, creating a massive population of angry, impoverished commoners.

## Background

The [[The Gilded Peace|Gilded Peace]] had enriched the new trading powers while the Republic stagnated. Wealth concentrated in fewer hands, and political power followed money.

## What Happened

The Republic ceased to function as a representative government. Populist demagogues rose to channel the anger of the disenfranchised, but they were co-opted or silenced by the oligarchic establishment.

## Consequences

The hollowed-out government became easy prey for the [[Shadewalkers]], who would eventually control the Republic from the shadows in the [[Shadewalker Hegemony]]. The Republic was a shell, waiting to crack.
