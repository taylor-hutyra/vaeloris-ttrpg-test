# Dissident of Aethelas

A young Elven scholar, [[Elaruniel]], published "On the Efficacy of Dissonance." She argued that harmonic magic was too slow for warfare and that "breaking" reality was more efficient than "bending" it.

## Background

The [[Founding of Aethelas|Aethelas]] academic establishment was built on the principles of harmonic magic — working with the natural flow of arcane energy. Elaruniel's thesis was a direct challenge to this orthodoxy.

## What Happened

Elaruniel was formally censured by the academics for her dangerous theories. But the Coalition of Mages, formed at the [[Luminas Conclave]], knew they would need ugly weapons to fight dragons. They recruited her in secret.

## Consequences

Elaruniel's research into dissonance magic would prove critical in the coming war. The academic establishment's rejection of her ideas demonstrated how institutional conservatism nearly cost the surface nations the tools they needed to survive.
