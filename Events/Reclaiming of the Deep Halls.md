# Reclaiming of the Deep Halls

Dwarven clans, previously scattered while fighting [[Kael'Zorai]]'s aberrations on the surface, began a concerted push back into the roots of the mountains at [[Kharnzarak]].

## The Tunnel War

Led by the [[Stone-Sworn Elders]], they found their ancestral halls infested with [[Skitter-Gloom]] — blind, chitinous predators that feed on heat and magical residue. These creatures spin webs of "shadow-silk" that dampen sound, making them silent killers in the deep dark. The reclamation was a brutal, room-by-room tunnel war fought in total darkness.

## Outcomes

The campaign yielded two crucial results: the development of specialized tunnel-fighting tactics that would define Dwarven military doctrine for centuries, and the rediscovery of the First Forge — though it remained cold, a symbolic promise of future restoration.

This event marked the beginning of the Dwarves' return to their subterranean civilization and set the stage for the eventual [[Unification of Dwarven Clans]] under [[Korin Barazkor]].
