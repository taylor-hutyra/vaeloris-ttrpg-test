# Coastal Expansion

Seeking to control maritime trade, the [[Varnathi Empire]] marched east to the coast. They founded [[Port Farrick]] at the mouth of the Great River but encountered the [[Mirennians]] -- now a loose confederation of wealthy city-states. When Imperial tax collectors arrived, the Mirennian captains threw them into the harbor.

## Background

The [[Codex of the Road]] had expanded the Empire's logistical reach across the continent. Control of maritime trade was the natural next step for Imperial ambitions.

## What Happened

The Empire founded [[Port Farrick]] and attempted to impose tariffs on Mirennian maritime trade. The Mirennian captains rejected Imperial authority outright, physically ejecting tax collectors.

## Consequences

This sparked the beginning of the "Cold War" between the Land (Empire) and the Sea (Mirennia). The Mirennians formalized their loose confederation into the [[Mirennian League]] for mutual defense, establishing the rivalry that would define centuries of Vaelorian politics.
