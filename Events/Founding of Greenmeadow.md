# Founding of Greenmeadow

Human expansion pushed into the fertile western river valleys, unknowingly encroaching on Gnomish territories. The [[Halflings|Halfling]] clans, migrating away from the militarized Republic, settled the region they named [[Greenmeadow]].

## Background

The militarization of the [[Varnathi Republic]] under [[Castellum]]'s influence made it increasingly uncomfortable for the peaceful Halfling clans. They sought new lands in the west.

## What Happened

When the Halflings settled Greenmeadow, they discovered they were not alone. The Forest [[Gnomes]], rather than fight, revealed themselves in the "Gnomish Unveiling." The two peoples signed the Declaration of Hollowkin Rights, establishing a shared society of "Burrows and Hollows" that baffled human tax collectors with its lack of hierarchy.

## Consequences

Greenmeadow became a unique political entity in Vaeloris -- a non-hierarchical Halfling-Gnome cooperative. This partnership would produce innovations like the [[Invention of Printing Press|printing press]] and the agricultural power that enabled the [[Green-Thumb Strike]].
