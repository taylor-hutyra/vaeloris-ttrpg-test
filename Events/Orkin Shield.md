# Orkin Shield

## Background

While the air war raged between the Allied fleet and the [[Drakona Empire]], the Drakona launched a ground invasion through the [[Barren Hills]] to flank the Alliance. The [[Varr-Darg]] Orkin stood in their path, defending their ancestral homeland.

## What Happened

The Orkin refused to retreat. Using tunnel warfare and suicide tactics honed over centuries of survival in the harsh Barren Hills, they held the pass for a decade against superior Drakona numbers.

## Consequences

The [[Allied Command]] was forced to acknowledge the Orkin contribution, sending supplies and advisors to support the defense. This recognition laid the critical groundwork for future Orkin integration into surface society, eventually contributing to the [[Charter of Civic Unity]].
