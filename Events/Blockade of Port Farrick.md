# Blockade of Port Farrick

[[Captain Vane]]'s fleet grew bold enough to blockade the Republic's primary port. For six months, no ship entered or left [[Port Farrick]].

## Background

The [[Scourge of Serpent's Tooth]] had transformed piracy into a geopolitical force. Vane's growing power eventually enabled him to directly challenge the Republic.

## What Happened

The blockade strangled Port Farrick's economy for six months. The siege was broken only when the [[Shadewalkers]] assassinated Vane, fracturing the pirate coalition back into warring crews.

## Consequences

The Republic established the Republic Naval Guard, a dedicated pirate-hunting fleet. The legend of Captain Vane inspired a "Golden Age" of romanticized piracy. The blockade also exposed the [[False Dawn of Port Farrick|Governor's secret betrayal]].
