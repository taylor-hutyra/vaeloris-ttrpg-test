# Ground War

## Background

The [[Earth-Anchor Ritual]] brought the Cloud Cities crashing to earth. Drakona survivors crawled from the wreckage, broken and flightless but unwilling to surrender.

## What Happened

The survivors of the Cloud Cities fought a desperate last stand in the ruins. The combat was brutal and close-quarters, fought in the twisted wreckage zones where the massive cloud-ships had fallen.

## Consequences

The Drakona, surrounded and without the advantage of flight, were slowly ground down. Their defeat led directly to the [[Treaty of the Broken Peaks]] and the formal end of the war.
