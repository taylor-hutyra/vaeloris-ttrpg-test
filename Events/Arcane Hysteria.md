# Arcane Hysteria

Without training or discipline, the "New Mages" caused chaos across the [[Varnathi Republic]]. In [[Port Farrick]], a dockworker accidentally boiled the harbor water. In [[Vernath]], a student set the Great Library on fire with a sneeze. The public was terrified.

## Background

Following [[Melek the Grey]]'s distribution of translated arcane texts, hundreds of ordinary people began manifesting magical abilities. With no established traditions of training or safety, accidents were inevitable and devastating.

## What Happened

Mob justice erupted as vigilantes began lynching anyone suspected of "witchcraft." Charlatans exploited the fear by selling "anti-magic amulets" — painted rocks — draining the savings of the terrified peasantry. The period became known as "The Wild Years."

## Consequences

The widespread panic and destruction gave political ammunition to those who wanted magic eliminated entirely. The military generals of [[Castellum]] pressured the Senate, leading directly to the [[Decree of Prohibition]] in SA:1425.
