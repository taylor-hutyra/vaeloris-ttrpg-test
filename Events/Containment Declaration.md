# Containment Declaration

## Background

After a century of struggle against the [[Outbreak of the Grey Rot|Grey Rot]], the [[Magocracy]] was ready to declare victory -- whether the victory was genuine or not.

## What Happened

The Magocracy declared the Grey Rot "Contained" and erected massive, permanent "Pylons of Warding" around the infected zones, now known as The Scars. The world breathed a collective sigh of relief.

## Consequences

The Rot was not truly gone; it was merely caged. The Magocracy took credit for saving the world, solidifying their political legitimacy and setting the stage for their transformation from crisis managers into absolute rulers through the [[First Decree]].
