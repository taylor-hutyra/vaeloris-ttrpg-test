# Founding of Windsail

The [[Mirennian League]] established [[Windsail]], the first permanent settlement on [[Novaterra]]'s western coast. To keep it secret from the Republic, they utilized "Ghost Charts" -- maps enchanted by Wind-Shapers that appeared blank to anyone who did not know the cipher key.

## Background

The [[Discovery of Novaterra]] had given the League a secret advantage. The decision to colonize was driven by the desire to exploit the continent's resources without Republican interference.

## What Happened

Only the most loyal House members were permitted to emigrate, creating a highly aristocratic colonial culture from the outset. Ghost Charts ensured the settlement's location remained a closely guarded secret.

## Consequences

Novaterra became a secret garden for the League, providing exotic timber and spices that baffled Republican economists who could not determine their origin. The colony would remain hidden until the [[The Secret is Sold|Ghost Charts were sold]] to the Republic centuries later.
