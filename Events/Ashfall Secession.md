# Ashfall Secession

The industrial barony of [[Ashfall]] declared independence from the [[Federation of City-States]]. When the [[Magocracy]] tried to intervene, [[Ashfall]] unveiled its ultimate deterrent: Necro-Industrialism — factories and armies powered by the undead.

## The Three Invasions

The [[Magocracy]] invaded three times. Each time, the dead rose to fight again. A war of attrition against an enemy that replenished its soldiers from the fallen was mathematically impossible to win.

## The Ashfall Compact

Realizing the futility, the [[Magocracy]] recognized [[Ashfall]]'s sovereignty. In exchange, [[Ashfall]] became the dark laboratory of the world — conducting the forbidden research that the [[Magocracy]] needed but could not publicly sanction. The "Gray Market" made [[Ashfall]] the world's supplier of forbidden and unethical technology, a necessary evil that every power quietly relied upon.
