# Charter of Civic Unity

## Background

The [[Magocracy]] needed to rebuild the workforce devastated by centuries of war. The Orkin, who had proven their value during the [[Orkin Shield]], were the obvious untapped labor source.

## What Happened

The Magocracy pressured the successor states of the [[Varnathi Empire]] to integrate the Orkin. The Charter of Civic Unity granted Orkin "Subject Status," allowing them to live in cities but confining them to specific districts known as "The Quarters."

## Consequences

Excluded from legal guilds, the Orkin formed "Shadow-Syndicates" to survive -- criminal networks that became the only path to economic participation. The charter created a permanent underclass, offering the appearance of integration while ensuring structural inequality.
