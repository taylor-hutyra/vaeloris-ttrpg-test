# Cloud-Shadow Incidents

[[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] ships crossing the deep ocean began disappearing. Not sinking — vanishing. No wreckage was ever found.

## Background

The [[Drakona]] had been probing surface defenses since the [[Beast of Black Rock]] incident. Now their floating cloud-cities were casting literal shadows across the trade routes.

## What Happened

Mirennian "Wind-Listeners" reported strange, high-altitude disturbances — like "mountains moving through the air." The Magister's Council dismissed these reports as weather anomalies, refusing to consider the implications.

## Consequences

The pattern of willful blindness continued. Ships vanished, sailors reported impossible phenomena, and the authorities refused to investigate. The surface nations were given every warning of what was coming and ignored them all.
