# Standardization of Steel

To fuel the new Imperial Army, Varnathi smiths, aided by [[Thulkorun]] (exiled Dwarf) consultants, perfected the "Blast-Furnace" technique. This allowed for the mass production of standardized steel armor and weapons, replacing the bronze and iron mix of the previous epoch.

## Background

The [[Unification of the High Plateau]] created a centralized military that required uniform equipment. The [[Thulkorun]], exiled from [[Kharnzarak]] but carrying advanced metallurgical knowledge, proved invaluable.

## What Happened

The Blast-Furnace technique was perfected through collaboration between Varnathi smiths and Thulkorun consultants, enabling mass production of standardized steel. This technological leap transformed the Imperial Army.

## Consequences

The Varnathi Legions became the most heavily armed force on the continent. The elite heavy infantry corps, the [[Iron-Bound]], was created. This military advantage enabled the [[March of Order]] and the Empire's rapid expansion.
