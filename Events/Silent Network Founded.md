# Silent Network Founded

Fearing that the frantic pace of expansion was erasing the past, the [[Lothari Mystics]] formally organized the [[Silent Network]]. They began archiving histories, artifacts, and myths in hidden desert vaults.

## Background

The Age of Expansion had turned all eyes toward the future -- colonization, trade, warfare. Ancient knowledge about the First Age and the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]] was being lost to secular revisionism and simple neglect.

## What Happened

The Lothari Mystics, already keepers of desert lore within the [[Desertborn Confederacy]], formalized their preservation efforts into the Silent Network. They believed the "Age of Noise" would eventually collapse, and the world would need its memory back.

## Consequences

The Silent Network preserved knowledge regarding the First Age and the Paroxysm that would otherwise have been lost. These hidden archives would prove invaluable in later ages when the world needed to understand its ancient past.
