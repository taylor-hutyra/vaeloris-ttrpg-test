# Great Betrayal

Seeking to install a puppet dictator, the [[Shadewalkers]] leaked forged documents implicating the five most powerful city-states of the [[Varnathi Republic]] in a plot to enslave the others.

## Background

The [[Shadewalker Hegemony]] had controlled the Republic from the shadows for decades. Now they sought to replace their covert control with open domination through a compliant dictator.

## What Happened

The forged documents triggered immediate paranoia and violence. The Succession Wars erupted as the Republic shattered into warring factions. [[Castellum]] declared independence. [[Argentum]] hired mercenaries to besiege [[Vernath]]. The "Pax Magica" on the mainland was broken.

## Consequences

The Great Betrayal plunged the mainland into [[The Bleeding Years]] — forty years of devastating civil war that left the continent in ruins and set the stage for the rise of a new order.
