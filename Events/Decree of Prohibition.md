# Decree of Prohibition

The Senate of the [[Varnathi Republic]], pressured by the military generals of [[Castellum]], passed the Decree of Prohibition. The practice, study, or possession of arcane materials was made punishable by death.

## Background

The [[Arcane Hysteria]] had terrified the population and destabilized public order. The military establishment saw magic as an existential threat to the Republic's conventional power structure.

## What Happened

Great bonfires were lit in every city square to destroy [[Melek the Grey]]'s texts and any other arcane materials. The [[Shadewalkers]], ever opportunistic, quietly hoarded the surviving texts, recognizing their immense future value.

## Consequences

The decree drove magic underground but could not eliminate it. It set the stage for the [[Mage-Purge Wars]] and the formation of the Legio Null — the Inquisitors who would enforce the ban with brutal efficiency.
