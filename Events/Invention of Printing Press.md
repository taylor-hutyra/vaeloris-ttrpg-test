# Invention of Printing Press

In the mixed burrow-city of [[Gear-Hollow]] — a [[Halflings|Halfling]]/[[Gnomes|Gnome]] settlement — a Gnome tinkerer named [[Bimble Pockets]] invented the movable type printing press.

## The Revolution of the Written Word

The printing press unleashed an explosion of literacy and pamphlets across the continent. Knowledge that had been hoarded by guilds, academies, and secret societies for centuries was suddenly available to anyone who could read — and with cheap pamphlets, more people learned to read than ever before.

## Threatened Monopolies

The "Secrets of the Guilds" began to leak to the common public, threatening the information monopoly of the [[Shadewalkers]] and the trade secrets of artisan guilds. This democratization of knowledge was perhaps the most quietly revolutionary event of the Third Epoch, reshaping the balance of power between elites and commoners for all time.
