# Rise of the Shadewalkers

Veterans of the [[Salt War]], spies, and disgraced diplomats formed a secret society in the shadows of [[Port Farrick]]. They realized that information was more profitable than war.

## Background

The [[Salt War]] had produced a generation of soldiers, spies, and diplomats who had seen the futility of open conflict. Port Farrick, as a trade gateway, was the perfect breeding ground for an intelligence network.

## What Happened

The [[Shadewalkers]] organized as a formal espionage guild, selling state secrets to both the [[Varnathi Republic]] and the [[Mirennian League]]. By ensuring neither side gained a decisive advantage, they made themselves indispensable.

## Consequences

The Shadewalkers would grow to become one of the most powerful forces in Vaeloris, eventually [[Expansion of the Shadewalkers|infiltrating the high courts]] of the Republic and establishing the "Whisper-Market" in Port Farrick where secrets were traded like grain.
