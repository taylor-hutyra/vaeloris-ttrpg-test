# Battle of the Grey Peaks

## Background

With the [[Launch of Allied Air Fleet]], the Allies could finally contest the skies. The [[Drakona Empire]] attempted to push an armada across the mountains into the central plains, forcing a confrontation.

## What Happened

The first major fleet-on-fleet engagement of the war raged for three days in a thunderstorm over the [[Grey Peaks]]. Individual Drakona warriors proved devastating against wooden hulls, shredding Allied ships in close combat.

## Consequences

The Allies stopped the invasion but at terrible cost, losing 60% of their ships -- a Pyrrhic victory. The hard lesson led to future ships being plated in "Star-Steel" alloy to withstand dragon attacks.
