# Shadewalker Hegemony

By SA:1920, the Grandmaster of the [[Shadewalkers]], [[Varia the Whisper]], effectively ruled the [[Varnathi Republic]] from the shadows.

## Background

The [[Rise of the Oligarchs]] had hollowed out the Republic's institutions, leaving a shell of governance that was easy for the Shadewalkers to manipulate and control.

## What Happened

Varia manipulated grain prices to create artificial scarcity, incited border skirmishes to sell weapons to both sides, and assassinated any honest politician who tried to reform the system. The Republic was a puppet state in all but name.

## Consequences

Public trust in government was totally eroded. The Republic was ready to crack — and the Shadewalkers would be the ones to shatter it in the [[Great Betrayal]], seeking to install a puppet dictator they could control openly.
