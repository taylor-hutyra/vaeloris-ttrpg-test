# The Bleeding Years

For forty years, the mainland was a slaughterhouse. Warlords rose and fell. Magic was used indiscriminately, scarring the land. Famine was widespread.

## Background

The [[Great Betrayal]] had shattered the [[Varnathi Republic]] into warring factions. Without a central authority, the continent descended into chaos.

## What Happened

The Succession Wars consumed everything. Every would-be ruler fought for supremacy, deploying magic as a weapon of mass destruction. The land itself was scarred by the indiscriminate use of arcane power. Famine followed devastation.

## Consequences

The secular promises of the previous epochs — that mortals did not need gods — rang hollow amid the suffering. The spiritual vacuum created by decades of war led directly to the [[Founding of the Church]] and the return of faith to the world.
