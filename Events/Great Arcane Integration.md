# Great Arcane Integration

Following the [[Treaty of the Silver Quill]], nations scrambled to adapt to the new reality of legalized magic. Each civilization approached the challenge differently.

## Background

The treaty had ended centuries of persecution, but integrating magic into existing power structures required fundamental changes to governance, military doctrine, and economics.

## What Happened

[[Vernath]] established the Magister's Council to oversee state-sanctioned mages, turning magic into a bureaucracy. The [[Mirennian League]] integrated "Tide-Mages" into every ship crew, regaining their naval edge. [[Kharnzarak]] remained sealed, but the [[Wandering Kin]] began openly trading runic items for the first time.

## Consequences

The integration transformed every major civilization. Magic became a regulated part of statecraft rather than a forbidden art, setting the stage for the [[Rise of the Federation]] and the [[Founding of the Grand University]].
