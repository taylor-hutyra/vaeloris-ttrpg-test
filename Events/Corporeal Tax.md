# Corporeal Tax

## Background

In the immediate aftermath of the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual|Skyfall]], billions of tons of wreckage from the Cloud Cities littered the continent. The [[Coalition of Mages]], now the de facto world government, needed to clear the ruins before rebuilding could begin.

## What Happened

The Coalition instituted the Corporeal Tax: every able-bodied citizen was conscripted for three years of labor to clear the ruins of the Cloud Cities. Entire cities sprang up around the crash sites to harvest "Sky-Stone" and "Draconic Steel."

## Consequences

The Salvage Economy became the dominant economic force of the era. The [[Mirennian League]], their sky-ports now useless, pivoted again -- becoming masters of salvage logistics and rebuilding their wealth on the bones of their former enemies.
