# Warning of the Wind

Every Wind-Shaper in the [[Mirennian League]] simultaneously felt a massive displacement of air in the far north — a pressure wave that signaled the movement of millions of tons of mass.

## Background

The Mirennian Wind-Shapers had been reporting anomalies since the [[Cloud-Shadow Incidents]]. This event was on an entirely different scale — unmistakable evidence of something enormous approaching.

## What Happened

The League mobilized its fleet immediately. But the Empire refused to believe the reports, dismissing them as "Mirennian hysteria." The same willful blindness that had characterized the response to the [[Beast of Black Rock]] and the [[Cloud-Shadow Incidents]] persisted to the very end.

## Consequences

The warning came too late for meaningful preparation. Within five years, the [[Drakona]] Invasion Fleet would cross the cloud barrier and descend upon an unprepared world.
