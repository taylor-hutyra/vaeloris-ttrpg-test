# Launch of Allied Air Fleet

## Background

For twenty years following the [[Aethelas Contribution]], the nations of the Alliance poured every resource into building an air fleet capable of challenging the [[Drakona Empire]]'s aerial dominance.

## What Happened

The [[Allied Command]] unveiled the First Air Fleet, composed of 500 ships: Gearhaven Ironclads, Mirennian Sky-Galleons, and Varnathi Assault Barges. It was the largest concentration of military power in the history of Vaeloris.

## Consequences

For the first time, the Allies could contest air superiority. The war shifted from a desperate ground defense to a true two-front conflict, setting the stage for the [[Battle of the Grey Peaks]].
