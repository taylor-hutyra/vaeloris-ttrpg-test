# Fall of Vrak'Thul

## Background

The fortress-city of [[Vrak'Thul]] was a haven for renegade mages and [[Drakona]] loyalists who refused to submit to the [[Magocracy]]'s authority. When the city refused to pay its tithes, the Ruling Council decided to make an example.

## What Happened

The Magocracy deployed the Arcane Nullifier -- a weapon that created an "Anti-Magic Vacuum." The city, held together by old magic, crumbled into dust instantly. Every living soul within perished.

## Consequences

Total submission followed. No nation dared defy the Magocracy openly after this demonstration of absolute power. The destruction paved the way for the [[First Decree]] and the era of the Pax Magica.
