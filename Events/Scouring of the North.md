# Scouring of the North

## Background

The Drakona Empire had long ruled from floating cloud-cities hidden by ancient aeromancy. On the first day of spring in SA:2341, they descended upon the surface world, beginning with the northern Varnathi provinces.

## What Happened

The sky over the northern provinces darkened not with clouds but with the hulls of Drakona cloud-ships and thousands of dragon-kin warriors. The invasion began with systematic aerial bombardment of major fortifications using "Sun-Lances" -- focused beams of solar magic. Within three months, 30% of the Varnathi Empire was occupied.

## Consequences

The fall of the north triggered a massive refugee crisis as millions fled south, clogging roads and causing famine in the unoccupied lands. The sheer scale of destruction forced the fragmented nations of Vaeloris to set aside centuries of rivalry and negotiate the [[Treaty of Survival]].
