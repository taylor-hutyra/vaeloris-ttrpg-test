# Secular Decrees

In the wake of the [[Second Withdrawal]], the Senate of the [[Varnathi Republic]] passed the Secular Decrees. These laws stripped the Priesthood of the Ordained of their tax-exempt status and political veto power.

## Background

The [[Second Withdrawal]] left the [[Church of the Ordained]] unable to perform miracles. The state religion established by the [[Synod of the Ordained]] had relied on divine demonstrations of power that were no longer possible.

## What Happened

The Senate passed sweeping legislation stripping religious institutions of their privileges. Temples were repurposed as civic halls or granaries. The rapid secularization transformed human society almost overnight.

## Consequences

The Church survived only by transforming into a charitable organization, planting the seeds for its eventual rebirth as the Church of the Celestial Word in later ages. The power vacuum left by religion would be filled by military and commercial interests.
