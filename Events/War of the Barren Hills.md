# War of the Barren Hills

The [[Varnathi Republic]], needing resources to fuel the colonial race for [[Novaterra]], launched a war of extermination against the [[Varr-Darg]] [[Orkin]] in the [[Barren Hills]] to seize iron and coal deposits.

## Scorched Earth

The legions used "Scorched Earth" tactics, burning the limited food sources of the [[Orkin]]. It was a brutal, one-sided campaign designed not merely to conquer, but to erase the Orkin presence from the mainland entirely.

## The Mirennian Passage

Desperate and starving, thousands of [[Orkin]] paid [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] smugglers — who cared only for coin — to ferry them to [[Novaterra]]. This diaspora would reshape the new continent's politics, as the displaced Orkin joined with [[Bronrûn]] [[Dwarves]] to establish the [[Rise of the Free Holds|Free Holds]].

## Legacy

The Orkin population on the mainland was reduced to scattered guerrilla bands. The war's brutality also triggered the [[Green-Thumb Strike]] by the [[Halflings]] of [[Greenmeadow]], demonstrating that even the "lesser" races held leverage over the Republic.
