# Founding of Port Farrick

Seeking to control maritime trade, the [[Varnathi Empire]] marched east to the coast and founded [[Port Farrick]] at the mouth of the Great River.

## The First Clash

The Empire's eastward expansion brought it into direct contact with the [[Mirennians]], now a loose confederation of wealthy city-states. When Imperial tax collectors arrived to assert authority, the Mirennian captains threw them into the harbor — a defiant act that would echo through history.

## Consequences

This confrontation sparked the beginning of the "Cold War" between the Land ([[Varnathi Empire|Empire]]) and the Sea ([[Mirennian League|Mirennia]]). In response, the Mirennians formalized their alliance into the [[Mirennian League]] for mutual defense. [[Port Farrick]] itself would become a perpetual flashpoint — a gateway between two civilizations that could never quite agree on who controlled it.
