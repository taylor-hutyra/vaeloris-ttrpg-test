# Star-Tide Festival

To celebrate their continued independence, the diverse peoples of [[Aerinr]] — Elves, [[Waldkyn]], Orkin, and Humans — held the first Star-Tide Festival in [[Lanthir Haven]].

## Background

[[Aerinr]] had proven its ability to resist external aggression at the [[Silent Repulsion]]. The festival was a celebration of the diverse, cooperative society that made such resistance possible.

## What Happened

The Star-Tide Festival brought together art, music, and cultural traditions from all of Aerinr's peoples. It became an annual event that drew visitors from across the world.

## Consequences

The festival became a cultural beacon. Music and art produced here spread via [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] traders, subtly influencing the youth of the mainland to question their rigid, segregated societies. It was soft power at its most effective.
