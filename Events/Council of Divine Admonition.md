# Council of Divine Admonition

As tensions rose for a second [[Salt War]], the avatars of the minor deities appeared simultaneously in the Senate of [[Vernath]] and the Council of Mirenn. They spoke in unison: "Your hunger devours the future. We will not watch you consume the world we saved."

## Background

The [[Salt War]] had demonstrated the destructive potential of mortal conflict. With both the [[Varnathi Republic]] and [[Mirennian League]] rearming, the [[The Twelve Minor Deities|minor deities]] made a final attempt at intervention.

## What Happened

The divine avatars manifested simultaneously in the seats of both mortal powers, delivering an identical warning. It was the most dramatic divine intervention since the [[Day of Silence]].

## Consequences

The warning was largely ignored by secular leaders who believed the gods were powerless relics of the First Age. This dismissal directly precipitated the [[Second Withdrawal]], when the gods departed the mortal plane entirely.
