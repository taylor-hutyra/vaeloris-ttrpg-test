# Perfection of Wind-Shaping

Building on the [[Incident of the Burning Mast]], the Mirennian [[Guild of Tides]] codified the art of Wind-Shaping. It was not true magic in the later arcane sense, but a ritualistic communion with the elemental remnants of [[Gwynfaer|Gwynfaer the Windweaver]].

## The Breakthrough

Through years of study and practice, the [[Guild of Tides]] perfected techniques that allowed Mirennian ships to sail against the wind. This was a revolutionary capability — no other navy could match it, giving the [[Mirennian League]] absolute naval superiority.

## Economic Dominance

With ships that could outmaneuver any competitor regardless of wind conditions, the League established economic dominance over all coastal trade. This mastery of the elements would remain the cornerstone of Mirennian power until the Dragonfall era, when the skies themselves became the battlefield.
