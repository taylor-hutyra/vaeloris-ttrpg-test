# Siege of the Free Marches

The [[Varnathi Republic]] sent three legions to crush [[Gruk the Breaker]]'s revolt in the [[Free Marches]]. However, the [[Shadewalkers]], seeing profit in chaos, supplied the rebels with stolen Mirennian "Fire-Pots" — early grenades.

## Background

The Republic could not tolerate an independent slave state on its eastern border. The legions were sent expecting a quick victory against what they viewed as disorganized rebels.

## What Happened

Instead of the expected swift campaign, the legions became bogged down in a decade-long guerrilla war. The rebels used their knowledge of the terrain and the Shadewalker-supplied weapons to devastating effect against conventional forces.

## Consequences

The prolonged siege drained the Republic's military strength and shattered the myth of Varnathi invincibility. The Republic was forced to maintain a costly permanent garrison, weakening its ability to project power elsewhere.
