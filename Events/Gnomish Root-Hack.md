# Gnomish Root-Hack

As global tensions rose, the Forest [[Gnomes]] of the mainland felt threatened by expanding human armies. Instead of building walls or raising armies, they turned to what they knew best: the living network beneath their feet.

## The Mycelial Network

The [[Myco-Whisperers]] modified the mycelial network of the forests to act as a continental vibration-sensor. Every footfall of a marching army, every tremor of a siege engine, every movement of troops through forested territory was detected and relayed through the fungal network.

## The Info-Brokers

The [[Myco-Whisperers]] became the world's premier intelligence agency. They could track troop movements anywhere there were roots, selling this data to the highest bidder — often the [[Lothari]] or [[Elves]] — to maintain the balance of power. It was the greatest intelligence achievement of the age, and it was accomplished by a people most nations dismissed as harmless tinkerers.
