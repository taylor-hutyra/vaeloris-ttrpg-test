# The Paroxysm

The **Paroxysm** — the Divine War — was the cataclysmic conflict that ended the First Age, when the four united First Hands confronted and sealed [[Kael'Zorai]], the Fifth, whose obsession with total systematic control threatened to rewrite all of creation into a singular, static order.

## The Growing Corruption

As Kael'Zorai's influence expanded, the world strained under increasingly rigid systematic designs. Natural cycles became mechanically rigid rather than organically flowing. Creative work was "improved" according to Kael'Zorai's exacting standards. Independent thought and cultural variation were viewed as flaws to be corrected. Reality itself began showing stress fractures from systematic over-control.

## The Four Unite Against the Fifth

[[Tham'dran]], [[Virandros]], [[Myrasael]], and [[Elaraieth]] recognized that Kael'Zorai sought not harmony but total dominion — intending to rewrite all of creation into a singular, perfect, but ultimately static order. Their unity sparked a metaphysical upheaval that reshaped the foundations of reality.

## The Cosmic Conflict

Anticipating confrontation, Kael'Zorai unleashed its creations upon [[Vaeloris]]:

- Reality itself reshaped by warring divine wills
- Time fractured and became unstable across regions
- Natural cycles destabilized throughout the world
- The metaphysical bled into the physical realm

## The Mortal Response

- Dwarven strongholds became refuges protected by [[Bjalmund]]'s divine stonework
- Elven groves maintained pockets of natural stability through [[Ceridara]]'s teachings
- Human communities showed remarkable adaptability to chaotic conditions
- The minor deities fought to protect their mortal students and sacred sites

## The Monsters of the Paroxysm

In its final act of defiance, Kael'Zorai transformed [[The Circles Below]] from a realm of isolation into a crucible of twisted genesis, creating:

- **Aberrations** — embodiments of corrupted systematic structure
- **Riftborn** — creatures of dimensional instability
- **Echo-entities** — nightmare reflections of failed systematic creation

These beings spilled forth through arcane scars — rifts where Kael'Zorai's corrupted essence bled through reality, creating lasting **Scars of Dissonance** that remain as sites of cursed energy. Twisted experiments also created aberrations such as the [[Skitter-Gloom]] that infested the deep places of the world.

## The Binding

In the climactic act known as [[The Binding]], the united will of the four First Hands overcame Kael'Zorai. They stripped it of name and authority, imprisoned it beyond mortal reality, and erased it from memory. Yet Time, Magic, and the potential for Extremes remained woven into existence.

## Aftermath

The Paroxysm left the world shattered. Atmospheric debris dimmed the sun, creating the "Years of Grey." The [[The Elemental Unraveling|Elemental Unraveling]] — the cosmic backlash from removing Kael'Zorai — gave spontaneous birth to the [[Primalkyn]]. In response, the exhausted First Hands created the [[Waldkyn]] as one final act of collaborative harmony before the [[The Great Withdrawal|Great Withdrawal]].

The end of the First Age led to the [[Day of Silence]], when the minor deities also withdrew from the physical plane entirely.
