# The Paroxysm

The Paroxysm was the cataclysmic divine war that preceded the Second Age. [[Kael'Zorai]], a god of corruption and domination, waged war against the other gods of [[Vaeloris]], unleashing devastating forces across the physical plane.

## The Conflict

The war pitted [[Kael'Zorai]] against the [[The Twelve Minor Deities|twelve minor deities]], whose localized avatars led mortal armies in battle. The conflict scarred the land with [[Scars of Dissonance]] — wounds in the fabric of reality that would fester for millennia. [[Kael'Zorai]]'s twisted experiments created aberrations such as the [[Skitter-Gloom]] that infested the deep places of the world.

## Aftermath

The Paroxysm left the world shattered. Atmospheric debris dimmed the sun, creating the "Years of Grey." Elemental instability plagued the land, and the remnants of divine combat lingered as magical contamination. The psychological and physical scars of this war defined the early Second Age, as mortals found themselves alone in a broken world.

The end of the Paroxysm led directly to the [[Day of Silence]], when the gods withdrew from the physical plane entirely.
