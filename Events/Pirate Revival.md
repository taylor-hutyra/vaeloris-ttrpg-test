# Pirate Revival

## Background

The [[Pirate Confederacy]] of [[Serpent's Tooth]] had been grounded for centuries since the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual|Skyfall]] destroyed all flight capability. Rather than fade away, they adapted.

## What Happened

The pirates developed "Wave-Skimmers" -- extremely fast hydrofoil ships -- and early non-magical gliders launched from cliffs to scout prey. These innovations allowed them to return to the high seas in force.

## Consequences

Smuggling returned to challenge the [[Magocracy]]'s trade monopolies. The pirates' early glider experiments also contributed to the development of mechanical flight that would culminate in the [[Second Age of Flight]].
