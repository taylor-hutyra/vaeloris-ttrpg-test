# Gathering of Heroes

The [[Echo-Beasts]] coalesced into a unified threat led by the Echo-Lord, a psychic entity occupying a massive [[Scars of Dissonance|Scar of Dissonance]]. A call was sent out across the continent for heroes to gather at the Scar.

## Background

The [[Emergence of Echo-Beasts]] had proven that conventional weapons were ineffective against the semi-corporeal threats. The Echo-Lord represented an existential danger requiring unprecedented cooperation.

## What Happened

Heroes from all races -- Human knights, Dwarven defenders, Elven archers, and [[Thulkorun]] mercenaries -- gathered at the Scar. For the first time, the races fought side-by-side as equals, rather than merely allies of convenience. The Avatar of [[Bjalmund]] (The Shieldbearer) manifested to lead the charge.

## Consequences

The Gathering demonstrated that the races of Vaeloris could unite against a common threat. It set the stage for the [[Defeat of the Echo-Lord]], the climactic battle that would end Epoch I.
