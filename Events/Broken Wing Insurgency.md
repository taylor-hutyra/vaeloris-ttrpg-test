# Broken Wing Insurgency

A radical faction of flightless [[Drakona]], the [[Broken Wing]], launched a decades-long campaign of magical terrorism against the [[Magocracy]]. They bombed Mana-Pylons and assassinated Magisters to protest their people's confinement in the [[District of Scales]].

## The Cause

The [[Drakona]] had been stripped of flight, confined to reservations, and used as living batteries to power the anti-Rot wards. The [[Broken Wing]] argued that submission meant extinction — that only violence could force the [[Magocracy]] to acknowledge Drakona rights.

## Moral Complexity

The insurgency created a tragic paradox. The attacks justified the [[Magocracy]]'s harsh policing in the eyes of the public, creating a cycle of oppression and retaliation. It established the [[Drakona]] as an active internal threat, complicating the narrative of them being solely victims. The question of whether the Broken Wing were freedom fighters or terrorists remains one of the most divisive issues of the age.
