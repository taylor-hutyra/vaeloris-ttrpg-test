# War of Whispers

## Background

With standing armies made illegal under the [[First Decree]], nations could no longer resolve their grievances through open warfare. Conflict did not disappear -- it went underground.

## What Happened

The [[Shadewalkers]] entered a golden age, acting as proxies for national grievances. The Magocracy's Inquisitors fought a shadow war against them. Assassination became the primary tool of diplomacy.

## Consequences

The era demonstrated that peace enforced by decree does not eliminate conflict, only transforms it. The Shadewalkers grew more powerful and embedded in the fabric of international politics, making them increasingly impossible to eliminate.
