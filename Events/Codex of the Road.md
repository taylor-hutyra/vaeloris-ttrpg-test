# Codex of the Road

With the [[Varnathi Empire]] expanding under [[Caelius I]], the need for logistics grew. The [[Imperial Senate]] passed the Codex of the Road, a massive public works project that expanded the paved trade roads of Epoch I into a continental network.

## The Infrastructure

Building upon the [[Stone-Road Projects]] of SA:110, the Codex standardized road construction across the empire. Paved highways connected [[Vernath]], [[Castellum]], and the growing frontier settlements, enabling rapid movement of troops and trade goods.

## The Courier Corps

The Codex established the "Courier Corps," a relay system allowing messages to cross the empire in days rather than weeks. The "Imperial Mile" became the standard unit of measurement for the entire continent, imposing Varnathi order on the physical landscape itself.
