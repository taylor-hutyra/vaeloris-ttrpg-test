# Establishment of Drakona Protectorates

The [[Drakona]] did not simply bomb the northern [[Varnathi Empire|Varnathi]] provinces into submission. They offered an ultimatum: "Serve us, and live better than you did under the corrupt Senate." Many provinces accepted.

## The Principality of the Scale

The collaborating provinces formed the [[Principality of the Scale]], a human nation loyal to the Dragons that actively fought against the Allied Command. The Drakona backed their promise — the provinces under their rule saw reduced taxes, improved infrastructure, and an end to the chronic corruption of the Republic.

## The Permanent Divide

After the war, these humans were branded traitors. This created a permanent "North-South" divide in Varnathi culture that persists into the Third Age. Northerners are seen as "Dragon-Thralls," while Southerners are "Warmongers." Conversely, this era birthed a fierce underground resistance in the north that persists as anti-government militias to this day.
