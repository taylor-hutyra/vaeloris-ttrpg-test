# Ashfall Compact

## Background

Following the [[Ashfall Secession]], the [[Magocracy]] had tried and failed three times to conquer the [[Barony of Ashfall]] and its necro-industrial armies. They needed a different approach -- and a deniable source of forbidden research.

## What Happened

The Magocracy formally recognized the Barony of Ashfall as a sovereign state. In exchange, Ashfall would serve as the world's dark laboratory, conducting the unethical research the Magocracy could not be seen performing.

## Consequences

Ashfall became the supplier of forbidden weapons and technology to fight the Rot, while the Magocracy maintained plausible deniability. This cynical arrangement would be codified in the [[Convention of Accord]].
