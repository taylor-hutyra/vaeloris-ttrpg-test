# Rise of the Cloudborn Corsairs

## Background

Captain [[Kaelen Skysplitter Vance]], a Mirennian ace pilot, grew increasingly disillusioned with the [[Allied Command]]'s willingness to sacrifice crews in the grinding air war against the [[Drakona Empire]].

## What Happened

Vance led a mutiny, taking his squadron and vanishing into the cloud banks. The [[Cloudborn Corsairs]] established hidden bases on floating sky-islands -- chunks of land ripped up by raw magic during the Paroxysm -- and became a third faction in the conflict.

## Consequences

The Corsairs raided both Allied and Drakona supply lines, complicating the war for all sides. Their sky-island bases proved nearly impossible to locate, allowing them to operate with impunity throughout the remainder of the aerial conflict.
