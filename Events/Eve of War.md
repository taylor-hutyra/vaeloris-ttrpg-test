# Eve of War

Communications with the northernmost observation posts ceased. The skies turned a strange, bruise-colored purple. The birds stopped singing.

## Background

Every warning had been given — the [[Beast of Black Rock]], the [[Cloud-Shadow Incidents]], the [[Dragon's Tithe]], the [[Scaled Pact]], the [[Warning of the Wind]]. The surface nations had chosen not to listen.

## What Happened

The [[Drakona]] Invasion Fleet crossed the cloud barrier. The Coalition of Mages activated their emergency protocols, but for most nations the invasion came as a devastating surprise.

## Consequences

The Eve of War marked the end of Epoch V — the Silent Skies. The silence was broken by dragonfire. What followed was the Dragonfall — an era of invasion, occupation, and desperate resistance that would reshape the world.
