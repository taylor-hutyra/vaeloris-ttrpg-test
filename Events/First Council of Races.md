# First Council of Races

Fearing the growing coordination of [[Orkin]] warbands, the nature-attuned [[Waldkyn]] mediated a council at the neutral [[Stone-Circle of Oth]]. Delegates from [[Vernath]] (Human), [[Hannanore]] (Elf), and [[Kharnzarak]] (Dwarf) gathered to address the common threat.

## Background

The [[Season of Breaking]] had become a yearly occurrence, with Orkin raids growing more strategic and coordinated. No single race could patrol the vast borderlands alone.

## What Happened

The council produced the Pact of the Wardens, creating an elite, multi-racial paramilitary force -- the [[Wardens of the Wild]] -- to patrol borders and hunt remnants of the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]]. Crucially, the Orkin were deliberately excluded from the council. Elves cited their chaotic nature; Dwarves cited blood-feuds.

## Consequences

The Pact formally cast the [[Orkin]] as the "Common Enemy," cementing their role as outsiders for millennia. This exclusion would have profound consequences for inter-racial relations throughout the Second Age.
