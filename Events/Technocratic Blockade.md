# Technocratic Blockade

The industrializing Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]] ([[Novaterra]]) ran short of "Brimstone" — high-grade fuel essential to their magitech industry. They blockaded the ports of the [[Federation of City-States]] (Mainland) to force a favorable trade deal.

## The Iron Pact

In a shocking twist, the [[Varnathi Empire]] — historically the bitter enemy of the Federation — formed the Iron Pact, a temporary military alliance with the [[Federation of City-States]] to break the blockade. Former enemies stood shoulder to shoulder against the Novaterran superpower.

## Legacy

For thirty years, Imperial discipline mixed with Federalist innovation. The [[Federation of City-States]] emerged from the conflict with a modernized military doctrine that would eventually allow them to eclipse the Empire. The Technocratic Blockade proved a hard truth: resource needs superseded ideology. Even ancient hatreds could be shelved when survival demanded it.
