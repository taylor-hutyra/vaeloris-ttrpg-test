# Masked Rebellion

Tension arose in [[Gearhaven]] as "Mundane" workers — those without magical ability — felt treated as second-class citizens by the Mages. A group called The Masked seized the geothermal plants, threatening to blow up the city unless they were granted representation on the Cog-Council.

## Background

Despite the Binding Accord's promise of equality, a class divide had formed between those with magical talent and those without. The non-magical laborers who kept the city running were increasingly marginalized.

## What Happened

The Masked occupied the critical geothermal infrastructure that powered all of Gearhaven. Faced with the destruction of their city, the Mages capitulated. The Guild of Labor was granted two seats on the Cog-Council.

## Consequences

The Compromise established Gearhaven as a true meritocracy, where ability — whether magical or mechanical — determined status. This principle of earned worth over inherited talent became central to Gearhaven's identity and future strength.
