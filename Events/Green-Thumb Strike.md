# Green-Thumb Strike

During the height of the [[War of the Barren Hills]], Varnathi legions trampled the [[Halflings|Halfling]] harvest fields of [[Greenmeadow]] to create supply roads. The Halflings did not fight back with weapons — they simply stopped exporting grain and ale.

## The Embargo

Within three months, the Imperial Army faced mutiny due to starvation and plummeting morale. No swords were drawn. No blood was shed. The Halflings demonstrated that the mightiest army in the world marched on its stomach, and that stomach was filled by Halfling hands.

## Treaty of the Hearth

The [[Varnathi Republic]] was forced to sign a humiliating treaty granting [[Greenmeadow]] permanent neutrality and forbidding military boots on their soil. The Green-Thumb Strike established the [[Halflings]] as a "Sleeping Giant" of logistics whom no army dares anger — a reputation that endures to this day.
