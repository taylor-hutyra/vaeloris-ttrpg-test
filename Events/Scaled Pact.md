# Scaled Pact

The [[Drakona]] High Command approached the [[Pirate Lords]] of [[Serpent's Tooth]]. In exchange for "Air Cover" — immunity from navies — and a share of the spoils, the Pirates agreed to act as the Drakona's eyes and ears on the surface.

## Background

The [[Dragon's Tithe]] had demonstrated the Drakona's ability to project power onto the surface, but they lacked detailed intelligence about human defenses. They needed agents on the ground.

## What Happened

The Pirate Lords, offered protection that no navy could match, eagerly accepted the arrangement. Piracy spiked uncontrollably as the Pirates operated with impunity under Drakona air cover.

## Consequences

The Drakona gained detailed maps of human defenses, fleet dispositions, and coastal fortifications without risking a single scout of their own. The surface nations were being mapped for invasion.
