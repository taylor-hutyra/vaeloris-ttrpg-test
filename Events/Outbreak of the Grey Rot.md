# Outbreak of the Grey Rot

## Background

In the craters where the largest Cloud Cities fell during the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual|Skyfall]], the fabric of reality thinned. [[Kael'Zorai]]'s corrupted essence began seeping through the cracks between worlds.

## What Happened

The corruption manifested as the Grey Rot: a magical plague that turned flesh to ash and made stone weep blood. Panic spread as conventional healing magic was found to accelerate the Rot rather than cure it. The [[Magocracy]] cordoned off vast swaths of land, condemning millions to death to save the rest.

## Consequences

The Rot crisis became the defining challenge of the early Mage Ascendancy. It drove the [[Rise of the Plague-Wardens]], inspired [[Elaruniel]]'s [[Publication of The Frayed Weave]], and ultimately provided the [[Magocracy]] with the justification to consolidate absolute power over the nations of Vaeloris.
