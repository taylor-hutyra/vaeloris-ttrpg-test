# Rise of the Plague-Wardens

## Background

While Human mages faltered against the [[Outbreak of the Grey Rot|Grey Rot]], the [[Gnomes]] of the [[Mycelial Collective]] brought their unique understanding of fungal networks to bear on the crisis.

## What Happened

The Gnomes discovered that the Rot was a fungal-magical hybrid, not a purely arcane phenomenon. They developed "Spore-Masks" and alchemical sprays that could slow its spread -- the first effective countermeasures against the plague.

## Consequences

Gnomish prestige soared as they became the frontline medics of the crisis. Many Gnomes began incorporating the Rot into their study of the "Shared Pattern," sowing the seeds for the "Integrator" faction that would emerge later in history.
