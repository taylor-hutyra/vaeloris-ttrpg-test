# Hymn of the Hidden Star

In [[Mrenr]], a blind musician named [[Sylla]] gained fame for her haunting compositions. Upon her deathbed, she revealed herself as an [[Aetheris]] who had lived a mortal life to understand human suffering.

## Background

Since the [[Herald in Flame]], the Aetheris had been understood as transcendent beings who appeared only in moments of crisis. Sylla's revelation shattered that understanding.

## What Happened

The "Velo Aspectus" — the revelation that Aetheris could walk among mortals in disguise — caused a religious fervor. If a cosmic being chose to experience mortal suffering, what did that say about the value of mortal life?

## Consequences

"Sylla's Hymn" became the anthem of the [[Church of the Celestial Word]]. The revelation deepened faith and intensified the spiritual dimension of the era, but also sowed paranoia — anyone could be an Aetheris in disguise.
