# Unification of the High Plateau

[[Caelius I]], the great-grandson of Vardas (founder of [[Vernath]]), consolidated the seven major highland city-states through a mix of diplomacy and the "War of the Seven Spears." He declared the end of the tribal confederacy and the birth of the [[Varnathi Empire]].

## Background

With the [[Defeat of the Echo-Lord]], the survival mentality of Epoch I gave way to ambition. Caelius I claimed a "Mandate of Order" -- that since the gods had left, it was Man's duty to impose structure upon the chaotic world.

## What Happened

Standing atop the walls of [[Vernath]], Caelius I declared the birth of the Varnathi Empire. The "First Compact" was amended to include loyalty to the Throne alongside the State, centralizing military power under the Emperor.

## Consequences

The Unification marked the beginning of Epoch II and the Age of Ambition. The Varnathi Empire would rapidly expand through the [[Standardization of Steel]], the [[March of Order]], and the [[Codex of the Road]].
