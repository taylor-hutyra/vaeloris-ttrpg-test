# Desertborn Confederacy Founded

On the mainland, the chaos of the Guild Wars and the distraction of the [[Varnathi Republic]] allowed the nomadic tribes of the [[Southern Sands]] to unite. Under the [[Lothari Mystics]], they seized control of the overland Silk Road.

## The Toll of Sand

Any trade moving between East and West by land now paid tribute to the [[Desertborn Confederacy]]. This stranglehold on overland commerce made the Confederacy wealthy without the need for conquest or industry.

## Preservation of the Past

The [[Lothari Mystics]] formally organized the Silent Network — a secret order dedicated to archiving histories, artifacts, and myths in hidden desert vaults. They believed the "Age of Noise" (the era of Expansion) would eventually collapse, and the world would need its memory back. Their foresight preserved knowledge of the First Age and [[The Paroxysm]] that would otherwise have been lost to secular revisionism.
