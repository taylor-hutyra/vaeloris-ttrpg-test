# Silver Boom

A [[Thulkor'n]] prospector named [[Grogor the Eye]] discovered the "Deep-Vein" in the [[Central Mountains]] — a massive deposit of nearly pure silver. The [[Varnathi Empire]] annexed the region immediately.

## The Rise of Argentum

A mining camp exploded into [[Argentum]], a metropolis of stone and coin. The wealth pouring from the Deep-Vein transformed the economic landscape of the Empire, creating a new class of "Silver Lords" — merchant-nobility whose fortunes would eventually rival the Emperor's own power.

## Political Consequences

The Silver Lords represented a fundamental shift in imperial politics. Wealth, not just birth or military service, could now command power. This tension between the old aristocracy and the new money would simmer for a century before erupting in the [[Bloodless Coup]] of SA:581.
