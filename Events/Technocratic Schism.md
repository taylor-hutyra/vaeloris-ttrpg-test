# Technocratic Schism

## Background

[[Gearhaven]] faced a constitutional crisis. The [[Magocracy]] demanded all magical resources be hoarded to fight the [[Outbreak of the Grey Rot|Grey Rot]], while the Gearhaven engineers insisted on rebuilding the economy.

## What Happened

The [[Cog-Council]] split between mage loyalists and the engineering faction. After heated debate, a new constitution was written: the Binding Accord II. Gearhaven formally recognized the Magocracy's authority over magic but retained absolute autonomy over machines.

## Consequences

This compromise preserved Gearhaven's engineering tradition and independence. The autonomy over machines clause would prove critical centuries later, enabling the [[Steam Revolution]] that began to shift power away from the Magocracy.
