# Rise of the Merchant Princes

In the [[Mirennian League]], the "Council of Captains" evolved into a hereditary aristocracy. The [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] bought out its rivals, establishing a monopoly on the spice trade.

## Background

The [[Rise of the Merchant Houses]] had transformed Mirennian governance from tribal leadership to mercantile oligarchy. Over centuries, wealth concentrated into fewer hands.

## What Happened

The gap between rich and poor in the League widened dramatically. The Golden Compass Syndicate's monopoly drove prices up while wages stagnated, leading to civil unrest in the dock districts. Organized crime syndicates arose to protect the lower classes, and the elite retreated into walled "Gilded Districts."

## Consequences

The extreme inequality planted seeds for the [[Mirennian Guild Wars]] that would erupt when the Syndicate's monopoly eventually collapsed. The concentration of wealth also attracted the piracy of the [[Scourge of Serpent's Tooth]].
