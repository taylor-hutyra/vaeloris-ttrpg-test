# Waldkyn Migration

Intrigued by the cosmopolitan success of [[Morenore]], a faction of [[Waldkyn]] (primarily [[Simiah]] and [[Fungril]]) left the [[Whispering Vales]]. They believed the "Synthesis" could include civilization, not just nature.

## Background

The [[Founding of Morenore]] demonstrated that different races could build something greater together. Progressive Waldkyn questioned whether remaining isolated in the Vales was the only path.

## What Happened

The migrants became the [[Chartered Kith]], settling in urban enclaves and becoming master architects and mediators. They began experimenting with early bio-architecture -- living buildings grown from modified trees.

## Consequences

This was the first major cultural split within the Waldkyn people, mirroring the earlier [[Sael'anir Separation]] among the Elves. The Chartered Kith's bio-architectural innovations would later influence the founding of Nexus on Novaterra.
