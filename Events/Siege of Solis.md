# Siege of Solis

The Republican Legion of [[Castellum]] besieged the Mirennian city of [[Solis]]. The siege lasted two years before being broken not by armies, but by the "Grey Death" -- dysentery caused by unsanitary conditions in the massive army camps.

## Background

The [[Salt War]] had reached a critical point, with Republican forces pushing deep into Mirennian territory. Solis was a key strategic target on the southern coast.

## What Happened

The siege ground on for two years with neither side able to break the stalemate. The "Grey Death" -- dysentery born of unsanitary conditions -- ravaged the besieging army, forcing a withdrawal. The disease then spread back to the cities as the Soldier's Plague, killing more than the war itself.

## Consequences

The siege drove advancements in field medicine and sanitation. The devastating plague that followed helped push both sides toward the [[Morenore Accords|ceasefire]].
