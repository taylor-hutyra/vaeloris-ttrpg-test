# Orkin Awakening

In the eastern provinces of the [[Varnathi Republic]], slave labor — mostly captured Orkin and indebted humans — fueled the economy. An Orkin gladiator named [[Gruk the Breaker]] discovered he was a "Spark-User," an Earth-Mage who could infuse his skin with stone.

## Background

The Orkin had been systematically enslaved and exploited by the Republic for generations. The discovery that an Orkin could wield magic shattered assumptions about who could be a mage.

## What Happened

In the Stone-Fist Revolt, Gruk literally shattered the walls of the slave pens with his bare, stone-infused hands. Thousands of Orkin and human slaves rallied to him, seizing a swath of territory that became known as the Free Marches.

## Consequences

The Free Marches represented the first serious Orkin-led challenge to the Republic's authority. It drew Republican military resources away from other fronts and inspired hope among the oppressed across the continent.
