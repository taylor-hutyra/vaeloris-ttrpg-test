# Technocratic Isolation

Disgusted by the corruption of the mainland, the Cog-Council of [[Gearhaven]] passed the Isolation Edict. They restricted the export of advanced magitech to the Old World.

## Background

The [[Rise of the Oligarchs]] and general political decay of the mainland powers convinced the Technocracy that engagement with the Old World was counterproductive and morally compromising.

## What Happened

The Isolation Edict cut off the flow of advanced technomantic inventions to the mainland. [[Novaterra]] continued to advance rapidly — developing early computation and steam-turbines — while the mainland stagnated in the medieval-arcane era.

## Consequences

The technological divergence between Novaterra and the mainland grew into a chasm. This isolation would eventually prove unsustainable when [[Gearhaven]] needed resources it could not produce domestically, leading to the [[Technocratic Blockade]].
