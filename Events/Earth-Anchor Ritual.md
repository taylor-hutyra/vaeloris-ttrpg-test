# Earth-Anchor Ritual

## Background

Following the [[Conclave of Luminas]], [[Elaruniel]] was tasked with designing a ritual to exploit the Aeromancy vulnerability discovered during [[Operation Icarus]]. The result was the Earth-Anchor -- a metaphysical weapon of unprecedented scale.

## What Happened

On the winter solstice, thousands of Allied mages ascended the highest peaks of Vaeloris. At noon, they cast the Earth-Anchor: a metaphysical hook that grabbed the concept of "Flight" within the Drakona's Aeromancy matrix and violently grounded it. The great Cloud Cities plummeted. Millions of Drakona died in the impact.

## Consequences

The ritual was too broad. It destabilized all flight magic -- every Allied Airship, every Privateer vessel, every floating island also fell. In one hour, the Age of Flight ended and the skies were empty. The survivors crawled from the wreckage into the [[Ground War]], and the massive magical energies released would soon manifest as the [[Outbreak of the Grey Rot]].
