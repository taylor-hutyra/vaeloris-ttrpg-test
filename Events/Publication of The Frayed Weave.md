# Publication of The Frayed Weave

## Background

Haunted by her role in the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual|Skyfall]], [[Elaruniel]] emerged from seclusion in [[Aethelas]] to publish a dense theoretical text analyzing the true cost of the ritual.

## What Happened

The Frayed Weave argued that the Skyfall did not just break the [[Drakona Empire|Drakona]] -- it broke the immune system of the universe itself. The [[Magocracy]] denounced the book as alarmist heresy, banning it in human lands.

## Consequences

Despite the ban, The Frayed Weave became the bible of the [[Aen'valar]] and the secret societies of Novaterra. It created an Academic Underground that challenged the Magocracy's narrative of having saved the world, planting seeds of intellectual resistance.
