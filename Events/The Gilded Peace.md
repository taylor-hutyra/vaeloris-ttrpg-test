# The Gilded Peace

With magic legalized and integrated, a global economic boom occurred. The [[Federation of City-States]] and the Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]] established the "Trans-Oceanic Trade Route," bypassing the tariffs of the Varnathi and Mirennians.

## Background

The end of the Arcane Revolution had ushered in an era of stability known as the "Pax Magica." Trade flourished as magical transportation and communication accelerated commerce across the world.

## What Happened

[[Novaterra]] industrialized rapidly, leveraging its technomantic advantages. The Trans-Oceanic Trade Route allowed the Federation and Technocracy to trade directly, cutting out the traditional middlemen.

## Consequences

While Novaterra prospered, the [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] and [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] economies began to stagnate, sowing the seeds of social unrest that would destabilize the old powers in the coming centuries.
