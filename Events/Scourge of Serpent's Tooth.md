# Scourge of Serpent's Tooth

As the [[Mirennian League|League]] and [[Varnathi Republic|Republic]] grew richer, piracy exploded. [[Captain Vane]], a renegade Mirennian admiral, united the disparate pirate crews of the southern archipelago and fortified the island of [[Serpent's Tooth]].

## Background

The growing maritime trade between the League and Republic created rich targets for pirates. Previously disorganized, the pirate crews lacked the coordination to pose a strategic threat.

## What Happened

Captain Vane established the "Code of the Coast" and transformed Serpent's Tooth into a fortified pirate nation. Under his leadership, piracy became a geopolitical power rather than a mere nuisance.

## Consequences

Both the Varnathi and Mirennian navies were forced to convoy merchant ships, driving up prices and slowing trade. Vane's growing boldness would eventually lead to the [[Blockade of Port Farrick]].
