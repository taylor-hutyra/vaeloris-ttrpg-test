# Landing at Rust-Water

A massive flotilla of refugee ships, carrying a mix of human mages, exiled [[Elm-korin]] Dwarves, and opportunistic [[Gnomes]], landed in a desolate bay on [[Novaterra]]. They found the land rich in geothermal vents — a natural source of power.

## Background

The refugees had fled the [[Mage-Purge Wars]] via the [[Mirennian Exodus]], paying exorbitant prices for passage. After weeks at sea, they reached the unknown shores of [[Novaterra]].

## What Happened

The landing site at Rust-Water Bay was barren but promising. The geothermal vents provided heat and energy, and the bay offered natural shelter. The diverse group of refugees — humans, Dwarves, and Gnomes — made the collective decision to stop running and start building.

## Consequences

This decision marked the birth of what would become the Technocracy. The collaboration between races with complementary skills — Dwarven engineering, human magic, Gnomish invention — would produce the revolutionary fusion of magic and technology known as Technomancy.
