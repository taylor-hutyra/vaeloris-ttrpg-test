# Founding of Grand University

## Background

The [[Magocracy]] recognized that controlling the present was not enough -- they needed to control the narrative of the past. Higher education had remained fragmented and potentially subversive.

## What Happened

The Magocracy centralized all higher education in [[Morenore]], founding the Grand University. Ostensibly open to all, the curriculum was heavily curated to promote the Magocracy's version of history.

## Consequences

Generations of leaders were raised to view the Magocracy as benevolent guardians who saved the world from the Grey Rot and the Drakona. The indoctrination was effective, though institutions like [[Aethelas]] would soon challenge this intellectual monopoly through the [[Loremaster's Mandate]].
