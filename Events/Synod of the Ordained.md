# Synod of the Ordained

To unify the disparate cultures within the expanding [[Varnathi Empire]], the [[Imperial Priesthood]] established the Pantheon of the Ordained. This state religion syncretized local beliefs into a structured hierarchy, emphasizing gods of Law, Harvest, and War.

## Theological Imposition

The Synod deliberately suppressed "chaotic" nature worship, deeming it primitive and unsuitable for an ordered empire. This created religious tension with the [[Mirennian League]], who favored fluid, elemental deities connected to the sea and wind.

## Alienation of the Waldkyn

The [[Waldkyn]], whose nature-worship formed the core of their identity, were deeply alienated by the Synod's pronouncements. This religious imperialism widened the cultural gap between the Empire and the forest-dwelling peoples, sowing seeds of mistrust that would persist for ages.
