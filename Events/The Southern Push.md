# The Southern Push

[[Vernath]], its population booming due to trade and safe roads, began colonizing the southern lowlands. These lands were wild, filled with [[Primalkyn]] anomalies. The expansion was bloody, a war against the land itself.

## Background

The [[Stone-Road Projects]] had dramatically reduced merchant mortality and enabled rapid economic growth. With the [[Orkin]] driven to the [[Barren Hills]], the southern lowlands were nominally open for settlement.

## What Happened

Varnathi colonists pushed south into territory still scarred by the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]]. Primalkyn manifestations made the expansion perilous, requiring constant military escort and systematic study of the anomalies.

## Consequences

The military outposts established during the Southern Push eventually grew into the fortress-city of [[Castellum]], which would become culturally distinct from the philosophical capital of [[Vernath]]. Human scholars began the first systematic categorization of [[Primalkyn]] manifestations.
