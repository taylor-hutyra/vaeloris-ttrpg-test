# Kingdom of the Silver Hand

General [[Aric the Silver]], a hero of the [[Varnathi Republic]], discovered his own latent magical ability. Rather than submit to the [[Mage-Purge Wars|Mage-Purge]], he turned his legion against the Senate and carved out a sanctuary kingdom on the borderlands.

## Background

Aric briefly allied with local Orkin tribes to hold the line against Republican forces. For fifty years, the Kingdom of the Silver Hand was a utopia for the persecuted — a place where mages could live openly.

## What Happened

As Aric succumbed to the Fraying — the madness caused by channeling raw magic without proper training — the Orkin leaders betrayed him to the Republic in exchange for amnesty. The Republic accepted their help, crushed the Kingdom, and then slaughtered the Orkin anyway.

## Consequences

The fall of the Kingdom created the enduring cultural stigma of the "Mage-Tyrant" in human lands and cemented Orkin cynicism toward human alliances. The Republic's treachery toward the Orkin who had aided them reinforced the cycle of mistrust between the races.
