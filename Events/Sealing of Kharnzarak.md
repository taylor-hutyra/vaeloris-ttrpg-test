# Sealing of Kharnzarak

In the final year of Epoch III, High King [[Thorgar VI]] ordered the Great Adamant Gates of [[Kharnzarak]] sealed. The runes were hammered shut. For the next several centuries, the Dwarven Kingdom would be a tomb of silence to the outside world.

## The Final Act

The sealing was the culmination of centuries of growing isolation, from the [[Dwarven Isolation|Closing of the Lower Deep]] in SA:615 to the [[Paranoia of High King|purges of Thorgar VI]]. Every gate, every passage, every ventilation shaft was sealed with runic wards. The mountain became impenetrable.

## A World Without Dwarves

The [[Wandering Kin]] and the Novaterran [[Free Holds]] became the only visible face of Dwarvenkind to the rest of the world. The loss of Dwarven stability and craftsmanship left a vacuum — and with the old, reliable powers retreated underground, the ambitious and unstable younger races were free to stumble upon forces they did not understand. The Age of Arcane Revolution was about to begin.
