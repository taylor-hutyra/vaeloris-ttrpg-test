# Whisperwood Outbreak

## Background

The Grey Rot, declared "contained" centuries earlier in the [[Containment Declaration]], was never truly eliminated -- only caged. In SA:3217, it broke through in the [[Barony of Whisperwood]].

## What Happened

The [[Magocracy]] responded with terrifying efficiency, burning the entire barony to the ground in a single night. Every structure, every field, and the ancient whisperwood trees themselves were reduced to ash.

## Consequences

The growing rebelliousness of the populace was temporarily suppressed. "Better the Mages than the Rot" became the common refrain, reminding the world why the Magocracy ruled -- even as the event proved their claim to have contained the Rot was a lie.
