# March of Order

Emperor [[Caelius I]], great-grandson of [[Vardas the Elder|Vardas]] and first ruler of the [[Varnathi Empire]], launched the "March of Order" into the southern wilds. These territories had been held by scattered [[Orkin]] clans since their retreat in Epoch I.

## The Campaign

Using the new steel legions — standardized armor and weapons perfected with [[Thulkor'n]] Dwarven consultants — and rigid phalanx tactics, the Empire shattered the disorganized Orkin warbands. [[Caelius I]] claimed a "Mandate of Order": since the gods had left, it was Man's duty to impose structure upon the chaotic world.

## Founding of Castellum

A massive fortress was built on the southern frontier to hold the conquered territory. [[Castellum]] quickly evolved into a militaristic city-state, culturally distinct from the philosophical capital of [[Vernath]]. The [[Orkin]] were pushed further into the inhospitable badlands and western marshes.
