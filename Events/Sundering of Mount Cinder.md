# Sundering of Mount Cinder

The dormant volcano on the Mirennian outpost island of [[Vol]] erupted with cataclysmic force. The explosion was not natural — later scholars theorize a Mirennian Wind-Shaper attempted to tap into geothermal vents and failed catastrophically.

## The Gloom Famine

Volcanic ash choked the skies for three years, devastating agriculture across the region. The "Gloom Famine" echoed the "Years of Grey" that followed [[The Paroxysm]], reminding the world that nature's fury could match any divine catastrophe.

## The Tsunami of Tears

Massive waves destroyed forty percent of the [[Mirennian League]]'s fleet and harbor infrastructure. The economic backbone of the League was shattered in a single day. This devastation left the Mirennians vulnerable and reshaped the balance of power between land and sea for generations.

## The Bronr'n Connection

The seismic shockwaves from the eruption were felt as far as [[Kharnzarak]], contributing to the geological instability that would eventually cause the Earthwake and the [[Bronr'n]] Exodus centuries later.
