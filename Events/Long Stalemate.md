# Long Stalemate

## Background

Following the [[Battle of Serpent's Pass]], the [[Drakona Empire]] retreated to their high-altitude Cloud Cities. The war settled into an exhausting stalemate with no clear path to victory for either side.

## What Happened

The Drakona held the "High Sky" above 20,000 feet, while the Allies held the "Low Sky" and the ground. Neither side could strike at the other's heart. The world was exhausted after centuries of continuous warfare.

## Consequences

The grinding futility of the stalemate drove the [[Allied Command]] to authorize increasingly desperate measures, ultimately leading to [[Operation Icarus]] -- the suicide reconnaissance mission that discovered the secret of the Cloud Cities.
