# Mirennian Exodus

The [[Mirennian League]], ever pragmatic, saw an opportunity in the [[Mage-Purge Wars]]. They publicly condemned magic to appease the [[Varnathi Republic]] but privately sent agents to offer passage to fleeing mages.

## Background

The Purge created a massive refugee crisis. Thousands of mages and their families were desperate to escape the Republic. The Mirennians positioned themselves as the only viable route to safety.

## What Happened

The price for passage to [[Novaterra]] was exorbitant — a lifetime of servitude, family heirlooms, or magical secrets. The "Ticket of Gold" became a byword for Mirennian exploitation. Yet for the desperate, it was the only choice.

## Consequences

Mirennian coffers overflowed with the proceeds, funding the restoration of their aging fleets. The refugee ships would eventually reach [[Novaterra]], culminating in the [[Landing at Rust-Water]] and the founding of a new civilization.
