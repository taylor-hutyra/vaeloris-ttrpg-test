# Salt War

With food scarce due to the Gloom Famine, salt became more valuable than silver. The [[Varnathi Republic]], seeing the [[Mirennian League]] weakened by the [[Sundering of Mount Cinder|Tsunami of Tears]], launched an invasion to seize the vast salt flats of the southern coast.

## Background

The [[Sundering of Mount Cinder]] had choked the skies with ash for three years and destroyed 40% of the Mirennian fleet. Salt, essential for food preservation during the famine, became the most valuable commodity.

## What Happened

The Republic launched the first large-scale mortal-versus-mortal conflict in the history of Vaeloris. The League, lacking soldiers, hired thousands of [[Thulkorun]] Dwarves and [[Orkin]] exiles as mercenaries. The war dragged on through the [[Siege of Solis]] until mediated by the [[Morenore Accords]].

## Consequences

The Salt War established the pattern of total war between the Republic and the League. The rise of mercenary forces changed the nature of warfare, and veterans of the conflict would go on to found the [[Rise of the Shadewalkers|Shadewalkers]].
