# Conclave of Luminas

## Background

Following [[Operation Icarus]], the Allied leadership possessed the knowledge to destroy the Drakona Cloud Cities -- but doing so would mean killing millions of civilians. Meanwhile, intelligence indicated the Drakona were preparing a "Final Descent" weapon capable of inducing a magical ice age.

## What Happened

The Allied leadership gathered at [[Luminas]] to debate the "Icarus Option." Military generals demanded the strike. Religious leaders of the [[Church]] pleaded for mercy. With the threat of the Final Descent weapon looming, the Alliance voted to strike.

## Consequences

[[Elaruniel]] was tasked with designing the ritual that would become the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual]]. The decision to prioritize survival over mercy would haunt the nations of Vaeloris for centuries and fundamentally reshape the moral landscape of the world.
