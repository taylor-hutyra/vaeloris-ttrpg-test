# Warning of the Silent Canopy

As Human logging operations began cutting into the ancient woods bordering [[Hannanore]], the [[Cirhannim]] Elves sent a single, white-robed emissary to the court of [[Vernath]].

## Background

The [[Completion of the Great Weaving]] had sealed [[Hannanore]] behind a barrier of mist, but the forests outside the barrier still held ancient power. Human expansion threatened these border woods.

## What Happened

The emissary delivered a prophecy to the Council of Vernath: "The forest breathes. The stone remembers. Should you deafen the world with the clamor of your ambition, you will one day find yourselves screaming into silence."

## Consequences

The warning was dismissed by the ruling Council of [[Vernath]] as arrogance. Human expansion continued, though a lingering superstition took hold among the common woodcutters who worked the forest edge.
