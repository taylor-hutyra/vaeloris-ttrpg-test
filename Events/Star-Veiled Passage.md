# Star-Veiled Passage

The [[Aen'valar]] Elves of [[Mrenr]], disgusted by the brutality of the [[Mage-Purge Wars|Purge]], intervened covertly. They established the Star-Veiled Passage — an underground railroad for persecuted mages.

## Background

While the [[Varnathi Republic]] hunted mages with the Legio Null, the Elves could not stand idle. Their long memory recalled the devastating consequences of suppressing knowledge and persecuting the gifted.

## What Happened

Using [[Waldkyn]] guides who knew every hidden trail and sympathetic [[Halflings|Halfling]] smugglers who could move people through ports undetected, the Star-Veiled Passage moved refugees to neutral territories. The operation saved over 4,000 "Spark-Users" from execution.

## Consequences

The covert operation deepened the rift between the Elves and the Human Republic. It demonstrated that the elder races would not passively accept human brutality, setting the stage for future interventions.
