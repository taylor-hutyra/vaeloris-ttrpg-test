# Treaty of Ash and Iron

The Succession Wars ended in mutual exhaustion. Warlord [[Aetius IV]] of [[Castellum]] had conquered [[Vernath]] but realized he could not hold it without the support of the merchant guilds.

## Background

The [[The Bleeding Years|Bleeding Years]] had devastated the mainland for forty years. No faction had the strength to achieve total victory. A compromise was the only path forward.

## What Happened

The Treaty of Ash and Iron established the Varnathi Imperial Democracy — a hybrid government where a hereditary Emperor (controlled by the military) presided over an elected Senate (controlled by the merchants). It was a stable, if corrupt, compromise.

## Consequences

The new government restored order to the mainland and ended the Succession Wars. Though riddled with structural compromises, it provided the stability needed for rebuilding — and prompted rival powers to respond with the [[Declaration of Sovereignty]].
