# Loremaster's Mandate

## Background

At the [[Aen'valar]] Conclave in [[Aethelas]], a pivotal election pitted two visions of the Elven future against each other. [[Archmage Voronwe]], a [[Magocracy]] loyalist, argued for isolation. [[Elaruniel]] argued for engagement to fix the Fraying.

## What Happened

Elaruniel ran for Loremaster Prime against Voronwe. Decades of underground academic support -- built on the foundation of her banned [[Publication of The Frayed Weave|Frayed Weave]] -- delivered a landslide victory.

## Consequences

Under Elaruniel's leadership, [[Aethelas]] began to drift away from the [[Magocracy]]'s influence, becoming a bastion of independent thought and the primary institutional challenger to the Magocracy's control over knowledge and history.
