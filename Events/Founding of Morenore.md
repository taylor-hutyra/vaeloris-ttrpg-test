# Founding of Morenore

Rejecting the isolationism of [[Hannanore]], the progressive [[Aen'valar]] Elves joined with human architects and [[Waldkyn]] diplomats to found [[Morenore]] on a neutral peninsula. It was the first "Open City," governed by a Triumvirate representing the three founding races.

## Background

The [[Morenore Accords]] had established Morenore as a neutral sanctuary. The [[Aen'valar]], progressive Elves in the tradition of the [[Sael'anir Separation]], sought to build something permanent.

## What Happened

The three founding races established a Triumvirate government, making Morenore the first truly multi-racial city. Scholars standardized the "Common" language, facilitating global trade and diplomacy.

## Consequences

Morenore became the center of an Intellectual Renaissance in philosophy, art, and law. The standardization of Common language was perhaps its most lasting contribution, enabling communication across cultures for the rest of the Second Age. Its success inspired the [[Waldkyn Migration]].
