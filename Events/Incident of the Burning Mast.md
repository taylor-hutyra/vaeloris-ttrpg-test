# Incident of the Burning Mast

An Imperial warship attempted to blockade a [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] port to enforce tariffs. In response, a Mirennian "Sea-Whisperer" — an early elementalist — summoned a localized gale that drove the ship onto the rocks.

## Significance

The incident marked the first recorded combat use of Wind-Shaping, a ritualistic communion with the elemental remnants of [[Gwynfaer|Gwynfaer the Windweaver]]. It demonstrated that the [[Mirennian League]]'s mastery of the sea went beyond seamanship — they commanded the elements themselves.

## Strategic Impact

The [[Varnathi Empire]] was forced to accept a harsh reality: it could not challenge the League on the open ocean. This realization defined the strategic balance of the Second Age for centuries. The land belonged to the Empire; the sea belonged to [[Mirennian League|Mirennia]]. The incident directly inspired the [[Perfection of Wind-Shaping]] three decades later.
