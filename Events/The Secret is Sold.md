# The Secret is Sold

A disgruntled [[Azure Hand]] bookkeeper, fleeing the [[Mirennian Guild Wars]], sold the "Ghost Charts" of [[Novaterra]] to the Varnathi Consul of Trade. The Republic immediately commissioned an expeditionary fleet.

## Background

The [[Mirennian Guild Wars]] had fractured the League. A bookkeeper from the Azure Hand, seeking to escape the violence and profit from what he knew, approached the Republic with the most valuable intelligence in the world.

## What Happened

The Ghost Charts -- enchanted maps revealing the route to Novaterra -- were sold to the Varnathi Republic. This ended centuries of Mirennian monopoly on the new continent. The Republic launched a massive colonization effort to catch up to the League.

## Consequences

The "Great Scramble" began as the Republic rushed to establish a presence on Novaterra. Panic swept through [[Windsail]] as the colonists prepared for invasion. The inevitable clash came at the [[Skirmish of Red Clay]].
