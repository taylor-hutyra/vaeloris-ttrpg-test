# Sky-Tax Crisis

## Background

Humiliated by the legal loophole that allowed mechanical flight despite the [[First Decree]]'s ban on Aeromancy, the [[Magocracy]] sought to reassert control through economics rather than law.

## What Happened

The Magocracy imposed a heavy tax on "Atmospheric Displacement." The Merchant Houses of the [[Mirennian League]] refused to pay. The [[Federation of City-States]] mobilized its (illegal) reserves of battle-mages in a show of solidarity. Faced with potential open revolt, the Magocracy backed down, agreeing to a reduced tax in exchange for cargo inspection rights.

## Consequences

It was the first time the Magocracy blinked. The concession demonstrated that their absolute power was eroding, accelerating the political dynamics that would lead to the [[Convention of Accord]].
