# Rise of the Free Holds

The displaced [[Orkin]] and [[Bronr'n]] [[Dwarves]] on [[Novaterra]], rejecting the authority of both Human colonies, moved inland to the rugged central highlands. They established the [[Free Holds]] — fortified, independent city-states based on meritocracy and survival.

## A New Political Model

Unlike the feudal structures of the mainland or the oligarchic colonies of the coast, the Free Holds were governed by proven ability. An Orc who could build was valued equally to a Dwarf who could fight. This radical egalitarianism was born of desperation — neither race could afford the luxury of prejudice.

## Tri-Polar Novaterra

The establishment of the Free Holds transformed [[Novaterra]] into a tri-polar political landscape: the Varnathi East, the Mirennian West, and the Independent Central Highlands. This three-way tension would define the continent's politics for centuries to come.
