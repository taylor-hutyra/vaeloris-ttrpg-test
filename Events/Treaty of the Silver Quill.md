# Treaty of the Silver Quill

The humiliated [[Varnathi Republic]] was forced to sign a treaty with the Free Peoples of [[Novaterra]] following the devastating [[Battle of Crimson Field]].

## Background

The Republic's total military defeat left it with no bargaining power. The Coalition of the Arcane dictated terms from a position of absolute strength.

## What Happened

The treaty's terms were sweeping: immediate end to the [[Mage-Purge Wars|Mage-Purge]], legalization of magic with regulation, and formal recognition of [[Gearhaven]] as a sovereign power. Centuries of persecution were officially ended with the stroke of a pen.

## Consequences

The treaty reshaped the political order of the world. Nations scrambled to adapt to the new reality in the [[Great Arcane Integration]], and the balance of power shifted permanently away from conventional military might toward arcane capability.
