# Founding of the Church

In the neutral city of [[Luminas]], a former soldier turned pacifist named [[Iovan]] began preaching. He did not call for the return of the old gods, but for the "Celestial Word" — the idea that the universe responds to moral resonance.

## Background

The [[The Bleeding Years|Bleeding Years]] had shattered the secular confidence of the previous epochs. The promises that mortals could thrive without divine guidance rang hollow amid famine, war, and suffering.

## What Happened

[[Iovan]] founded the Church of the Celestial Word. Rather than theology, he offered practical aid — soup kitchens, healing, and hope. The Church filled the void left by the collapsed state, providing community and purpose to the desperate.

## Consequences

The Church grew rapidly, becoming a major political and spiritual force. Its doctrine — that the cosmos listens and responds to moral action — would soon receive dramatic validation at the [[Herald in Flame]].
