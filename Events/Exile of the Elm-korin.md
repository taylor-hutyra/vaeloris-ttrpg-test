# Exile of the Elm-korin

In [[Kharnzarak]], a sect of Dwarven smiths led by [[Tharum Flame-Touched]] claimed that magic was simply another ore to be worked. They performed the "Ritual of the Soul-Forge," infusing a hammer with arcane fire. The High King declared this a corruption of the Stone.

## Background

The [[Dwarven Isolation]] had turned Dwarven culture increasingly traditionalist. Any deviation from orthodox smithing practices was viewed with suspicion, and the exploration of magic was considered heretical.

## What Happened

The [[Elm-korin]] faction was exiled in the "Great Banishment." Unlike the [[Thulkorun]] (criminals exiled through the Ritual of Shattering), these were visionaries cast out for innovation. They wandered the human lands, seeking a place to build.

## Consequences

The Elm-korin would eventually join with the [[Thulkorun]] to form the [[Network of the Wandering Kin]], and later play a crucial role in founding [[Gearhaven]] on Novaterra. Their exile drained Kharnzarak of its most innovative thinkers.
