# Pact of Bark and Bronze

During maintenance of the Great Weaving, Elven wards began to destabilize due to iron ore deposits in the forest floor. A reclusive Gnome artisan, [[Tinbrel Quillroot]], revealed himself to the [[Elven High Council]] and offered a solution.

## The Solution

[[Tinbrel Quillroot]] proposed weaving bronze lattice-work into the roots of the border trees to ground the magic. The work was done in secret — the existence of [[Gnomes]] within [[Hannan'r']] was unknown to the outside world.

## The Hollows

In exchange for his service, the first "Hollows" — Gnomish homes — were established beneath the roots of Elven great-trees. This created a symbiotic relationship between the two races: Gnomish ingenuity supporting Elven magic, hidden beneath the forest floor. This partnership would endure for millennia.
