# Orkin Integration

To rebuild the shattered infrastructure of the Empire, Emperor [[Aetius IV]] passed the Labor Integration Act. [[Varr-Darg|Orkin]] were granted citizenship — of a sort — in exchange for labor.

## Background

The [[The Bleeding Years|Bleeding Years]] had devastated the Empire's infrastructure and depleted its labor force. The [[Treaty of Ash and Iron]] had restored political order, but rebuilding required hands the Empire did not have.

## What Happened

Orkin workers flooded into human cities, forming the "Orkin Quarters" — designated districts where they lived and worked. While legally citizens, they were socially ostracized and excluded from mainstream society.

## Consequences

The social ostracism drove Orkin communities to form tight-knit, mafia-like Guilds for mutual protection. These Guilds became parallel power structures within Imperial cities — a legacy of segregation that would persist for centuries.
