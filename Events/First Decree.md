# First Decree

## Background

With the [[Fall of Vrak'Thul]] ensuring total submission and the [[Containment Declaration]] providing political legitimacy, the [[Magocracy]] moved to codify its absolute authority into law.

## What Happened

The Magocracy issued the First Decree under the banner "War is inefficient. Order is paramount." Standing armies were abolished; nations could keep only city guards. The study or use of Flight Magic (Aeromancy) was punishable by "Stilling" -- lobotomy of the magical center of the brain. The [[Magister's Watch]], a global police force of mages, was established to enforce these laws.

## Consequences

The Pax Magica brought peace, but at the cost of liberty. The army ban pushed conflict underground into the [[War of Whispers]]. The magic restrictions drove engineers toward non-magical alternatives in the [[Steam Revolution]]. And the specific ban on Aeromancy -- rather than all flight -- created the legal loophole that would eventually enable the [[Second Age of Flight]].
