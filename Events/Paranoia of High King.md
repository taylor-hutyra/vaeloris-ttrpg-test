# Paranoia of High King

In [[Kharnzarak]], High King [[Thorgar VI]] grew increasingly erratic. He claimed the "surface taint" caused the catastrophic Earthwake that had shattered several major clan halls. He began a systematic purge of any Dwarf who had contact with the surface world or the [[Wandering Kin]].

## The Pure-Blood Doctrine

[[Thorgar VI]]'s paranoia solidified into ideology. The "Pure-Blood" doctrine became law — mandating that Dwarven culture must be purified of all external influence. Those who had traded with Humans, spoken with the [[Wandering Kin]], or even purchased surface-made goods were arrested, interrogated, and often executed.

## Civil War

The purges tore [[Kharnzarak]] apart. Families were divided. Clan loyalties shattered. The mountain kingdom that had once been a beacon of Dwarven unity descended into internal warfare, setting the stage for the final act: the [[Sealing of Kharnzarak]].
