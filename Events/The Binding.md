# The Binding

The **Binding** was the climactic act of the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]] — the moment when the four united First Hands overcame [[Kael'Zorai]] and sealed it away.

## The Act

Despite Kael'Zorai's formidable power and desperate resistance, the united will of [[Tham'dran]], [[Virandros]], [[Myrasael]], and [[Elaraieth]] slowly overcame the Fifth. In the Binding, they:

- Stripped Kael'Zorai of its name and divine authority
- Imprisoned it beyond the reach of mortal reality
- Erased it from memory — it became the **Nameless One**

## What Remained

Yet fundamental elements of Kael'Zorai's design remained woven into existence:

- The flow of **Time** continued according to its original establishment
- The laws of **Magic** remained accessible to mortals
- The potential for **Extremes** remained prominent in mortal nature

The Binding was a cosmic surgery — necessary, but not without devastating side effects. The sudden removal of such a fundamental being of Law and Order created a profound imbalance, unleashing the [[The Elemental Unraveling|Elemental Unraveling]].
