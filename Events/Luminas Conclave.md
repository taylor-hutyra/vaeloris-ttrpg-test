# Luminas Conclave

Realizing the national governments were too paralyzed by internal politics to act, the Archmages of the [[Grand University]], [[Gearhaven]], and the [[Federation of City-States]] met in secret.

## Background

The evidence of a growing [[Drakona]] threat was mounting — the [[Dragon's Tithe]], the [[Scaled Pact]], the [[Cloud-Shadow Incidents]] — but no government would take action. The mages decided to act on their own.

## What Happened

The Conclave formed the Coalition of Mages — a "Deep State" organization dedicated to preparing for the inevitable war. Operating outside official channels, they began stockpiling mana crystals and researching anti-aerial combat techniques.

## Consequences

The Coalition represented the first coordinated preparation for the Drakona invasion. Working in the shadows, they would recruit radical thinkers like [[Dissident of Aethelas|Elaruniel]] and develop the weapons that would eventually help the surface nations survive.
