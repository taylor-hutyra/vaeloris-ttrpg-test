# Pact of the Northern Sanctuary

A separate Mirennian expedition discovered the smaller continent of [[Aerinore]]. Finding it inhabited by peaceful [[Waldkyn]] and [[Orkin]] ([[Vorok-Varr]]), the Mirennian captains, weary of the corruption of the Old World, swore a pact of non-interference.

## Background

The same era of exploration that produced the [[Discovery of Novaterra]] led to the discovery of Aerinore, a smaller landmass to the north. The captains who found it were moved by its peaceful inhabitants.

## What Happened

Rather than claiming the land for the League, the Mirennian captains swore a pact of non-interference with the Waldkyn and Vorok-Varr inhabitants. The existence of Aerinore was kept secret.

## Consequences

Aerinore became a myth to the general public -- a "paradise" that sailors whispered about but never mapped. This secrecy preserved the continent from colonial exploitation for centuries.
