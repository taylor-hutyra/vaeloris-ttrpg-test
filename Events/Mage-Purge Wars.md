# Mage-Purge Wars

The [[Varnathi Republic]] formed the Legio Null — the Inquisitors. Clad in "Cold-Iron" armor thought to dampen magic and led by zealot-captains, they began a systematic hunt across the Republic. Villages were razed if they harbored a single "Spark-User."

## Background

The [[Decree of Prohibition]] had outlawed all magic, but enforcement required a dedicated military apparatus. The Legio Null became that instrument of terror, operating with absolute authority.

## What Happened

The Purge drove mages from the Republic in droves. Persecuted mages began fighting back with fire and lightning to defend their families, which only confirmed the Republic's propaganda that they were dangerous monsters. The cycle of violence intensified.

## Consequences

The diaspora scattered magical knowledge across the world. The [[Mirennian Exodus]] exploited refugees for profit, the [[Star-Veiled Passage]] saved thousands through an underground railroad, and [[Kingdom of the Silver Hand|General Aric the Silver]] carved out a sanctuary kingdom for the persecuted.
