# Declaration of Sovereignty

Seeing the new Varnathi state emerging from the [[Treaty of Ash and Iron]], the Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]] and the [[Federation of City-States]] formally declared the Free Peoples Alliance.

## Background

The reconstituted Varnathi Empire represented a potential threat to the independence of both Novaterra and the southern mainland. A united front was needed to maintain the balance of power.

## What Happened

The Alliance warned the Empire that any aggression would be met with overwhelming magitech force. The declaration formalized what had been an informal understanding into a binding mutual defense pact.

## Consequences

The world settled into a tri-polar deadlock: the Empire in the North, the Federation in the South, and the Free Peoples of [[Novaterra]]. This balance would hold until the [[Drakona]] shattered it from above.
