# Treaty of the Broken Peaks

## Background

Following the [[Ground War]], the [[Drakona Empire]] was defeated. Empress [[Drakona Empress Valkiss|Valkiss]] was forced to surrender to the [[Allied Command]].

## What Happened

The terms were harsh: unconditional surrender and dissolution of the Drakona Empire. The Drakona were magically bound to the earth, their ability to naturally fly stripped as part of the surrender ritual. However, the Allies, weary of death, chose not to exterminate them, instead granting the Drakona a reservation in the ruins of their greatest city.

## Consequences

The [[Coalition of Mages]], having wielded the power to pull down the sky, emerged as the only force capable of maintaining order. They began consolidating power, transitioning from a military alliance to a global government -- the [[Magocracy]]. Travel returned to the speed of horses and sailing ships as the Dark Age of the Skies began.
