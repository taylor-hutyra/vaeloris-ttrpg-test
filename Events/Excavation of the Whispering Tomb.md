# Excavation of the Whispering Tomb

A [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] archaeological team, funded by the [[Shadewalkers]], excavated a pre-Paroxysm ruin near the city of [[Argentum]]. They breached a sealed vault containing texts from the First Age "Divine Workshop."

## Background

The Shadewalkers had long recognized the value of ancient knowledge and invested in expeditions to uncover lost artifacts. This particular ruin dated to the era before the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]], when the gods still shaped the world directly.

## What Happened

The team broke into a sealed chamber containing raw arcane residue and First Age texts. The lead scholar, [[Melek the Grey]], was exposed to the residue. Rather than killing him, the exposure forcibly opened his mind to the "ambient field" — the latent magical energy left behind by the departed gods.

## Consequences

Melek became the first human to channel arcane magic in the Second Age. His exposure marked the beginning of the Arcane Revolution and would lead, six years later, to his public demonstration in [[Argentum]] that changed the world forever.
