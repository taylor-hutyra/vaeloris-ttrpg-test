# Dragon's Tithe

A single [[Drakona]] wing descended upon the remote mining town of [[Oakhaven]]. They did not burn it. The commander demanded 50% of the mine's output in gold and gems, to be left on a high peak every month.

## Background

The [[Cloud-Shadow Incidents]] had hinted at an organized aerial presence. The Dragon's Tithe confirmed it — these were not random attacks but a systematic campaign of resource extraction.

## What Happened

Oakhaven paid. The Drakona left. This pattern repeated across the remote frontiers of the surface nations. The world was forced to confront the truth: these were not mindless monsters but a disciplined empire gathering resources for a larger purpose.

## Consequences

The extortion continued unchecked because no individual settlement had the power to resist. The Drakona gained a steady flow of precious materials without risking open conflict — yet.
