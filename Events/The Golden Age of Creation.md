# The Golden Age of Creation

The **Golden Age of Creation** was the period during the First Age when [[Vaeloris]] existed in perfect collaborative harmony under the guidance of the Four First Hands and their twelve minor deity aspects.

## Perfect Harmony

Under divine guidance, Vaeloris became a world of wonder:

- Landscapes embodied perfect balance between all divine domains
- Mortal races learned directly from divine teachers
- Function and beauty intertwined naturally in all creation
- Perfect harmony existed between the various divine influences

## Cultural Flowering

- The [[Dwarves]] developed legendary techniques in stone, metal, and gem work under divine guidance
- The [[Elves]] created forest cities and mastered natural magic with direct divine instruction
- Sacred sites became centers of learning where advanced knowledge was preserved
- Trade and cultural exchange flourished between the races

## The Secret Creation of the Gnomes

In the midst of this flourishing harmony, [[Ceridara]] the Greenward and [[Illysia]] the Craftmother quietly grew the first [[Gnomes]] in [[The Hollow Glade]], hidden deep in [[The Dreaming Grove]]. The Gnomes were designed to be unseen harmonizers and stewards of the natural world.

## End of the Golden Age

The Golden Age ended as [[Kael'Zorai]]'s growing obsession with systematic control began to strain the world. Natural cycles became mechanically rigid, creative work was "improved" according to Kael'Zorai's exacting standards, and the harmony that defined this era began to fracture.
