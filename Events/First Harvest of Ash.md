# First Harvest of Ash

With the sun dimmed by the atmospheric debris of [[The Paroxysm]], agriculture faced total collapse. The human tribes of the lowlands, unable to rely on the Minor Deity [[Belthorn|Belthorn the Seasonturner]] to bless their crops, faced starvation.

## The Discovery

Survival was achieved only through the discovery of "Ash-Root," a tuber resistant to low light, by [[Waldkyn]] foragers. The Waldkyn shared this knowledge with starving human refugees on the border of the [[Whispering Vales]], marking the first significant act of inter-species cooperation in the Second Age.

## Legacy

The First Harvest of Ash established the first uneasy truce between Human and [[Waldkyn]] populations. It fundamentally shifted human agriculture toward hardy, subterranean crops and demonstrated that survival in the post-divine world would require cooperation across racial boundaries.
