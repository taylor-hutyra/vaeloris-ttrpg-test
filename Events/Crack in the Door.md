# Crack in the Door

## Background

The [[Num-Khazalun]] of [[Kharnzarak]] had been sealed in isolation since the Dwarven withdrawal centuries earlier. Observing the stability of the surface world under the Pax Magica, they reconsidered their complete isolation.

## What Happened

A single trade gate was opened. The Dwarves would sell steel and gold, but buy nothing from the surface -- fearing contamination from the Grey Rot and the corrupted magic of the surface world.

## Consequences

The influx of high-quality Dwarven steel fueled the industrial revolution in [[Gearhaven]] and the [[Federation of City-States]], providing the superior materials needed for the advanced engineering projects that would lead to the [[Second Age of Flight]].
