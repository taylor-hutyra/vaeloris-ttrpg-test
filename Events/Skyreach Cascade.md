# Skyreach Cascade

## Background

The [[Allied Command]] launched an assault on the [[Drakona Empire]]'s fortress of [[Skyreach]]. The Drakona had developed a devastating countermeasure: the "Resonance Shriek," a magical frequency designed to detonate the Mana-Drives powering the Allied ships.

## What Happened

When the Drakona unleashed the Shriek, [[Elaruniel]] -- present at the battle -- broadcast a "Null-Harmony" that cancelled the resonance and saved the fleet. However, the interaction of these massive magical forces created a localized magical dead-zone that persisted for a century.

## Consequences

The event validated Elaruniel's darker theories about the cost of high-energy magic. The knowledge that magical forces could be weaponized and countered at such scales would later inform the design of the [[Earth-Anchor Ritual]] that ended the war.
