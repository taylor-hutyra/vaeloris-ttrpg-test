# Convention of Accord

## Background

The [[Magocracy]] sensed the shifting tides: the rise of technology, the restlessness of the nations after the [[Sky-Tax Crisis]], and the subtle return of the Grey Rot. They summoned all world leaders to [[Aurumport]] to codify the status quo before it collapsed.

## What Happened

The Laws of Accord were drafted and signed. National borders were fixed in perpetuity. [[Aerinore]] was declared a Sanctuary of Nature. The Magocracy retained exclusive jurisdiction over magical crimes and education. Non-magical air travel was guaranteed as a universal right, protected by the Magocracy in exchange for cargo inspection rights.

## Consequences

The signing marked the official transition from the Second Age to the Third Age. The world was stable but rigid -- ancient grudges suppressed by law rather than resolved. The Rot festered in the dark. The "lesser" races chafed under their chains. And the Magocracy sat on a throne of lies, terrified that the Fraying they claimed to fix was about to unravel everything.
