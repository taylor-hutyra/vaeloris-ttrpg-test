# Kyn-Clan Scourge

In the [[Whispering Vales]], a radical sect of [[Waldkyn]] — the Kyn-Clans — claimed the "Dragon Shadow" was punishment for the "Single-Forms" (Humans and Elves) abandoning nature. They launched a terrorist campaign against [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] border towns.

## Background

The growing signs of the [[Drakona]] threat were interpreted differently by different cultures. The Kyn-Clans saw the dragons as a natural correction — divine punishment for the sins of civilization.

## What Happened

The Kyn-Clans struck at border settlements, farms, and supply lines. Their knowledge of the forests made them nearly impossible to catch, forcing the Empire to commit significant military resources to the northern frontier.

## Consequences

The campaign destabilized the Empire's northern border at the worst possible moment — right before the Drakona invasion. The legions moved north to deal with the Kyn-Clans, leaving the key southern ports undermanned and vulnerable.
