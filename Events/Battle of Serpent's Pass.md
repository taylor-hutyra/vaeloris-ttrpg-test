# Battle of Serpent's Pass

## Background

The aerial war had raged for over a century with neither side able to deliver a knockout blow. Mirennian Admiral [[Lyra Wind-Caller]] devised a plan to use the terrain against the [[Drakona Empire]]'s superior fleet.

## What Happened

In a masterclass of aerial tactics, Wind-Caller lured a superior Drakona fleet into a narrow mountain pass. She then detonated pre-planted magical charges on the cliffs, creating an avalanche of rock and snow that swatted the Drakona ships out of the sky.

## Consequences

The Drakona realized they were losing the tactical war. They retreated to their high-altitude Cloud Cities to regroup, leading to the [[Long Stalemate]] that would define the next century of conflict.
