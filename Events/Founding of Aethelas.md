# Founding of Aethelas

On [[Novaterra]], the [[Aen'valar]] Elves founded [[Aethelas]] — a city-sized library and observatory dedicated to the "Aen'valan" (The Unwritten Song), the pursuit of future knowledge.

## Background

The Aen'valar had long sought to understand the deeper patterns of reality. With magic now an established science and Novaterra providing a stable environment, they could finally build an institution worthy of their ambitions.

## What Happened

Aethelas became more than a library — it was a living center of research that attracted the most brilliant minds from all races. Scholars, mages, and scientists from across the world made the pilgrimage to study within its walls.

## Consequences

The brain drain was significant. The mainland lost much of its intellectual capital to Aethelas, concentrating magical and scientific expertise on Novaterra. This would prove both a strength and a vulnerability when war came.
