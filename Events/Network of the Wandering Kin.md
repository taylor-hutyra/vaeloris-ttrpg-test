# Network of the Wandering Kin

On the mainland, the exiled [[Elm-korin]] (Magic-Smiths) and the [[Thulkorun]] (Broken-Crafted) realized they needed each other to survive. They formed the [[Wandering Kin]], a loose confederation of caravans.

## Background

Both the [[Exile of the Elm-korin]] and the [[Formalization of the Thulkorun]] had cast skilled Dwarves out of [[Kharnzarak]]. Alone, each group was vulnerable. Together, they formed something greater.

## What Happened

The Thulkorun provided protection, while the Elm-korin provided goods and repairs that no human smith could match. They established the "Wagon-Vow" -- a sacred oath of mutual aid sworn between caravaneers, superseding local laws.

## Consequences

The Wandering Kin became a "State without Borders," operating across national boundaries. Their caravan network would later prove vital during the [[Earthwake of Kharnzarak]], facilitating the exodus of the Bronrun Clan to the surface.
