# Great Re-Scaffolding

## Background

After the [[Sacking of Mirenn]] destroyed the League's harbor and fleet, the Mirennians faced a choice: rebuild what was lost, or adapt to the new reality of aerial warfare.

## What Happened

Using Dwarven steel and Elven wood-singing, the Mirennians rebuilt their capital city upward. Massive docking towers and gantries rose above the ruins of the old harbor, transforming [[Mirenn]] from a sea-port into a sky-port.

## Consequences

The Mirennians applied their centuries of naval tradition to the air, becoming the premier pilots of the Alliance. Their expertise in navigation, logistics, and fleet coordination proved invaluable to the [[Allied Command]]'s air war effort.
