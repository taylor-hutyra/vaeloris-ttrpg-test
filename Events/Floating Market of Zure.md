# Floating Market of Zure

A massive chunk of land ripped up by [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]] magic became a neutral zone in the high atmosphere. Known as [[Zure]], it became the "Casablanca" of the Dragonfall War.

## The Sky Bazaar

Allied spies, [[Drakona]] defectors, [[Cloudborn Corsairs]], and [[Shadewalkers]] met on Zure to trade intelligence and goods. Allegiance meant nothing here — only coin. The market operated for nearly two centuries as the war's unofficial back channel, where enemies sat at the same tables and deals determined the course of battles.

## Zure's Grave

The market was destroyed during the Skyfall when the Earth-Anchor ritual grounded all flight magic. [[Zure]] plummeted to earth, creating a massive underwater ruin now rumored to hold the lost treasury of the [[Shadewalkers]] and priceless pre-Skyfall technology. Treasure hunters and scholars alike seek its location, but none have confirmed finding it.
