# False Dawn of Port Farrick

During the [[Blockade of Port Farrick]], the Governor of Port Farrick, feeling abandoned by the Republic, secretly petitioned the [[Mirennian League]] for annexation. In a stunning act of realpolitik, the League refused and leaked the petition to the Republic to buy goodwill.

## Background

The six-month [[Blockade of Port Farrick]] had strangled the city. With no relief from the Republic, the Governor sought any way out -- including defection to the enemy.

## What Happened

When the Republic broke the blockade, they did not liberate the city -- they purged it. The nobility was stripped of titles and wealth for their treasonous petition. The Mirennian League's refusal demonstrated their pragmatic approach: they feared a total war on two fronts more than they wanted the port.

## Consequences

The betrayal by both the Republic and the League created a deep-seated "Separatist" culture in [[Port Farrick]] that persisted into the Third Age, making the city a permanent hotbed for anti-Imperial sentiment.
