# Silent Repulsion

A [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] timber syndicate, backed by mercenaries, attempted to clear-cut the coastal forests of [[Aerinr]]. They were not met by armies.

## Background

The Varnathi hunger for resources drove them to exploit territories held by the elder races. The timber syndicate expected resistance they could overwhelm with mercenary force.

## What Happened

[[Sael'anir]] wind-shapers redirected the fog to trap the loggers in a disorienting maze, while [[Waldkyn]] druids caused rapid overgrowth that dismantled their logging camps overnight. Not a single sword was drawn.

## Consequences

The expedition went bankrupt, defeated by nature itself. The incident cemented [[Aerinr]]'s reputation as a "haunted" land that defends itself — a reputation that would deter future incursions more effectively than any army.
