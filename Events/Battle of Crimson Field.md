# Battle of Crimson Field

The defining battle of the epoch. The Grand Army of the [[Varnathi Republic]] — 50,000 steel-clad soldiers — met the Coalition of the Arcane — 3,000 Battle-Mages and [[Gearhaven]] Riflemen.

## Background

The [[Return of the Mages]] had forced a confrontation. The Republic committed its full military strength, confident that numbers would prevail against the smaller arcane force.

## What Happened

The Republican cavalry charged. The Mages of [[Gearhaven]] combined their power to cast Tectonic Rupture, creating a mile-long canyon that swallowed the cavalry whole. Arcane Cannons decimated the infantry lines from a mile away. The Republican army surrendered within hours.

## Consequences

The battle proved definitively that magic was superior to conventional arms — the "End of the Age of Steel." The humiliated Republic was forced to negotiate, leading to the [[Treaty of the Silver Quill]].
