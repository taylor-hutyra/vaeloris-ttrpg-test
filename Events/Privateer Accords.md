# Privateer Accords

## Background

Desperate for more ships during the grinding war, the [[Allied Command]] turned to an unlikely source: the [[Pirate Confederacy]] of [[Serpent's Tooth]].

## What Happened

The Allied Command offered a full pardon to the pirates. If they fitted their sea-ships with flight-cores and fought for the Alliance, they would be granted legal status as Sky-Privateers.

## Consequences

The Sky-Privateers proved devastatingly effective, using hit-and-run tactics honed over generations of piracy to wreak havoc on Drakona supply lines. This unconventional alliance added a new dimension to the aerial war.
