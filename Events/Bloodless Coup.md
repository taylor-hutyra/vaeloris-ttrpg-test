# Bloodless Coup

Emperor [[Titus II]], a decadent ruler who spent the treasury on pleasure palaces while ignoring the roads, attempted to seize the assets of the [[Silver Lords]] in [[Argentum]].

## The Alliance Against the Throne

The Governors of [[Argentum]], [[Castellum]], and [[Port Farrick]] secretly allied. They cut off silver, soldiers, and trade goods to the capital. Starved of resources and abandoned by his guard, [[Titus II]] abdicated without a battle being fought.

## The Republic

The monarchy was dissolved. Power was transferred to a Senate composed of representatives from the major city-states. The Declaration of the Republic marked the end of the [[Varnathi Empire]]'s imperial phase and the beginning of the [[Age of City-States]] — a loose federation where each city developed its own distinct identity and political culture.
