# Operation Icarus

## Background

The [[Long Stalemate]] had exhausted the world. The [[Coalition of Mages]] authorized a suicide mission to infiltrate the primary Drakona Cloud City and find a way to end the war.

## What Happened

An elite team, disguised with high-level illusions, infiltrated [[Aethelgard]], the primary Drakona Cloud City. They discovered that the cities were held aloft by a continuous, localized ritual of Air Magic -- a critical vulnerability. But they also saw Drakona nurseries, schools, and civilians.

## Consequences

The team returned with the knowledge to win the war, but also the devastating knowledge that winning would mean genocide. This moral crisis led directly to the [[Conclave of Luminas]], where the Allied leadership debated whether to use this terrible weapon.
