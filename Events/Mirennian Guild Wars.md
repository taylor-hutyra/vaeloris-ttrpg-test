# Mirennian Guild Wars

The monopoly of the [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] collapsed when the [[Azure Hand]] (a rival house) exposed evidence of embezzlement. The [[Mirennian League]] descended into fifty years of assassinations, sabotage, and street battles between private house militias.

## Background

The [[Rise of the Merchant Princes]] had concentrated wealth in the Golden Compass Syndicate. When evidence of embezzlement surfaced, the fragile trust between the houses shattered.

## What Happened

The Guild Wars consumed the League for approximately fifty years. Private militias fought in the streets, and assassinations became routine. Mirennian naval power effectively ceased to exist as a unified force.

## Consequences

The secret supply lines to [[Novaterra]] were disrupted, leaving the colonists at [[Windsail]] to fend for themselves. The chaos also produced a disgruntled [[Azure Hand]] bookkeeper who would [[The Secret is Sold|sell the Ghost Charts]] to the Republic, ending the League's monopoly on the new continent.
