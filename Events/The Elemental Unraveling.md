# The Elemental Unraveling

The **Elemental Unraveling** was the cataclysmic release of chaotic energy that followed [[The Binding]] of [[Kael'Zorai]]. While the containment saved reality, it did not leave it unscarred.

## Cause

The sudden removal of a fundamental being of Law and Order created a profound imbalance. The cosmic surgery that sealed Kael'Zorai unleashed a shockwave of chaotic energy across [[Vaeloris]].

## The Birth of the Primalkyn

Where the First Hands created the [[Waldkyn]] from unified harmony, this violent backlash gave spontaneous birth to the [[Primalkyn]]. Formed from the cosmic imbalance and raw elemental will left in Kael'Zorai's wake, these beings were the antithesis of collaborative creation. They were not made, but *unraveled* from the very fabric of Vaeloris — each a splinter of untamed nature, an echo of the fundamental forces that existed before divine guidance gave them shape.

## Legacy

The Elemental Unraveling established the foundational nature of the Primalkyn as varied, unpredictable beings of pure elementalism. Their existence is a constant reminder of the price of the Divine War.
