# Return of the Mages

[[Gearhaven]], now a superpower, decided to intervene on the mainland to stop the persecution of mages. They launched the First Fleet of Iron — steam-powered ironclad ships equipped with "Arcane Cannons."

## Background

For over two centuries, the mages who fled to [[Novaterra]] had built a technologically advanced civilization. Now they had the power to challenge the [[Varnathi Republic]] directly.

## What Happened

The Gearhaven Armada broke the blockade of Novaterra and landed troops on the mainland coast. The hidden mages who had survived the Purge rallied to their banner, creating the Coalition of the Arcane.

## Consequences

The return of the mages set the stage for the [[Battle of Crimson Field]] — the decisive confrontation that would prove once and for all that the age of conventional warfare was over.
