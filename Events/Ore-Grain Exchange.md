# Ore-Grain Exchange

A harsh winter forced the nascent city of [[Vernath]] to seek aid. [[Vardas the Elder]] sent emissaries to the lower foothills of the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]], encountering the [[Iron-Hand Clan|Iron-Hand Dwarven clan]].

## The Historic Trade

A landmark agreement was struck: Dwarven iron (needed for [[Vernath]]'s walls) in exchange for Human grain (needed for the Dwarves' deep larders). This was the first formal economic treaty between [[Humans]] and [[Dwarves]] in the Second Age.

## The Trade Road

The agreement led to the establishment of the "Trade Road" — the first paved inter-city highway of the Second Age. This road would later be expanded into the continental network during the [[Stone-Road Projects]], transforming [[Vernath]] into the trade hub of the north.
