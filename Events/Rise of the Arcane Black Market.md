# Rise of the Arcane Black Market

Despite the [[Mage-Purge Wars|Purge]], demand for magic grew. Wealthy [[Varnathi Republic|Varnathi]] senators secretly hired "Hedge-Wizards" to cure illnesses or spy on rivals, creating a massive hypocrisy at the heart of the Republic.

## Background

The [[Decree of Prohibition]] had driven magic underground but could not eliminate human desire for its benefits. The [[Wandering Kin Integration|Wandering Kin]] smuggling networks provided the infrastructure for illicit trade.

## What Happened

Magic did not disappear — it just went underground and became expensive. A thriving black market emerged where arcane services and artifacts commanded premium prices. The very senators who voted for the Purge were among the most eager customers.

## Consequences

The black market's profits became a destabilizing force. The [[Mirennian League]] fractured over whether to maintain the ban or embrace the lucrative trade openly, leading directly to the [[Mirennian Civil War]].
