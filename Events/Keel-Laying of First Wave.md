# Keel-Laying of First Wave

On the eastern coast, the ancestors of the [[Mirennians]], separated from the highlands by the treacherous [[Iron-Peak Range]], turned to the sea for survival. Without the direct aid of [[Gwynfaer|Gwynfaer the Windweaver]], they were forced to innovate.

## The Deep-Hull Ships

The first "Deep-Hull" ships were constructed, capable of withstanding the rougher, unregulated post-divine oceans. These vessels represented a fundamental leap in maritime technology — built for endurance rather than the calm, divinely-tamed seas of the First Age.

## Cultural Divergence

This event marked the beginning of the distinct divergence between "Highlander" ([[Varnathi]]) and "Coaster" ([[Mirennians|Mirennian]]) human cultures. While [[Vernath]] looked inward to stone and philosophy, the Mirennians looked outward to the horizon. This split would define the political landscape of the Second Age for millennia.
