# Aethelas Contribution

## Background

The Allied resistance was failing because they could not reach the [[Drakona Empire]]'s forces at high altitude. The [[Technocracy of Gearhaven]] had airship prototypes, but they lacked an engine powerful enough to lift heavy armor into the sky.

## What Happened

[[Elaruniel]], the controversial Elven scholar, was brought in to solve the propulsion problem. She applied her "Dissonance Theory" to the engines, creating a volatile but immensely powerful "Mana-Combustion Drive." The prototype heavy cruiser The Iron Will successfully launched from [[Gearhaven]].

## Consequences

The Mana-Combustion Drive gave the Allies the ability to contest the skies for the first time. This breakthrough enabled the massive shipbuilding program that would culminate in the [[Launch of Allied Air Fleet]], though the volatile nature of the drives would later prove a critical vulnerability during the [[Skyreach Cascade]].
