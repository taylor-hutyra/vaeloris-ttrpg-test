# Sacking of Mirenn

## Background

The [[Drakona Empire]] recognized that the [[Allied Command]]'s supply lines depended heavily on sea trade controlled by the [[Mirennian League]]. A massive aerial fleet was dispatched to strike at the heart of Mirennian power.

## What Happened

The Drakona descended on [[Mirenn]], the capital of the League. The Mirennian navy, designed entirely for surface combat, was helpless against attacks from directly above. The entire fleet was destroyed at anchor in what became known as the Burning Harbor.

## Consequences

The League ceased to exist as a naval power overnight. This catastrophic loss forced the Mirennians to pivot their entire economy toward the new "Sky-Tech," ultimately leading to the [[Great Re-Scaffolding]] of their capital city.
