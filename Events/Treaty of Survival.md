# Treaty of Survival

## Background

Following the [[Scouring of the North]], the nations of Vaeloris faced extinction at the hands of the [[Drakona Empire]]. The leaders of the major powers gathered at the neutral city of [[Luminas]], setting aside centuries of hatred.

## What Happened

The leaders of the [[Varnathi Empire]], [[Mirennian League]], [[Federation of City-States]], and [[Technocracy of Gearhaven]] signed the Treaty of Survival. This created the [[Allied Command]], a unified military structure, and merged all national treasuries into a single war fund known as the Resource Pool.

## Consequences

The treaty represented the most unprecedented cooperation in Vaeloris history. The Allied Command gave the surface nations a fighting chance against the Drakona, though it would take decades of frantic industry before they could contest air superiority.
