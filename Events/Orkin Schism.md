# Orkin Schism

Following the loss of [[Kael'Zorai|Grom-Kain]] ([[Kael'Zorai]]), the [[Orkin]] tribes fell into a bitter theological war. The [[Gral]] (Orcs) argued for a crusade to find and free their creator, while the [[Skorn]] (Goblins) argued for survival and adaptation to the new world.

## The Night of Severed Bonds

The conflict resulted in the "Night of Severed Bonds," where the two sub-species nearly wiped each other out before agreeing to a tenuous, pragmatic hierarchy: Strength ([[Gral]]) would lead in war, Cunning ([[Skorn]]) would lead in peace.

## Aftermath

The Schism codified the Varr (Clan) structure that would define Orkin society for ages. Pushed by [[Humans|Human]] and [[Dwarves|Dwarven]] expansion, the Orkin migrated toward the harsh [[Barren Hills]], beginning their long history as outsiders to the "civilized" world.
