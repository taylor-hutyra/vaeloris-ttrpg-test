# Emergence of Echo-Beasts

Strange, semi-translucent monsters began attacking travelers near the sealed [[Scars of Dissonance]]. These creatures mimicked the voices of their victims, heralding a growing power in the metaphysical undercurrents.

## Background

The [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]] had left Scars of Dissonance across the landscape. While most had been sealed, growing energy within the metaphysical realm began producing increasingly dangerous manifestations.

## What Happened

The [[Echo-Beasts]] appeared near the Scars, attacking travelers on trade roads. Semi-translucent and capable of mimicking human voices, they proved devastating against conventional forces. The [[Wardens of the Wild]] suffered heavy casualties, discovering that their weapons were ineffective against the semi-corporeal threats.

## Consequences

Panic spread across the trade roads. The emergence signaled that a far greater threat was coalescing within the Scars -- the [[Echo-Lord]] -- leading directly to the [[Gathering of Heroes]].
