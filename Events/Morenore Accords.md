# Morenore Accords

The [[Aen'valar]] Elves, observing the carnage of the [[Salt War]] from their enclave, intervened. They invited Republican and League leaders to the neutral ground of [[Morenore]], where Elven diplomats mediated a ceasefire.

## Background

The [[Siege of Solis]] and the Soldier's Plague had exhausted both sides. The [[Salt War]] had achieved nothing but devastation, and both the [[Varnathi Republic]] and [[Mirennian League]] were ready for peace.

## What Happened

Elven diplomats brokered the "Salt Truce" at Morenore. Borders were returned to pre-war status, but trade tariffs were lowered. Morenore itself was recognized as a "Sanctuary City," declared immune to future conflicts.

## Consequences

The Accords established Morenore's neutral status, which would later enable its growth into the great [[Founding of Morenore|Open City]]. The lowered tariffs facilitated trade recovery, though the underlying tensions between Republic and League remained unresolved.
