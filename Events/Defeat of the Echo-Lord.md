# Defeat of the Echo-Lord

In the final battle of Epoch I, the coalition of heroes engaged the Echo-Lord in a three-day battle. [[Bjalmund]] the Shieldbearer sacrificed his physical avatar form to trap the Echo-Lord's essence inside a shield of crystallized despair.

## Background

The [[Gathering of Heroes]] had assembled an unprecedented multi-racial fighting force. The Echo-Lord, a psychic entity born from the [[Scars of Dissonance]], represented the last great threat of the [[The Paroxysm|Paroxysm]] era.

## What Happened

The battle raged for three days at the Scar. [[Bjalmund]] sacrificed his avatar to crystallize the Echo-Lord's essence. The Scar was then sealed by the combined magic of Elven weavers and Dwarven runesmiths. The Hero's Mound was established at the battle site as a pilgrimage point.

## Consequences

With the last great Paroxysm threat defeated, the world was deemed "safe." Following Bjalmund's sacrifice, the remaining minor deities withdrew their avatars completely -- the "Final Silence" -- marking the true transition to the Age of Mortals and the beginning of Epoch II.
