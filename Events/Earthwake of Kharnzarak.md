# Earthwake of Kharnzarak

A catastrophic seismic event struck the Dwarven capital of [[Kharnzarak]]. It was not natural -- it was a structural failure of the "Deep Law" architecture caused by centuries of stagnation and the sealing of the lower levels.

## Background

The [[Dwarven Isolation]] had turned Kharnzarak inward for centuries. The sealed lower levels and lack of innovation led to gradual structural degradation that went unnoticed.

## What Happened

Several major clan halls collapsed into the abyss. The [[Bronrun Clan]] (the Growth/Reformist faction) was blamed for the disaster by the paranoid High King. Using the [[Wandering Kin]] network, thousands of Dwarves migrated to the surface, eventually seeking passage to [[Novaterra]] where they hoped to build anew.

## Consequences

The Bronrun Exodus drained Kharnzarak of its reformist element, leaving only traditionalists. The refugees would help establish the [[Rise of the Free Holds|Free Holds]] on Novaterra. The disaster deepened the [[Paranoia of High King|High King's paranoia]], ultimately leading to the [[Sealing of Kharnzarak]].
