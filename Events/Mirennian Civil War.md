# Mirennian Civil War

As the black market for magic grew, the [[Mirennian League]] fractured along economic lines. The "Old Money" — Salt and Timber merchants — wanted to uphold the ban on magic to appease the [[Varnathi Republic]]. The "New Money" — smugglers and artifact traders — wanted to embrace it openly.

## Salt vs Silk

The conflict escalated from political maneuvering to open naval warfare. The Southern Cities, hiring [[Thulkor'n]] mercenaries and battle-mages, defeated the Northern traditionalists in a series of brutal engagements.

## The Southern Shift

Victory by the New Money faction shifted the capital and moral center of the League southward. The [[Mirennian League]] became a nation that valued profit over tradition or law — a fundamental transformation in character. This moral flexibility laid the groundwork for their eventual technological dominance and their pragmatic approach to future conflicts.
