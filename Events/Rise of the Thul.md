# Rise of the Thul

In [[Kharnzarak]], the rigid caste system began to fracture. Dwarves who could not meet the perfectionist standards of their guilds turned to crime or black-market smithing, earning the name "Thul" -- the Broken.

## Background

The [[Unification of Dwarven Clans]] had established a strict caste system of High Clans, Guild Clans, and Gatestone Clans. Over generations, the perfectionist standards became increasingly difficult to meet.

## What Happened

Crime rates spiked in the deep halls as failed artisans turned to illegal smithing and underground commerce. The King's Guard responded with harsh crackdowns, overcrowding the prison-mines.

## Consequences

Growing resentment among the lower castes planted the seeds for the eventual [[Formalization of the Thulkorun|Thulkorun exile]]. The crisis demonstrated fundamental flaws in the Dwarven caste system that would haunt [[Kharnzarak]] for centuries.
