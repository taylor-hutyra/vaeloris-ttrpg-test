# Night of the Weeping Stone

## Background

A massive outbreak of [[Outbreak of the Grey Rot|Grey Rot]] struck the Dwarven surface city of [[Iron-Beach]] in [[Novaterra]]. The [[Magocracy]] proposed its standard solution: "cleansing" the city by burning it to the ground.

## What Happened

The [[Thulkorun]] Dwarves refused to abandon their city. They fought off the Mages while Gnomish medics worked to cure the outbreak using their fungal-alchemical methods -- and succeeded.

## Consequences

The event proved that the Magocracy's "burn it all" tactic was not the only way to combat the Rot. It deepened the trust between the "Wandering Kin" (Dwarves and Gnomes) and the Free Peoples, while undermining the Magocracy's claim to exclusive authority over Rot containment.
