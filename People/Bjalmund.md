# Bjalmund

**Bjalmund the Shieldbearer** is a minor deity created by [[Thamédran]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. Bjalmund is the guardian of protection and defensive strength — the divine embodiment of the principle that true power lies in sheltering others.

## Sacred Works

Bjalmund built [[Ironheart Citadel]] as the model for all defensive architecture, teaching that the greatest warriors fight to protect rather than conquer. He established networks of hidden guardians and blessed strongholds throughout [[Vaeloris]].

## Role During the Paroxysm

During the Divine War, dwarven strongholds became critical refuges for mortal races, their defenses reinforced by Bjalmund's divine stonework. The principles of defensive architecture he taught proved essential to mortal survival.

## Legacy

The location [[Bjalmund's Spire]] and the [[Frost-Forge of Bjalmund's Spire]] faction bear his name in the current age, preserving his teachings of protective strength.
