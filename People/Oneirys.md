# Oneirys

**Oneirys the Dreamwalker** is a minor deity created by [[Elaraieth]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. Oneirys is the guide through prophetic dreams and visions, and the founder of [[The Dreaming Realms]].

## Sacred Works

Oneirys established [[The Dreaming Realms]] — a safe space for the exploration of prophetic consciousness. They created the Codex of Dreams, teaching the art of vision interpretation, and taught the principle that divine guidance comes through dreams but requires mortal wisdom to interpret correctly.
