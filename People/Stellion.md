# Stellion

**Stellion the Star-Speaker** is a minor deity created by [[Elaraieth]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. He is the master of celestial wisdom and cosmic perspective, and the builder of [[The Starfall Peaks]].

## Sacred Works

Stellion built [[The Starfall Peaks]] as the ultimate astronomical observatories. He created star-maps and navigation techniques that enabled global exploration, and taught the reading of celestial signs for timing and guidance.
