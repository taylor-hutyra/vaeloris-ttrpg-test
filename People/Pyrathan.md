# Pyrathan

**Pyrathan the Flame-Bearer** is a minor deity created by [[Elaraieth]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. Pyrathan is the keeper of sacred ritual and spiritual tradition, and the kindler of [[The Sacred Hearths]].

## Sacred Works

Pyrathan kindled [[The Sacred Hearths]] — a network of eternal flames connecting communities across [[Vaeloris]]. He created the Codex of Sacred Fire, documenting proper ceremonial practice, and taught that ritual creates genuine pathways to divine wisdom. The Eternal Brazier, one of the great divine artifacts, continued kindling sacred flames long after the Great Withdrawal.
