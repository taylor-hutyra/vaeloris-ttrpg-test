# Empress Valkiss

Empress Valkiss was the ruler of the [[Drakona]] Empire at the end of the devastating conflict known as the Drakona War. After the catastrophic [[Earth-Anchor Ritual]] of SA:2688 brought the great Cloud Cities crashing to earth — killing millions of Drakona civilians — the surviving Drakona fought a desperate, hopeless ground war in the wreckage zones for over a decade.

In SA:2700, Valkiss surrendered to the [[Allied Command]] and signed the [[Treaty of the Broken Peaks]]. The terms were absolute: unconditional surrender, dissolution of the Drakona Empire, and the magical binding of the Drakona to the earth — their ability to naturally fly stripped as part of the surrender ritual. Despite having grounds for extermination, the war-weary Allied nations chose integration over genocide, granting the Drakona a reservation in the ruins of their greatest city.

Valkiss's surrender marked the end of an era. The once-unchallenged rulers of the sky became a broken, earthbound people, beginning centuries of second-class citizenship under the [[Magocracy]]. Her name is remembered with complex emotions among the Drakona — as both the leader who preserved her people from annihilation and the one who signed away their sovereignty and their sky.
