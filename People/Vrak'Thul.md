# Vrak'Thul

**Vrak'Thul** is a historical figure after whom the fortress-city of [[Vrak'Thul]] was named. The available historical record focuses on the [[Fall of Vrak'Thul|fall of the city]] rather than on this individual's personal history.

## The City of Vrak'Thul

The fortress-city of [[Vrak'Thul]] served as a haven for renegade mages and [[Drakona]] loyalists who refused to submit to the [[Magocracy]]'s authority. In **SA 2851**, when the city refused to pay the Magocracy's tithes, the [[Ruling Council]] decided to make an example. The Magocracy deployed the Arcane Nullifier -- a weapon that created an "Anti-Magic Vacuum." The city, held together by old magic, crumbled into dust instantly, and every living soul within perished.

The [[Fall of Vrak'Thul]] ensured total submission across the known world. No nation dared defy the Magocracy openly afterward, paving the way for the [[First Decree]] and the era of the Pax Magica.
