# Kyria Solanis

Kyria Solanis — the Response of Faith — is the name given to the first [[Aetheris]] to manifest in the world of [[Vaeloris]]. The term *Kyria Solanis* in [[Species/Aetheris|Celestian]] means "the Response of Faith," and while it became the collective designation for the entire Aetheris species, it originally referred to this singular, defining moment of manifestation.

In SA:2032, during a massive refugee crisis outside [[Luminas]], a bandit army attacked the helpless camp where [[Iovan]] and his followers of the nascent [[Church of the Celestial Word]] had gathered. As the refugees prayed — not for salvation, but for the strength to endure — the sky ignited. A winged figure of pure light descended, placing itself between the bandits and the refugees. It did not attack; it simply *stood*. The bandits, overcome by a wave of unnatural dread and awe, fled.

The entity dissolved after the danger passed, leaving no trace but the memory of warmth and light. This event, known as the [[Herald in Flame]], validated the Church's central doctrine: that the cosmos listens and responds to moral resonance. Conversion rates to the Church skyrocketed in the aftermath.

Whether Kyria Solanis was a singular being or simply the first expression of a collective phenomenon remains a matter of theological debate. The Aetheris themselves do not distinguish between individual and collective identity in the way mortals do — their nature is fluid, shaped by the faith that sustains them. What is certain is that this moment marked the birth of a new kind of being in Vaeloris, one born not from divine creation but from the desperate hope of mortals reaching into an empty sky.
