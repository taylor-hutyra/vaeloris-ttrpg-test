# Belthorn

**Belthorn the Seasonturner** is a minor deity created by [[Virandros]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. He is the master of agricultural cycles and sustainable cultivation, and the founder of [[The Seasonal Circles]].

## Sacred Works

Belthorn created [[The Seasonal Circles]] — sacred sites for observing and celebrating agricultural rhythms. He taught sustainable farming practices that prevent land exhaustion and established the first agricultural festivals celebrating natural cycles. His teachings ensured that mortal races could feed themselves in harmony with the land rather than at its expense.
