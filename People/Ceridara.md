# Ceridara

**Ceridara the Greenward** is a minor deity created by [[Virandros]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. She is the teacher of natural harmony and druidic wisdom, and the founder of [[The Dreaming Grove]].

## Sacred Works

Ceridara established [[The Dreaming Grove]] as the center of forest learning, where she taught the principles of working *with* rather than commanding nature. She created networks of sacred groves connecting all woodlands throughout [[Vaeloris]].

## The Secret Creation of the Gnomes

In the midst of the Golden Age, Ceridara and [[Illysia]] the Craftmother collaborated on a unique and quiet creation. Within [[The Hollow Glade]], hidden deep in the sanctuary of the Dreaming Grove, they *grew* the first [[Gnomes]]. Birthed from the deep magic of the forest floor and imbued with an artisan's soul, the Gnomes were designed to be quiet tenders of the natural world — unseen harmonizers and stewards. This act established the Gnomes' divine lineage from the domains of Natural Harmony and Refined Creation.

The [[Gnomes]] remember Ceridara as **Ceryn-Maia** — the Green Mother, one of the [[The Twin Roots|Twin Roots]].

## Role During the Paroxysm

Elven groves maintained by Ceridara's teachings served as pockets of natural stability during the Divine War, sheltering those who sought refuge from the chaos.
