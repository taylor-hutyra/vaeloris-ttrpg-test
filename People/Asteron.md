# Asteron

**Asteron the Gemheart** is a minor deity created by [[Myrasael]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. He is the master of precious materials and concentrated beauty, and the creator of [[The Crystal Gardens]].

## Sacred Works

Asteron created [[The Crystal Gardens]] — a place where perfect gems grow naturally, embodying beauty in its most concentrated form. He established the Codex of Valuation for recognizing true quality, and taught the principle that genuine wealth comes from appreciation rather than accumulation.
