# Vardris

**Vardris the Eternal** is a minor deity created by [[Tham'dran]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. Vardris is the keeper of sacred oaths and binding commitments — the divine guarantor that words given in earnest carry the weight of permanence.

## Sacred Works

Vardris created [[The Oathstone Circles]] — sacred sites where unbreakable vows could be sworn. These became the foundation for the first legal systems, built on permanent principles rather than shifting convenience. Vardris made the eternal vow to witness sacred commitments as long as mortals honor their word.

## After the Great Withdrawal

When the First Hands receded, Vardris assumed expanded responsibilities as a primary source of divine guidance for matters of law, oath, and binding commitment.
