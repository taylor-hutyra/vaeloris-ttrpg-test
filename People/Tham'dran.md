# Thamédran

**Thamédran** is the First Hand of Stability — one of the four divine beings brought forth by [[Aúrastheon]] in the Second Creation. Thamédran embodies foundational reality, permanence, and enduring structures. Of all the First Hands, Thamédran is that which anchors and maintains existence.

## Domain and Essence

Thamédran's domain encompasses the bedrock of reality: the mountains, the deep stone, the fundamental structures upon which all else is built. Where [[Virandros]] brings change and [[Myrasael]] brings refinement, Thamédran provides the immovable foundation that makes both possible.

## First Great Work

Thamédran's initial act of creation shaped the fundamental geography of [[Vaeloris]] and raised the mountain ranges that define the world's physical form.

## Mortal Creations

### The Dwarves

Through collaborative creation with [[Myrasael]], Thamédran brought forth the [[Dwarves]] — beings of methodical endurance and perfection-seeking. The Dwarves embody the combined essences of Stability and Refinement: they are masterful craftspeople who create objects of enduring beauty, mountain-dwelling and clan-oriented.

### The Waldkyn

After the Paroxysm, Thamédran joined with the other three First Hands in one final act of creation, bringing forth the [[Waldkyn]] as living demonstrations of collaborative balance.

## Minor Deity Aspects

Recognizing that mortals needed divine teachers who could operate on personal levels, Thamédran created three aspects from their own essence:

- **[[Korthak]]** the Stonefather — master of stonework and divine craftsmanship
- **[[Vardris]]** the Eternal — keeper of sacred oaths and binding commitments
- **[[Bjalmund]]** the Shieldbearer — guardian of protection and defensive strength

## Cultural Names

Each mortal culture remembers Thamédran through their own lens:

- **Tharnûm** (Stone) — the Dwarven Pillar of endurance and structure
- **N'rthelion** (Stone resonance) — the Elven Harmony of endurance
- **The Mason** — the Human Maker who raised the mountains
- **Thamédran** (Earth/Stability) — the Waldkyn Voice, true name preserved

## The Great Withdrawal

After the Paroxysm and the creation of the Waldkyn, Thamédran chose to recede into metaphysical realms — withdrawing into foundational reality itself. Thamédran's presence is felt in enduring structures, stability, and permanence. The legacy: the foundation upon which all else is built.
