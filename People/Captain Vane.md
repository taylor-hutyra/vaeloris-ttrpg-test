# Captain Vane

**Captain Vane** was a renegade [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] admiral who transformed piracy from a scattered nuisance into a geopolitical power during the Age of Expansion. His founding of the [[Pirate Confederacy]] at [[Serpent's Tooth]] in **SA 1020** marked the birth of the "Freebooter Nation."

## The Scourge of Serpent's Tooth

As the [[Mirennian League]] and [[Varnathi Republic]] grew richer through trade and colonial expansion, the seas became rich hunting grounds. Captain Vane, drawing on his experience as a former Mirennian admiral, united the disparate pirate crews of the southern archipelago under a single banner. He established the "Code of the Coast" -- a set of laws governing pirate conduct -- and fortified the island of [[Serpent's Tooth]] as an impregnable base of operations. Both Varnathi and Mirennian navies were forced to convoy their merchant ships, driving up prices and slowing trade across the known world.

## The Blockade of Port Farrick

By **SA 1070**, Vane's fleet had grown bold enough to blockade [[Port Farrick]], the Republic's primary port. For six months, no ship entered or left the harbor. The blockade exposed the vulnerability of the Republic's maritime defenses and triggered a political crisis -- the Governor of Port Farrick secretly petitioned the [[Mirennian League]] for annexation, an act of desperation that would have lasting consequences for the city's identity.

## Death and Legend

The blockade was broken only when the [[Shadewalkers]] assassinated Vane, fracturing the pirate coalition back into warring crews. His death led directly to the establishment of the Republic Naval Guard, a dedicated pirate-hunting fleet. Yet Vane's legend endured far longer than his life -- his exploits inspired a "Golden Age" of romanticized piracy that shaped maritime culture for generations.
