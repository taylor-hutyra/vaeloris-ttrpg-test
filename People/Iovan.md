# Iovan

**Iovan** was a [[Human]] former soldier turned pacifist who founded the [[Church of the Celestial Word]] in the neutral city of [[Luminas]] around **SA 2020**, during the devastating Succession Wars that followed the fall of the Varnathi Republic.

## The Founding of the Church

After forty years of slaughter during the Bleeding Years (SA 1960-2000), the secular promises of previous epochs -- that mortals did not need gods -- rang hollow. Amid this spiritual vacuum, Iovan began preaching in [[Luminas]]. He did not call for the return of the old gods, but instead proclaimed the doctrine of the "Celestial Word" -- the idea that the universe responds to moral resonance. The Church he founded offered practical aid alongside spiritual guidance: soup kitchens, healing, and hope, filling the void left by the collapsed state.

## The Herald in Flame

In **SA 2032**, during a massive refugee crisis outside [[Luminas]], a bandit army attacked a helpless refugee camp. As Iovan and the refugees prayed -- not for salvation, but for the strength to endure -- the sky ignited. A winged figure of pure light descended, placing itself between the bandits and the refugees. It did not attack; it simply stood. The bandits, overcome by a wave of unnatural dread and awe, fled. The entity dissolved after the danger passed.

This was the first confirmed sighting of an [[Aetheris]], and it validated the Church's core doctrine: that the cosmos listens and responds to moral resonance. Conversion rates skyrocketed in the aftermath, and the Church of the Celestial Word grew from a local movement into one of the most powerful spiritual institutions in Vaeloris.
