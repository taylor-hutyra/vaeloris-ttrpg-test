# Gruk the Breaker

**Gruk the Breaker** was an [[Orkin]] gladiator enslaved in the eastern provinces of the [[Varnathi Republic]] who discovered he was a "Spark-User" -- an Earth-Mage capable of infusing his skin with stone. His revolt in **SA 1580** shattered the chains of thousands and founded the [[Free Marches]], the first nation built on anti-slavery principles.

## The Stone-Fist Revolt

In the Republic's eastern provinces, slave labor -- mostly captured [[Orkin]] and indebted humans -- fueled the economy. Gruk did not use conventional spells; he infused his body with the strength of stone itself. When he broke free, he shattered the walls of the slave pens with his bare hands. Thousands of Orkin and human slaves rallied to him, and together they seized a swath of territory that became known as the [[Free Marches]].

## The Siege of the Free Marches

In **SA 1610**, the Republic sent three legions to crush the revolt. However, the [[Shadewalkers]], seeing profit in continued chaos, supplied the rebels with stolen Mirennian "Fire-Pots" (early grenades). Gruk's forces fought a decade-long guerrilla war that bogged down the Republican legions and drained the Republic's military strength. The myth of Varnathi invincibility was shattered.

## Death and Legacy

Gruk was assassinated by a Republican agent around **SA 1650**. His lieutenants established a governing Council and formally declared the **Republic of the Free Marches**. Though the nation was short-lived, it was the first polity founded on anti-slavery principles, inspiring slave revolts across the continent and forcing the Varnathi Republic to keep its armies at home -- halting their colonial expansion on [[Novaterra]].
