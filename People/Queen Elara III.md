# Queen Elara III

Queen Elara III is the hereditary ruler of [[Hannanórë]], seat of the [[Cirhannim]] — the most traditionalist and isolationist of the [[Elves|Elven]] peoples. Her bloodline is said to be the most direct echo of the [[Evendarië]], the Archetypal Parents, and she holds the title of Monarch of the Verdant Throne. She acts as the living symbol of Hannanórë's unchanging grace, embodying the Cirhannim belief that the world-song must be preserved exactly as it was first played.

Despite her immense ceremonial power, the true governing authority in Hannanórë rests with the [[Talanvarë Council]], composed of the eldest and most powerful mystics from the three great houses. The Council can veto any royal decree by declaring it a *Song-Wrong* — an act of profound disharmony. This tension between symbolic authority and practical power defines Elara's reign.

The political landscape beneath her is shaped by the competing interests of [[House Valerion]], the isolationist power dominating the Council; [[House Silvanus]], the reformists who secretly maintain contact with [[Mórenórë]]; and [[House Lyrandor]], the pragmatic warrior house loyal to the throne. Elara must navigate these factions while maintaining the appearance of an eternal, unchanging harmony — even as the [[Blight]] and outside pressures test Hannanórë's isolation.
