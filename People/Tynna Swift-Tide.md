# Tynna Swift-Tide

**Tynna Swift-Tide** was a [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] navigator whose voyage of discovery in **SA 752** changed the course of history. Seeking a route around the [[Varnathi Republic|Republican]] blockades that strangled Mirennian trade, she sailed further east than any explorer before her and discovered [[Novaterra]] -- a massive, untamed continent.

## The Discovery of Novaterra

During the Age of Rivalry, the [[Varnathi Republic]] controlled much of the overland trade, and tensions between the Republic and the [[Mirennian League]] frequently erupted into blockades and economic warfare. Tynna Swift-Tide was dispatched to find an alternative maritime route that could bypass Republican interference. Her voyage took her beyond all known charts and into uncharted waters, where she made landfall on what would prove to be one of the most consequential discoveries in mortal history.

## Legacy

The [[Mirennian League]] declared the existence of "Novaterra" a level-zero state secret. A "Ghost Fleet" -- ships officially listed as lost at sea -- was established to ferry supplies to the new continent in secrecy. The discovery would eventually reshape the geopolitical landscape of the entire world, turning Novaterra into a proxy battlefield between the League and the Republic, a refuge for persecuted mages, and ultimately the birthplace of the [[Technocracy of Gearhaven]]. Tynna Swift-Tide's name endures as the explorer who opened the door to a new world.
