# Admiral Lyra Wind-Caller

Admiral Lyra Wind-Caller was a [[Mirennians|Mirennian]] naval commander who served in the [[Allied Command]] during the [[Drakona]] War. She is best remembered for her masterful victory at the [[Battle of Serpent's Pass]] in SA:2550 — widely regarded as the turning point of the entire conflict.

The Mirennians, whose ancient fleet had been destroyed at anchor during the Sacking of Mirenn in SA:2355, had rebuilt themselves as the premier pilots of the Alliance, applying centuries of seafaring tradition to the new domain of aerial warfare. Lyra Wind-Caller exemplified this transformation. Her understanding of terrain, weather, and the psychology of her opponents was unmatched.

At Serpent's Pass, she faced a superior Drakona fleet and chose not to meet it in open battle. Instead, she lured the enemy formation into a narrow mountain pass where their numerical advantage was negated. Once the Drakona were committed, she detonated pre-planted magical charges on the cliffs above, creating a devastating avalanche of rock and snow that swatted the Drakona ships out of the sky. The victory forced the Drakona to retreat to their high-altitude Cloud Cities to regroup, and they never regained the tactical initiative. Wind-Caller's name became synonymous with brilliance under pressure and the ruthless pragmatism that ultimately won the war.
