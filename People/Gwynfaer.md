# Gwynfaer

**Gwynfaer the Windweaver** is a minor deity created by [[Virandros]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. She is the mistress of weather, travel, and atmospheric currents, and the creator of [[The Wind Paths]].

## Sacred Works

Gwynfaer established [[The Wind Paths]] — reliable atmospheric currents enabling global travel. She created stable weather patterns that allowed predictable agriculture and commerce, and notably taught dragons the mastery of flight and aerial territories. The magical tradition of [[Wind-Shaping]] descends from her teachings.
