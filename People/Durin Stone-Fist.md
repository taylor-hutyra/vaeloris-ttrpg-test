# Durin Stone-Fist

**Lord Durin Stone-Fist** is a ranking member of the [[Barazkor Clan]] (the Iron-Gavels) -- the Royal Clan of [[Kharnzarak]] -- and the most trusted enforcer of [[High King Thorgar VII]]. He is a fiercely traditionalist and militant figure who views any deviation from the Stone-Lays as an existential threat to Dwarven civilization.

## Role in Kharnzarak

As the king's enforcer, Durin Stone-Fist serves as the iron hand of the Barazkor Clan's authority within the sealed kingdom. The Barazkor are the wardens of Kharnzarak's military and its laws, and Durin embodies their militant philosophy. He is a staunch defender of the rigid caste system established by [[Thane Korin Barazkor]] millennia ago and actively suppresses any movement toward reform or contact with the outside world.

## Rivalry with Brenna

Durin's primary rival is [[Brenna]], whose reformist views and sympathies toward the exiled [[Bronrûn Legacy|Bronrûn]] philosophy represent everything he opposes. Where Durin sees strength in tradition and isolation, Brenna sees stagnation and eventual doom. Their conflict reflects the deeper ideological schism within Kharnzarak: the choice between preserving the old ways that have kept the Dwarves safe for centuries, or embracing change before the mountain becomes a tomb.

**Note:** Durin Stone-Fist is a distinct character from [[Gruk the Breaker]], the Orkin gladiator whose "Stone-Fist Revolt" (SA 1580) was named for his Earth-Magic fighting style. The shared epithet is coincidental.
