# Illysia

**Illysia the Craftmother** is a minor deity created by [[Myrasael]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. She is the patron of artisans and refined creation, and the founder of [[The Divine Workshop]].

## Sacred Works

Illysia founded [[The Divine Workshop]] where all races learned divine craftsmanship. She created the [[Artisan's Codex]], establishing the principles of perfect craft, and taught the marriage of function and beauty in all creative work.

## The Secret Creation of the Gnomes

Together with [[Ceridara]] the Greenward, Illysia collaborated on the secret creation of the [[Gnomes]] in [[The Hollow Glade]]. While Ceridara provided the deep magic of the forest floor, Illysia imbued the Gnomes with an artisan's soul — their meticulous craftsmanship and eye for intricate design.

The [[Gnomes]] remember Illysia as **Ilys-Tekne** — the Bright Artisan, one of the [[The Twin Roots|Twin Roots]].
