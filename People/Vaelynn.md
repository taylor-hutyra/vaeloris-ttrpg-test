# Vaelynn

**Vaelynn the Songkeeper** is a minor deity created by [[Myrasael]] during the expanded phase of the Second Creation. She is the muse of music, poetry, and harmonic expression, and the founder of [[The Resonance Halls]].

## Sacred Works

Vaelynn established [[The Resonance Halls]] — spaces with perfect acoustic properties where sound achieves its purest form. She created the Great Symphony, a work incorporating all cultural musical traditions, and taught that music can heal, unite, and inspire across cultural divides. The Singer's Harp was preserved in the Resonance Halls for true musicians.
