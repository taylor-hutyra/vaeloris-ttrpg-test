# Captain Kaelen Vance

Captain Kaelen "Skysplitter" Vance was a [[Mirennians|Mirennian]] ace pilot who served in the [[Allied Command]]'s air fleet during the [[Drakona]] War. Renowned for his skill in aerial combat, he became increasingly disillusioned with the Alliance's high command and their willingness to sacrifice entire crews in what he saw as pointless engagements.

In SA:2480, Vance led a mutiny, taking his squadron and vanishing into the high cloud banks that blanketed the war zone. There, he founded the [[Cloudborn Corsairs]] — a third faction in the war that owed allegiance to neither the Alliance nor the Drakona Empire. The Corsairs raided both sides for supplies, establishing hidden bases on floating sky-islands — chunks of land ripped from the earth by raw magic during the Paroxysm.

The Corsairs became a wild card in the conflict, their unpredictable raids disrupting carefully planned operations on both sides. Vance's intimate knowledge of Allied tactics and Mirennian airship design made his force disproportionately dangerous for its size. His reputation as the "Skysplitter" — earned for his daring maneuvers through storm fronts and narrow mountain passes — became legendary among both the crews who feared him and the deserters who sought to join him.
