# Arcane Conduits

Arcane Conduits -- also known as Focusing Crystals -- are precisely shaped crystalline components that channel, regulate, and direct magical energy. They are the essential interface between raw arcane power and the mechanical systems of [[Technomancy]], enabling non-magical operators to safely use mana-powered devices. Without Arcane Conduits, the fusion of magic and machinery that defines the Third Age would be impossible.

## Principles

Raw magical energy is volatile and unpredictable. Left unchanneled, it surges, dissipates, and interferes with nearby systems in chaotic ways. Arcane Conduits solve this problem through crystalline structure: the regular atomic lattice of certain minerals naturally guides mana flow along predictable paths, much as a copper wire guides electrical current.

The key properties of a conduit crystal are:

- **Resonance**: The crystal vibrates at a frequency compatible with the mana field, drawing energy into its structure
- **Coherence**: Internal lattice geometry straightens chaotic mana flow into a uniform, directional stream
- **Regulation**: Impurities and deliberate structural inclusions act as resistance, controlling the rate of flow
- **Stability**: High-quality conduits can sustain flow indefinitely without degrading

## The Crystal-Singers

The finest Arcane Conduits are produced by the [[Crystal-Singers of M'ren'r']] -- an order of Elven artisans based in the cosmopolitan city of [[M'ren'r']]. Drawing on the Elven tradition of harmonic magic, Crystal-Singers shape raw crystals through sustained vocal resonance, literally singing the crystal into its optimal form. The process can take weeks for a single conduit, as each note adjusts the crystal's internal lattice at a molecular level.

Crystal-Singer conduits are prized for their exceptional coherence and longevity. A Singer-crafted conduit produces less waste energy, generates less heat, and maintains its properties for centuries longer than mechanically cut alternatives. They are correspondingly expensive, and the Crystal-Singers guard their techniques as closely as the [[Cirhannim]] guard the secrets of the [[The Great Weaving|Great Weaving]].

## Applications

Arcane Conduits are found in virtually every technomantic system:

- **Mana-Pylon networks**: Conduit-lined channels carry energy between pylons
- **Clank Servitors**: Conduit cores provide the arcane motive force for autonomous constructs
- **Arcane weaponry**: Cannons, wands, and focusing rods all rely on conduits to shape destructive energy
- **Communication**: Long-range messaging systems use conduit relays to transmit encoded mana pulses
- **Medicine**: Healing devices channel restorative energy through precision-cut conduits

## Manufacturing

Mass-produced conduits are cut mechanically in [[Gearhaven]] and [[Federation of City-States|Federation]] workshops, using diamond-tipped tools and standardized templates. These "Common Conduits" are adequate for most purposes but lack the precision and longevity of Singer-crafted versions. The quality gap has created a tiered market: common conduits for everyday devices, Singer-crafted conduits for critical infrastructure and military applications.

## Strategic Importance

Control of conduit production is a matter of national security. The [[Magocracy]] maintains strict export controls on raw conduit-grade crystals. [[Gearhaven]] has invested heavily in synthetic crystal research to reduce dependence on natural deposits. And the Crystal-Singers of M'ren'r', as the premier artisans in the field, enjoy a level of diplomatic immunity that few other civilian groups can claim.
