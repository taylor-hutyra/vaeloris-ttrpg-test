# Hollowkin Settlement

The **Hollowkin Settlement** is a unique joint [[Halflings|Halfling]]-[[Gnomes|Gnome]] community on the peaceful continent of [[Aerin'r']], established when small family groups of both peoples settled together in the fertile forested lowlands. It is a living testament to the historic **[[Declaration of Hollowkin Rights]]** -- the treaty that first established a shared society of "Burrows and Hollows" between the two peoples.

## Government

The settlement is governed by the **[[Joint-Hollow Council]]**, a consensus-based body where [[Halflings|Halfling]] family elders and [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] Myco-Whisperers meet to decide matters affecting both peoples. There are no votes. Decisions are reached by finding a "sensible arrangement" -- a solution that ensures both the Halfling pantries remain full and the Gnomish patterns remain undisturbed.

This governance model baffles outsiders accustomed to hierarchies and formal law. In practice, it works because both peoples share a fundamental commitment to community well-being, practical problem-solving, and a deep suspicion of anyone who seeks power for its own sake.

## Society

The Hollowkin Settlement blends the best qualities of both peoples:

- **[[Halflings]]** bring their agricultural expertise, community spirit, and culinary arts. Their [[Hearthfolk]] traditions ensure well-stocked larders, lively taverns, and the kind of warm hospitality that makes even strangers feel like family.
- **[[Gnomes]]** bring their affinity for the natural world's hidden patterns, their skill with mushroom cultivation and herbal remedies, and their quiet, intricate craftsmanship. The Gnomish Myco-Whisperers maintain the health of the surrounding forest's mycelial network.

The settlement's architecture reflects this partnership: cozy Halfling burrows with round doors and flower boxes sit alongside and above Gnomish hollows -- subterranean workshops and mushroom gardens woven into the root systems of ancient trees.

## History

The [[Halflings|Halfling]] and [[Gnomes|Gnome]] families who settled on [[Aerin'r']] came seeking the same thing: a quiet place far from the "Big Scramble" of the mainland. They found natural allies in each other and in the continent's other peaceful inhabitants -- the [[Sael'anir]] Elves, the [[Waldkyn]] of the [[Aerin're Kith]], and the [[Orkin]] of [[Vorok-Varr]].

The community participates in the **Star-Tide Festival** (first held SA:1890 at [[Lanthir Haven]]), contributing generous feasts and Gnomish light-displays to the celebrations.

## Character

The Hollowkin Settlement is everything the wider world is not: unhurried, well-fed, and content. The loudest sound on a typical day is the sizzle of something frying in a kitchen or the gentle tapping of a Gnome's tools. Arguments are settled over tea. Ambition extends no further than growing a larger pumpkin than last year or perfecting a new mushroom soup recipe. It is, by any reasonable measure, the happiest place in [[Vaeloris]].
