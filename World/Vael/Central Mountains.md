# Central Mountains

The Central Mountains are the mineral-rich spine of [[Vael]], home to the greatest concentration of underground wealth on the continent. Beneath their peaks lie the sealed Dwarven kingdom of [[Kharnzarak]], the silver metropolis of [[Argentum]], and the silent Rock Gnome city of [[Lithos]].

## Geography

The range dominates the central interior of Vael, with peaks reaching into the permanent snowline and root systems extending deep into geothermal zones. The mountains are honeycombed with natural caverns, Dwarven-carved halls, and Gnomish tunnels. Veins of silver, iron, gold, and the rare "Star-Metal" (meteorite iron) run through the stone.

## History

The Dwarven clans were the first to claim the Central Mountains, beginning the brutal reclamation of their ancestral halls from [[Skitter-Gloom]] infestations in SA:12. By SA:91, Thane [[Korin Barazkor]] had unified the clans under the first High King, founding [[Kharnzarak]] and establishing the rigid caste system of High Clans, Guild Clans, and Gatestone Clans.

Human interest in the range grew with the discovery of massive silver deposits in SA:230. The [[Treaty of Coin]] between Vernath and Kharnzarak established mining rights and standardized the "Vernathi Silver Mark" as currency. When the Deep-Vein was discovered in SA:485, the mining camp of [[Argentum]] exploded into a metropolis that would eventually rival [[Vernath]] in political power.

The catastrophic Earthwake of SA:1200 shattered major clan halls within Kharnzarak, triggering the Bronr'n Exodus. The paranoid [[High King Thorgar VI]] sealed the Great Adamant Gates in SA:1400, cutting the Dwarven kingdom off from the surface world for centuries. The Rock Gnomes of [[Lithos]] remained hidden throughout, their silent clockwork city unknown to most surface-dwellers.

## Present Day

The Central Mountains remain politically fractured. [[Kharnzarak]] maintains limited surface trade through a single gate opened circa SA:3050, while [[Argentum]] functions as a human industrial powerhouse. [[Lithos]] operates in near-total secrecy, its existence known only to a handful of outsiders.
