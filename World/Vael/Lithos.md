# Lithos

Lithos, known as the Silent Gyre, is the greatest city of the [[Rock Gnomes]] -- a breathtaking subterranean metropolis of silent clockwork, glowing crystals, and geothermal vents, hidden deep within the [[Central Mountains]]. Its existence is virtually unknown to surface-dwellers.

## Geography

Lithos occupies a vast natural cavern system within the Dragon's Tooth range, lit by phosphorescent fungi and powered by volcanic vents. The city is a marvel of silent engineering: gears turn without noise, crystal arrays channel light through precise angles, and geothermal energy drives intricate clockwork mechanisms. The architecture is both organic and precisely mathematical, carved from living stone with a jeweler's precision.

## Government

Lithos is governed by the [[Guild-Conclave]], a council of nine members where each is the undisputed master of their respective craft: the Master Gem-Facer, the Prime Harmonist, the Chief Chronometrist, and others. A seat is held for life or until a peer demonstrably surpasses the master's skill.

The [[Deep Warden]] leads the Conclave. Their role is not to rule but to "listen" to the great machine of the world -- the grinding of tectonic plates, the hum of leylines -- and direct the Conclave's efforts to maintain stability.

## Society

Lithos is rigidly structured around guilds. An individual's worth is measured by the precision and quiet perfection of their work. There is little room for sentiment; a gear that does not turn smoothly is a flaw to be corrected.

Two philosophical schools compete for influence:
- The **Clockwork School** (orthodox): focused on pure, silent mechanics. They see the universe as a vast, intricate timepiece and respect the [[N'm-Khazal'n]] Dwarves for their mastery of structure, though they find Dwarven methods "loud and imprecise."
- The **Resonance School** (heretical): studies the harmonic properties of crystals and metals, believing true perfection lies in perfectly tuned resonances that can influence magic and reality. The orthodox consider this dangerously close to the "chaotic" magic of the Loud-Walkers.

## Relationship with Other Races

The [[N'm-Khazal'n]] Dwarves of [[Kharnzarak]] know the Rock Gnomes as the [[N'm-kith]] ("Stone-kin"), treating them with quiet, almost invisible respect. It was Rock Gnomes who secretly aided the Elves in stabilizing the wards around [[Hannan'r']] in SA:89. This pattern of hidden, essential contribution defines Lithos's relationship with the wider world: always present, rarely seen, indispensable.
