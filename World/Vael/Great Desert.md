# Great Desert

The Great Desert, also known as the Southern Sands, is a vast arid expanse in the south of [[Vael]]. Home to the [[Lothari]] mystics and the [[Desertborn Confederacy]], the desert controls the overland trade routes between the eastern and western halves of the continent.

## Geography

The Great Desert is a landscape of shifting dunes, rocky plateaus, and hidden oases connected by secret routes known only to the Lothari. Beneath the sands lie First Age ruins, including the red-stone ziggurats of the fallen [[Vermillion Dynasties]]. Deep aquifers sustain pockets of life, tended by the reclusive [[Sand Gnomes]] who inhabit the desert's most remote corners.

## History

The desert's first major political power was the Vermillion Dynasties (SA:955-1150), a line of Sorcerer-Kings who united the scattered tribes using primitive "Blood-Binding" rituals found in First Age ruins. They severed overland trade routes, directly accelerating the Mirennian discovery of Novaterra. The dynasties collapsed in SA:1150 from magical drought and inbreeding, leaving behind ruins and a fierce culture of independence.

In SA:1300, the [[Lothari]] Mystics united the nomadic tribes into the [[Desertborn Confederacy]], seizing control of the overland Silk Road. The "Toll of Sand" ensured that any trade moving between east and west by land paid tribute to the Desertborn, transforming the Confederacy into a wealthy, neutral buffer state.

## The Silent Network

The Lothari formally organized the [[Silent Network]] in SA:1350, driven by the belief that the "Age of Noise" (Expansion) would eventually collapse and the world would need its memory. They began archiving histories, artifacts, and myths in hidden desert vaults, preserving knowledge of the First Age and the Paroxysm that would otherwise have been lost to secular revisionism. The Network remains the world's premier repository of ancient knowledge.

## Culture

The Lothari are semi-nomadic, practicing ancient rituals and dream-interpretation. Their origin myth speaks of "The Breathless Dream" -- a sleeping god whose thoughts became reality. They are led by the [[Silent Network]], a council of respected mystics who guide their people by interpreting dreams and celestial patterns. Common sayings include "Wind writes where we forget" and "Even silence has a pattern."
