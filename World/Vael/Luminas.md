# Luminas

Luminas is a major highland city in the [[Varnathi Highlands]], renowned as a site of diplomacy, religious significance, and political neutrality. It is the birthplace of the [[Church of the Celestial Word]] and has hosted some of the most consequential conclaves in Vaelorian history.

## History

Originally one of the highland city-states incorporated into the Varnathi Empire under [[Caelius I]] in SA:381, Luminas distinguished itself not through military power but through its reputation as neutral ground. Its position made it the natural site for critical negotiations.

During the devastating Bleeding Years (SA:1960-2020), the former soldier [[Iovan]] began preaching in Luminas. He called not for the return of old gods but for the "Celestial Word" -- the idea that the universe responds to moral resonance. The [[Church of the Celestial Word]] offered soup kitchens, healing, and hope, filling the void left by the state. The first [[Aetheris]] sighting occurred outside Luminas in SA:2032, when a winged figure of pure light descended to protect a refugee camp from bandits, validating the Church's doctrine.

## The Orkin Quarter

Under Emperor [[Aetius IV]]'s Labor Integration Act of SA:2130, Orkin were granted "Subject Status" and allowed to live in cities -- though confined to specific districts. Luminas's [[Orkin Quarter]] became a microcosm of Orkin urban life: a tight-knit community governed by a [[Neighborhood Council]] of clan elders, with the [[Butchers' Guild]] and [[Masons' Guild]] serving as the true economic power brokers. While legally citizens, the Orkin face systemic prejudice and have formed mafia-like guilds for protection.

## Diplomatic Significance

Luminas has hosted the [[Luminas Conclave]] of SA:2305, where Archmages secretly formed the [[Coalition of Mages]]; the [[Treaty of Survival]] of SA:2345, which created the [[Allied Command]] against the Drakona; and the fateful Conclave of SA:2685, where Allied leadership debated and ultimately authorized the Earth-Anchor Ritual that brought down the Drakona Cloud Cities.

## Present Day

Luminas remains a center of religious authority and diplomatic neutrality. The Church of the Celestial Word operates from here, its influence growing as faith offers answers that secular politics cannot. The Orkin Quarter simmers with tension as the promise of integration remains unfulfilled.
