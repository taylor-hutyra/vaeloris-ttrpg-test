# Castellum

Castellum is the southern fortress-city of the [[Varnathi Highlands]], the military heart of the Varnathi people. Founded as frontier outposts during the Southern Push of SA:315, it evolved into a culturally distinct city-state defined by martial discipline, strategic pragmatism, and an iron-fisted approach to governance.

## Founding

The Varnathi expansion into the southern lowlands in SA:315 was a war against the land itself, filled with [[Primalkyn]] anomalies. The military outposts established to hold the frontier eventually coalesced into Castellum -- a fortress that became a city. Its character was fundamentally shaped by the March of Order in SA:410, when Emperor [[Caelius I]] used the new steel legions to shatter disorganized Orkin warbands and establish the southern frontier.

## History

Castellum developed a culture distinct from the philosophical capital of [[Vernath]]. Where Vernath debated, Castellum acted. When the Republic formed in SA:581, Castellum emerged as the city-state of "Military and Discipline -- the Iron Fist." By SA:600, the city's identity was inseparable from its military purpose.

Consul [[Marius the Iron-Hand]] (SA:820) exploited the threat of Orkin raids to garrison Castellum troops in other cities, effectively turning the Republic into a military hegemony. When the Republic shattered in SA:1950, Castellum declared independence immediately. It was Warlord [[Aetius IV]] of Castellum who ultimately conquered Vernath in SA:2085 and forged the current [[Varnathi Imperial Democracy]] -- a hybrid of hereditary Emperor (controlled by the military) and elected Senate (controlled by merchants).

## Culture

Castellum's culture is defined by duty, obedience, and physical prowess. The city's architecture is utilitarian: thick walls, wide parade grounds, orderly barracks, and forges. Citizens are expected to serve in the military or contribute directly to its supply chain. The [[Crimson Guard]] elite corps traces its origins to Castellum's founding legions.

## Present Day

Castellum remains the power base of the [[Consul of Blades]] and the [[Crimson Banner]] faction. [[General Valerius]]'s popularity is strongest here, where his calls for military expansion resonate with a population that has known nothing but the frontier for three millennia.
