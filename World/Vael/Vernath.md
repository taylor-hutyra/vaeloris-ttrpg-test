# Vernath

Vernath is the ancient capital of the [[Varnathi]] people, founded in SA:28 by the philosopher-warrior [[Vardas the Elder]] on a windswept high plateau in the [[Varnathi Highlands]]. Built upon the ruins of a First Age fortification, it is the political, philosophical, and legal heart of human civilization on [[Vael]].

## Founding

Vardas the Elder united seven warring highland tribes by preaching that the gods had left because mortals were ready to stand on their own. He laid the foundation stone of Vernath and established the [[First Compact]] -- "Stone breaks louder when ignored" -- a constitution mandating that all citizens contribute to defense and maintenance. Idleness was classified as a crime against the state.

## History

Vernath grew from a frontier city-state into the seat of an empire. The Ore-Grain Exchange of SA:62 with the [[Iron-Hand]] Dwarves established the first paved inter-city highway, and the Stone-Road Projects of SA:110 transformed Vernath into the trade hub of the north. [[Caelius I]] declared the Varnathi Empire from its walls in SA:381.

The city endured the Bloodless Coup of SA:581 (ending the monarchy), the systemic corruption of the [[Shadewalkers]] (SA:880), the Succession Wars (SA:1950), and conquest by [[Aetius IV]] (SA:2085). Through each upheaval, the city's identity as a center of philosophy and law persisted.

## Government

Vernath is currently governed by the [[Varnathi Pentarchy]] -- five co-equal Consuls representing the pillars of society: the [[Consul of Pillars]] (aristocracy), [[Consul of Coin]] (merchants), [[Consul of Scrolls]] (diplomacy), [[Consul of the People]] (citizenry), and [[Consul of Blades]] (military). The system is in near-paralysis, deadlocked between the hawkish [[Crimson Banner]], the traditionalist [[Marble Hand]], and the reformist [[Open Palm Movement]].

## Culture

Vernath's culture is scholarly and aristocratic, with an elite class of philosopher-guardians. The city's architecture reflects its values: imposing stone walls, orderly streets, and public forums designed for debate. Its citizens speak in formal, archaic patterns and measure worth by duty to the state.

## Present Day (412 T.A.)

[[General Valerius]], immensely popular after celebrated campaigns, pushes for increased military funding and aggressive border security. He is consistently blocked by the [[Marble Hand]], who fear provoking the [[Magocracy]]. The political deadlock paralyzes the Pentarchy while external threats mount.
