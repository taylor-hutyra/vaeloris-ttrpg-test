# Grand University

The Grand University is the premier institution of magical and academic learning in [[Vaeloris]], located in the neutral city of [[Mórenórë]]. It was originally founded in SA:1750 through a collaboration between the Magister's Council and the [[Aen'valar]] Elves, with the explicit goal of standardizing magical education and preventing a repeat of the catastrophic "Wild Years" that followed the first discovery of mortal magic.

Under its original charter, the Grand University transformed magic from a wild, dangerous talent into an academic science — with structured curricula, safety protocols, and formal certification for practitioners. This marked the end of Epoch IV and the stabilization of the world into a "High Magic" society.

However, following the rise of the [[Magocracy]] after the Skyfall, the University was co-opted as a tool of ideological control. In SA:2996, the Magocracy centralized all higher education in Mórenórë. While ostensibly open to all, the curriculum was heavily curated to promote the Magocracy's narrative — portraying itself as the benevolent guardian that saved the world from the Grey Rot and the Drakona threat. Generations of leaders were raised within its halls to view the Magocracy's rule as natural and necessary.

By the current era (TA:412), the Grand University remains the most prestigious seat of learning in the world, though its reputation for intellectual freedom is increasingly questioned by those who see through the Magocracy's carefully maintained version of history.
