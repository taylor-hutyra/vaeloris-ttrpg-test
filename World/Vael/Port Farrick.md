# Port Farrick

Port Farrick is a major trading port at the mouth of the Great River on [[Vael]]'s eastern coast, positioned at the crossroads of land and sea power. Founded by the Varnathi Empire circa SA:440, the city was immediately contested by the [[Mirennian League]] and has been a flashpoint of political rivalry ever since.

## Founding

The Varnathi Empire founded Port Farrick during the Coastal Expansion of SA:440, seeking to control maritime trade. When Imperial tax collectors arrived at the existing Mirennian settlements, the captains threw them into the harbor. This act of defiance catalyzed the formation of the [[Mirennian League]] for mutual defense, beginning the centuries-long "Cold War" between land and sea.

## History

Port Farrick emerged as the city-state of "Trade and Diplomacy -- the Gateway." Its position between empires made it a nexus of espionage: the [[Shadewalkers]] guild was founded in its shadows circa SA:710, and the notorious "Whisper-Market" (SA:880) became a bazaar where secrets were traded like grain.

The city endured Captain Vane's devastating six-month blockade (SA:1020-1070), broken only when the Shadewalkers assassinated the pirate lord. More traumatic was the "False Dawn" of SA:1071: during the blockade, the Governor secretly petitioned the Mirennian League for annexation. When the League leaked the petition to buy goodwill with the Republic, the Republic purged Port Farrick -- stripping the nobility of titles and wealth. This betrayal created the deep-seated separatist culture that persists into the Third Age.

## Culture

Port Farrick is cosmopolitan, chaotic, and morally flexible. The city operates under the Mirennian League's corporate thalassocracy, where political weight is measured by trade tonnage. The [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] and [[Azure Hand]] vie for dominance, while the [[Shadewalkers]] operate the Whisper-Market in the city's underbelly. Anti-Imperial sentiment runs deep, and the city remains a hotbed for separatist agitation.

## Present Day

Port Farrick serves as a critical node in global commerce and espionage. The Separatist roots planted by the False Dawn continue to influence local politics, making the city a volatile element in any conflict between the [[Varnathi Imperial Democracy]] and the [[Mirennian League]].
