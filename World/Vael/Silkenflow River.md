# Silkenflow River

The Silkenflow River is one of the great waterways of the mainland continent of [[Vael]], winding through fertile valleys that support agriculture, trade, and one of the most distinctive cultures in [[Vaeloris]] — the Halfling River-Folk.

The River-Folk of the Silkenflow are a semi-nomadic culture of Halflings who live on flotillas of interconnected river barges. They travel the length of the river, trading goods, telling stories, and steadfastly avoiding land-based politics. Their governance is handled by the Flotilla-Council, where the captain of each barge holds an equal vote. The council is led by a single Commodore, elected for their knowledge of the river's currents and their skill in navigating tricky trade negotiations with riverside towns.

The River-Folk are masters of smuggling and fiercely independent, maintaining a carefully cultivated neutrality that has served them well through centuries of conflict between the great powers. Their barges carry everything from Halfling ale and exotic spices to less savory cargo, and their network of contacts along the riverbanks makes them invaluable as couriers and information brokers for those who know how to ask politely and pay fairly.
