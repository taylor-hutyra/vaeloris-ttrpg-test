# Coastal Regions

The Coastal Regions of eastern [[Vael]] are a string of mercantile port cities and trading posts stretching along the continent's jagged coastline. Dominated by the [[Mirennian League]], this region is defined by commerce, seafaring, and a fierce independence from land-based empires.

## Geography

The coast is treacherous, with jagged reefs and unpredictable currents. The discovery of the "Safe-Harbor Currents" by Captain Hanno in SA:145 opened the coast to reliable navigation. The [[Iron-Peak Range]] separates the coast from the [[Varnathi Highlands]], creating a natural cultural divide between "Highlander" and "Coaster" human cultures.

## History

When [[The Great Withdrawal]] scattered the human tribes, those cut off from the highlands by the [[Iron-Peak Range]] turned to the sea. The early "Deep-Hull" ships evolved into sophisticated vessels, and by SA:275 the most successful captains had consolidated into the first Merchant Houses. The Mirennians formalized their alliance into the [[Mirennian League]] in SA:440 to resist Varnathi imperial taxation.

The coastal cities endured the Salt War (SA:654-665), the Guild Wars (SA:1100-1150), and the devastating Drakona attack on Mirenn in SA:2355. Through it all, the coast's identity as a mercantile thalassocracy endured, governed not by birthright but by the tonnage of one's trade fleet.

## Culture

Mirennian coastal culture values freedom, commerce, and expression. The language is flowing and musical, rich with idioms about wind, light, and luck. "Sails full or none at all" captures the Mirennian ethos of bold commitment. The [[Golden Compass Syndicate]], [[Azure Hand]], and [[Sea-Whisperers' Guild]] dominate the region's fractious politics, where status lasts only as long as one's fortune.
