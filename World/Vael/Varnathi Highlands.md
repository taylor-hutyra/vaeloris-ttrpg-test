# Varnathi Highlands

The Varnathi Highlands are the windswept northern plateau of [[Vael]], home to the oldest and most powerful human civilization on the continent. Separated from the coast by the treacherous [[Iron-Peak Range]], the highlands forged a culture of stoic self-reliance, philosophical rigor, and martial discipline.

## Geography

The highlands occupy the elevated northern interior of Vael, a vast plateau of rolling grasslands, rocky escarpments, and wind-carved valleys. The terrain is defensible but harsh, with brutal winters and a short growing season. The elevated stone-road network, first built in SA:110 and expanded under the Empire, connects the region's three major cities: [[Vernath]], [[Luminas]], and [[Castellum]].

## History

Following [[The Great Withdrawal]] in SA:1, human tribes migrated to the defensible high ground to survive the chaos of the post-Paroxysm world. For nearly three decades, seven tribes warred among themselves until [[Vardas the Elder]] united them under the [[First Compact]] and founded [[Vernath]] in SA:28.

The highlands became the seat of the Varnathi Empire when [[Caelius I]] declared the "Mandate of Order" in SA:381. From here, the Empire expanded south to [[Castellum]] and east to [[Port Farrick]], driven by the conviction that mortal order must fill the void left by departed gods.

The region endured the Succession Wars (SA:1950), the Bleeding Years, and the Drakona invasion before stabilizing under the current [[Varnathi Pentarchy]] system. In the present day, political tensions between the [[Crimson Banner]], [[Marble Hand]], and [[Open Palm Movement]] factions threaten the fragile balance of the Pentarchy.

## Culture

Varnathi highland culture emphasizes honor, legacy, and self-discipline. The people speak in formal, archaic patterns heavy with metaphors of crafting and architecture. Common sayings include "Stone breaks louder when ignored" and "Measure twice, live once." Society is highly stratified by perceived duty to the state, from the aristocratic [[Pillar Houses]] to the elite [[Crimson Guard]] to the common citizenry and the [[Assimilated]] non-human populations.
