# Solis

Solis is a Mirennian city on the southern coast of [[Vael]], best known as the site of one of the most infamous sieges in the history of the continent. During the Salt War (SA:654-665), the Republican Legion of [[Castellum]] besieged Solis for two grueling years, seeking to seize the city as part of the [[Varnathi Democracy|Varnathi Republic]]'s campaign to control the vital salt flats of the southern coast.

The siege was not broken by force of arms, but by the "Grey Death" — dysentery caused by the unsanitary conditions of the massive army camps surrounding the city. The epidemic spread through the besieging forces with devastating efficiency, killing more soldiers than the fighting itself. The survivors carried the disease back to the cities, where it became known as "The Soldier's Plague" and caused widespread devastation that dwarfed the casualties of the war.

The horrific toll of the siege and its aftermath led to significant advancements in field medicine and sanitation, grim lessons learned at terrible cost. The conflict was ultimately resolved through the Mórenórë Accords (SA:665), where [[Aen'valar]] Elven diplomats mediated a ceasefire on the neutral ground of [[Mórenórë]], returning borders to their pre-war status while lowering trade tariffs between the two powers.
