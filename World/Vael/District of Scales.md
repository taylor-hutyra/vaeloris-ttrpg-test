# District of Scales

The District of Scales is a segregated quarter within the city of [[Luminas]], established in SA:2725 by the [[Magocracy]] to confine the surviving [[Drakona]] population after the devastation of the Skyfall. Built in the grounded ruins of their former capital, the district represents one of the darkest chapters in post-war Vaeloris — a place where an entire people were stripped of flight, dignity, and freedom.

The Magocracy classified the Drakona as "Wards of the State," a designation that granted them neither citizenship nor autonomy. Under this system, Drakona were used as living batteries to power the anti-Rot wards that held back the Grey Rot, their innate magical energy drained to fuel the Magocracy's containment infrastructure. This exploitation, carried out under the guise of "civic contribution," was one of the regime's most carefully hidden atrocities.

The simmering rage of the confined Drakona eventually gave rise to the [[Broken Wing]], a resistance movement that launched a decades-long campaign of insurgency beginning in SA:2962. The Broken Wing bombed Mana-Pylons and assassinated Magisters, protesting their people's confinement and forced labor. These attacks, while born of genuine oppression, were used by the Magocracy to justify ever-harsher policing — framing the Drakona as an internal threat and complicating the narrative of their victimhood in the eyes of the public.

By the current era (TA:412), the District of Scales remains a place of tension and quiet defiance, its inhabitants caught between the weight of history and the uncertain promise of change.
