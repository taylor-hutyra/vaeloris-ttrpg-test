# Adamant Throne

The Adamant Throne is the seat of the High King of [[Kharnzarak]], the ancestral Dwarven mountain kingdom. Located in the deepest and most sacred halls of the mountain, it has been the symbol of unified Dwarven authority since the founding of the kingdom in SA:91, when Thane [[Korin Barazkor]] united the warring clans and established himself as the first High King.

The Throne is paired with the [[Crown of the Mountain]] — the seven-metal regalia of Dwarven kingship, whose [[Star-Metal]] central band is said to heat up if the wearer speaks a lie while seated upon the Adamant Throne. Together, the Throne and Crown represent the twin pillars of Dwarven governance: the endurance of stone and the accountability of truth.

The current occupant is High King [[Thorgar VII]] of the [[Barazkor]] Clan, who rules from behind the sealed Great Adamant Gates. His authority is absolute, though the Stone-Lay Council of nine clan-lords serves as advisors, high court, and legislative body. Since the sealing of Kharnzarak in SA:1400 — when High King Thorgar VI ordered the gates hammered shut against the "surface taint" — the Adamant Throne has been hidden from the outside world, accessible only to the Dwarves who remain within the mountain.

For the surface world, the Adamant Throne is more legend than reality — a symbol of the Dwarven kingdom's unyielding isolationism and the ancient, immovable power that lies sealed beneath the stone.
