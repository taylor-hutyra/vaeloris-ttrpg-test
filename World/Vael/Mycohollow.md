# Mycohollow

Mycohollow is the largest and most influential [[Forest Gnome]] settlement on [[Vael]], a city woven into a gargantuan, semi-sentient fungal grove. It is the political, spiritual, and intelligence center of Gnomish civilization on the mainland.

## Geography

Mycohollow is built within and around a massive symbiotic fungal grove, its structures grown organically from living mycelium, shaped wood, and carefully cultivated mushroom caps. The city exists in the dappled light of the forest floor, virtually invisible to outsiders. Bioluminescent fungi illuminate winding pathways, and the air carries the rich scent of loam and spores. The word [[Fynora]] -- the soft, dappled green light filtering through a forest canopy -- describes the city's perpetual atmosphere.

## Government

Mycohollow is governed by the [[Mycelial Council]], composed not of elected officials but of Gnomes who have achieved the deepest symbiotic link with the grove. The council is led by two co-equal [[Twin Speakers]]:
- The **[[Myco-Whisperer]]**: spiritual leader, responsible for the grove's health and its relationship with the natural world (representing [[Ceryn-Maia]])
- The **[[Geo-Weaver]]**: lead artisan, responsible for the city's physical structures and the integration of craft and nature (representing [[Ilys-Tekne]])

## The Philosophical Divide

Three factions compete for Mycohollow's direction:
- The **[[Balancers]]** (majority): gentle guidance and subtle intervention; the current ruling philosophy
- The **[[Purifiers]]** (zealous): believe the world's pattern is actively corrupted by the Loud-Walkers and must be excised
- The **[[Integrators]]** (innovators): argue the [[Syn-Koinos]] must evolve by weaving in new elements like foreign magic and technology

## Intelligence Network

In SA:2100, Forest Gnomes modified the mycelial network of the forests to act as a continental vibration-sensor -- the "Root-Hack." The [[Myco-Whisperers]] became the world's premier intelligence agency, capable of tracking troop movements anywhere there are roots. They sell this data to maintain the balance of power, making Mycohollow an invisible but indispensable geopolitical force.

## The Grey Rot

During the Grey Rot crisis of SA:2710, the Gnomes of Mycohollow discovered that the Rot was a fungal-magical hybrid. Their development of "Spore-Masks" and alchemical sprays proved that the [[Magocracy]]'s "burn it all" approach was not the only solution, elevating Gnomish prestige immensely.
