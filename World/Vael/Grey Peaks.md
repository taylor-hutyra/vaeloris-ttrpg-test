# Grey Peaks

The Grey Peaks are a range of high, inaccessible mountains on [[Vael]], home to the [[Crag-Striders]] -- an isolated enclave of primarily [[Firbolg]] and [[Galapa]] [[Waldkyn]] who have adapted their philosophy of balance to the unforgiving nature of stone.

## Geography

The Grey Peaks feature steep, weathered summits and high-altitude valleys accessible only through narrow, easily defended passes. The climate is harsh, with long winters and brief, intense summers. The Crag-Striders inhabit sheltered valleys between the peaks, where they practice a patient, mountain-adapted form of Waldkyn life.

## The Crag-Striders

Governance is by a [[Council of Elders]], where leadership is based purely on age and lived experience. Their politics are defined by [[Still-Growth]] -- patient, deliberate progress. The Crag-Striders are slow to anger and slower to act, but once their course is set, they are as unmovable as the mountains themselves. They embody the Firbolg's stoic nurturing ("A steady hand holds the heaviest pail") and the Galapa's patient judgment ("A slow truth is a strong truth").

## Historical Significance

The Grey Peaks gained wider renown during the Drakona War. In SA:2410, the [[Battle of the Grey Peaks]] saw the [[Allied Fleet]] intercept a Drakona armada attempting to cross the mountains into the central plains. The battle raged for three days in a thunderstorm, ending in a Pyrrhic victory: the Allies stopped the invasion but lost 60% of their ships. The engagement proved that Drakona individual warriors could shred wooden hulls, leading to the adoption of "Star-Steel" alloy plating for future vessels.

The Crag-Striders themselves remained largely uninvolved in the aerial battle, watching from their valleys as the sky burned above them -- a characteristic response from a people who refuse to be rushed into action by the urgencies of the [[Single-Forms]].
