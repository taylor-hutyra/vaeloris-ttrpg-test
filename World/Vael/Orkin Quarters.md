# Orkin Quarters

The Orkin Quarters are segregated urban districts within the cities of the [[Varnathi Democracy]], established under the Charter of Civic Unity (SA:2879) as part of the [[Magocracy]]'s post-war reconstruction effort. The most prominent of these districts is located in [[Luminas]], though similar quarters exist in other major Varnathi cities.

The Charter was framed as a progressive measure — granting Orkin "Subject Status" and the legal right to live within Human cities for the first time. In practice, it confined Orkin populations to specific districts and excluded them from legal guild membership, effectively creating second-class citizens who were tolerated but never truly integrated.

Within the Quarters, the Orkin have built their own governance structures out of necessity. In Luminas, a Neighborhood Council of elders representing the various clans mediates disputes, organizes community defense, and acts as the official liaison to the human city guard. Real power, however, often lies with the internal guilds — particularly the Butchers' Guild (which controls the food supply) and the Masons' Guild (which controls construction and repair). These guild leaders frequently hold more influence than the elders on the council.

Excluded from legitimate economic opportunities, many urban Orkin formed "Shadow-Syndicates" — criminal networks that became the primary means of survival and upward mobility within the Quarters. This underground economy, while born of systemic oppression, has further entrenched the prejudices of the surrounding Human population, creating a cycle of marginalization that persists into the Third Age. The "Assimilated" Orkin are granted rights under Varnathi law, but systemic prejudice remains a defining feature of their daily existence.
