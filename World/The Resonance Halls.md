# The Resonance Halls

**The Resonance Halls** are the sacred spaces of perfect acoustics established by [[Vaelynn]] the Songkeeper during the First Age. Within these halls, the Great Symphony incorporating all cultural musical traditions was created. The Singer's Harp remains preserved here for true musicians.
