# The Hollow Glade

**The Hollow Glade** is a hidden sacred site deep within [[The Dreaming Grove]], where [[Ceridara]] the Greenward and [[Illysia]] the Craftmother secretly grew the first [[Gnomes]] during the Golden Age of Creation. It is the birthplace of the Gnomish people — a quiet act of creation hidden from the grander stage of [[Vaeloris]].
