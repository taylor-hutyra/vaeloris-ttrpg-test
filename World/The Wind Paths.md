# The Wind Paths

**The Wind Paths** are reliable atmospheric currents established by [[Gwynfaer]] the Windweaver during the First Age, enabling global travel. Gwynfaer also taught dragons the mastery of flight through these currents. The magical tradition of [[Wind-Shaping]] descends from knowledge of these paths.
