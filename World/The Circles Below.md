# The Circles Below

**The Circles Below** is a separate dimension created by [[Kael'Zorai]] during the First Age for unconstrained experimentation. Originally a realm of isolation, it was transformed during [[The Paroxysm]] into a crucible of twisted genesis.

## The Monsters of the Paroxysm

In Kael'Zorai's final act of defiance, the Circles Below spawned:

- **Aberrations** — embodiments of corrupted systematic structure
- **Riftborn** — creatures of dimensional instability
- **Echo-entities** — nightmare reflections of failed systematic creation

These beings spilled forth through arcane scars — rifts where Kael'Zorai's corrupted essence bled through reality, creating lasting **Scars of Dissonance** that remain as sites of cursed energy.
