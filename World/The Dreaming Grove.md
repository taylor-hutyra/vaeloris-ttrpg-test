# The Dreaming Grove

**The Dreaming Grove** is the sanctuary established by [[Ceridara]] the Greenward during the First Age as the center of forest learning. From here, networks of sacred groves connecting all woodlands were created.

## The Hollow Glade

Deep within the Dreaming Grove lies [[The Hollow Glade]] — the hidden site where [[Ceridara]] and [[Illysia]] secretly grew the first [[Gnomes]] during the Golden Age of Creation.
