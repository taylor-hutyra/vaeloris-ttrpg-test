# The First Forge

**The First Forge** is the sacred workshop established by [[Korthak]] the Stonefather during the First Age. It was the birthplace of the dwarven crafting arts, where the [[Dwarves]] learned the fundamental techniques of metalworking and stone-shaping under divine guidance. The First Hammer remains here for worthy craftspeople.
