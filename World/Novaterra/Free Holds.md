# Free Holds

The Free Holds are a collection of fortified, independent city-states in the rugged central highlands of [[Novaterra]], established in SA:1250 by displaced Orkin and [[Kharnzarak|Bronrûn Dwarves]] who rejected the authority of both Human colonial powers on the continent.

The Holds were born from desperation and defiance. The Orkin arrived via the "Mirennian Passage" — thousands of starving refugees who paid Mirennian smugglers for transport after the Varnathi Republic's brutal War of the Barren Hills (SA:1210) devastated their mainland homeland. The Bronrûn Dwarves, blamed for the Earthwake of Kharnzarak (SA:1200) and exiled by the paranoid High King, had already migrated to Novaterra through the Wandering Kin network. Finding common cause in their mutual exile, these two peoples moved inland to territory neither Human colony claimed and built a new society on their own terms.

The Free Holds function more like mercenary corporations or industrial syndicates than traditional tribal governments. Leadership is won through a combination of business acumen, strategic alliances, and proven competence — a meritocracy where endurance is measured in wealth and influence as much as physical might. Major Holds include the Iron-Grit Syndicate (mining operations supplying [[Gearhaven]]), the Shattered-Spear Company (a legendary mercenary outfit), and the Dust-Diggers (independent prospectors roaming Novaterra's deserts).

Their establishment transformed Novaterra into a tri-polar political landscape: the Varnathi East, the Mirennian West, and the Independent Central Highlands — a balance of power that persists into the Third Age.
