# Nexus

**Nexus** is a chartered [[Waldkyn]] city in the Great Forests of [[Novaterra]], founded in SA:1510 by the [[Chartered Kith]] -- progressive Waldkyn who believed that **The Synthesis** could encompass civilization, not just nature. It is a marvel of bio-architecture: buildings grown from genetically modified trees, reinforced with living steel, fusing nature-magic with technomancy.

## Government

Nexus is governed by the **[[Chartered Kith Weaver's Synod]]**, a technocratic council composed of Arch-Weavers from each of the city's great Spires. A position on the Synod is earned by creating a masterwork of synthesis -- a new form of magitech, a perfectly integrated architectural design, or a revolutionary social policy.

The **First Weaver** is the elected leader of the Synod. Their primary role is to set the city's "Loom-Pattern" -- the grand, decades-long projects that guide Nexus's evolution.

## Philosophy

The Chartered Kith reject the naturalism of their [[Quiet Vales Kith]] cousins on the mainland, believing instead in **The Great Weaving** -- the active integration of technology and nature. They see urban life and technomancy not as corruptions of The Synthesis but as complex new notes to be woven into the world's song.

## Factions

Two major factions define the city's political landscape:

- **The Gear-Song Harmonists** -- the dominant faction, fascinated by the logic and efficiency of [[Gearhaven]]'s technology. They advocate deeper integration with technomancy, believing the predictable rhythm of machines is a beautiful new note in the song of creation.
- **The Green-Root Accord** -- a powerful opposition with strong [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] ties. They warn that over-reliance on sterile mechanics creates an imbalanced, "soulless" song. They champion bio-technology and symbiotic systems, arguing that true progress must honor the living half of The Synthesis.

## Character

Nexus is unlike any other city in [[Vaeloris]]. Its streets are living roots; its towers are ancient trees shaped into spiraling habitats. Bridges of woven vine connect canopy-level districts, while underground warrens house research laboratories where [[Waldkyn]] bio-engineers and [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] naturalists collaborate. The air smells of growing things and ozone. It serves as [[Novaterra]]'s agricultural and biological research hub.
