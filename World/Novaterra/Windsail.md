# Windsail

Windsail is the first permanent settlement established on the continent of [[Novaterra]], founded in SA:835 by the [[Mirennian League]] on the new continent's western coast. The colony was built and maintained in absolute secrecy, with its location concealed from the [[Varnathi Democracy|Varnathi Republic]] through the use of "Ghost Charts" — maps enchanted by Wind-Shapers that appeared blank to anyone who did not know the cipher key.

The settlement's founding represented the League's most audacious strategic move. Only the most loyal House members were permitted to emigrate, creating a highly aristocratic colonial culture distinct from the rougher mercantile society of the Mirennian homeland. The colony provided the League with exotic timber and spices that baffled Republican economists, who could not determine their origin.

Windsail's isolation was tested during the Mirennian Guild Wars (SA:1100), when supply lines from the homeland were severed. The colonists were forced to become self-sufficient, developing a resilient frontier culture that blended Mirennian naval traditions with the practical demands of taming a new continent. When the secret of Novaterra was eventually sold to the Republic in SA:1115, Windsail braced for invasion — but the continent proved too vast for either power to dominate entirely, and the western coast remained firmly within the Mirennian cultural sphere.
