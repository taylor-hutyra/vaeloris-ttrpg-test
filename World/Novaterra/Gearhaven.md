# Gearhaven

**Gearhaven** is the premier industrial and technomantic city on [[Novaterra]], a vertical metropolis of brass, steam, and glowing runes built into the sea cliffs of Rust-Water Bay. Founded in SA:1485 through the "Great Collaboration" of [[Bronr'n]] Dwarves, [[Elm-korin]] Dwarves, human mages, and [[Gnomes]], it represents the pinnacle of the fusion between magic and machine.

## Government

Gearhaven is governed by the **[[Gearhaven Cog-Council]]**, a technocratic meritocracy composed of nine Guild-Masters elected based on their proven contributions to the city's progress. Seats are not permanent -- a Guild-Master can be challenged and replaced if a subordinate presents a more innovative design.

The **Arch-Artisan** heads the council and sets the city's "Grand Design" -- its long-term goals for research and development. There are no clans in Gearhaven, only guilds. Status is determined entirely by skill, ingenuity, and project success.

The **Binding Accord** (SA:1485) ensured no single race ruled the city. After the Masked Rebellion of SA:1500, when "Mundane" workers seized the geothermal plants, the Guild of Labor was granted two seats, establishing Gearhaven as a true meritocracy.

## History

Gearhaven was born from desperation. A massive refugee flotilla -- human mages fleeing the [[Mage-Purge Wars]], exiled [[Elm-korin]] and [[Bronr'n]] Dwarves, and opportunistic [[Gnomes]] -- landed at Rust-Water Bay in SA:1475 and decided to stop running.

The city quickly became a superpower. In SA:1680, it launched the **First Fleet of Iron** -- steam-powered ironclads with Arcane Cannons -- to end the Mage-Purge on the mainland. The Battle of Crimson Field (SA:1695) proved that magic and technology together could defeat conventional armies.

The **Technocratic Blockade** (SA:1880) marked a darker chapter: short on Brimstone fuel, Gearhaven blockaded [[Federation of City-States]] ports. The [[Varnathi]] Empire and Federation formed the **Iron Pact** to break it, proving that resource needs superseded ideology.

## Notable Features

The city is built vertically into sea cliffs, powered by geothermal vents. Its architecture is a fusion of Dwarven stonework, Gnomish clockwork precision, and arcane engineering. The air hums with the **Gear-Song** -- the constant rhythm of steam turbines and mana-converters.

## Factions

- **The Mainframe Purity** -- dominant faction of zealous Elm-korin believers in perfect, sterile order.
- **The Symbiotic Weave** -- [[Gnomes|Gnomish]]-led faction advocating fusion of organic and mechanical systems.
