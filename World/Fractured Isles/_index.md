# Fractured Isles

The Fractured Isles are a sprawling archipelago of jagged volcanic islands, coral atolls, and hidden coves scattered across the southern seas of [[Vaeloris]]. They are the domain of the [[Pirate Confederacy]] — a lawless maritime nation built on the simple philosophy that freedom is worth more than respectability.

## Geography

The archipelago consists of hundreds of islands, ranging from bare rock spires jutting from the surf to lush tropical landmasses large enough to support small settlements. Treacherous reefs, unpredictable currents, and sudden storms make navigation deadly for the uninitiated. This natural fortress is the pirates' greatest defense — only those who know the secret channels can thread the labyrinth safely.

The largest and most fortified island is [[Serpent's Tooth]], the traditional seat of pirate authority. Its natural harbor is hidden behind a narrow channel flanked by sheer cliffs, and its interior holds fresh water springs, dense jungle, and a network of caves used for storing plunder and sheltering during hurricanes.

## The Code of the Coast

In 1020 S.A., the renegade [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] admiral [[Captain Vane]] accomplished what no navy could: he united the fractious pirate crews under a single code of law. The Code of the Coast established rules for the division of spoils, the treatment of prisoners, and the resolution of disputes between crews. It transformed piracy from a disorganized nuisance into a geopolitical force capable of blockading major ports and dictating trade terms.

Vane's assassination by the [[Shadewalkers]] in 1070 S.A. fragmented the Confederacy temporarily, but the Code survived its founder. It has been amended, debated, and occasionally violated ever since, but it remains the closest thing to a constitution the [[Fractured Isles]] have ever known.

## The Sky-War and After

During the [[Dragonfall]], the pirates played both sides with characteristic opportunism. The [[Scaled Pact]] of 2280 S.A. saw them serving as spies and scouts for the [[Drakona]] in exchange for aerial protection. When the tide turned, the [[Privateer Accords]] of 2595 S.A. brought them into the [[Allied Command]] — their sea-ships retrofitted with flight-cores to become the dreaded Sky-Privateers who ravaged Drakona supply lines.

After the Skyfall stripped all flight magic from the world, the pirates adapted as they always do. They developed [[Wave-Skimmers]] — extremely fast hydrofoil vessels — and early non-magical gliders launched from clifftops to scout prey. In the Third Age, the Confederacy thrives as a smuggling empire, moving contraband past [[Magocracy]] inspectors and maintaining a network of secret harbors and black markets that spans the globe.

## The Pirate Identity

The [[Fractured Isles]] attract every outcast, exile, and malcontent the "civilized" world produces. [[Orkin]] who refuse the indignity of the Quarters, [[Drakona]] who will not submit to the District of Scales, mages fleeing the Magister's Watch, and humans simply unwilling to kneel — they all find a berth here. The Confederacy asks no questions about your past, only whether you can pull your weight. It is a brutal meritocracy, violent and free, and for many it is the only place in [[Vaeloris]] where they can breathe.
