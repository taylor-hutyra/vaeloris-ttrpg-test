# Serpent's Tooth

Serpent's Tooth is a fortified island in the southern archipelago of the [[Fractured Isles]], serving as the de facto capital of the [[Pirate Confederacy]]. Established as a pirate stronghold by the renegade Mirennian admiral Captain Vane in SA:1020, the island transformed piracy from a disorganized nuisance into a genuine geopolitical force.

The island's natural geography makes it an ideal naval fortress. Hidden coves and treacherous reefs protect the harbor approaches, while the tropical climate supports a self-sustaining population of pirates, smugglers, and those who prefer to live outside the laws of the great powers. The "Code of the Coast," first drafted by Vane himself, serves as the only law on the island — a rough framework governing conduct between crews, the division of plunder, and the resolution of blood-feuds.

Governance on Serpent's Tooth is handled by the [[Pirate Confederacy|Captains' Council]], an irregular gathering of the most feared and respected pirate captains. There is no single ruler; alliances are fleeting, betrayal is common, and political power flows to whoever commands the most ships and the fiercest crew. Despite this chaos, the pirates are united in their hatred of the [[Mirennian League]] and the Varnathi navy.

During the Drakona War, the Confederacy was offered legal status through the Privateer Accords in exchange for retrofitting their ships with flight-cores and attacking Drakona supply lines. After the Skyfall grounded all flying vessels, the pirates adapted once again — developing fast hydrofoil "Wave-Skimmers" and cliff-launched gliders to resume their trade in the post-war era.
