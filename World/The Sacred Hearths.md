# The Sacred Hearths

**The Sacred Hearths** are a network of eternal flames kindled by [[Pyrathan]] the Flame-Bearer during the First Age, connecting communities across [[Vaeloris]] through shared sacred fire. They serve as pathways to divine wisdom through proper ritual.
