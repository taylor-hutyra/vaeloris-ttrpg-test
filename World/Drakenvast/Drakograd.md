# Drakograd

**Drakograd** ("The Hearth of Scales") is the ancestral capital of the [[Drakona]], a fortress-city carved into the heart of a dormant volcano in [[Drakenvast]]. It is the seat of the **[[Vozhd]]**, the supreme autocratic leader of the Drakona people, and the political, military, and religious center of the **[[Vozhdate]]**.

## Geography

Drakograd is built into and around a dormant volcanic cone in the mountainous highlands of [[Drakenvast]]. Its architecture is monumental and fortress-like -- basalt spires, flame-lit halls carved from cooling lava, and massive sculptures wrought from volcanic stone. The city descends deep into the volcano's interior, where geothermal heat powers the legendary **volcanic forges** that produce fire-forged metals and [[Drakona|Draconic]] Steel.

The surrounding landscape is rich in **Ember Crystal** -- a naturally occurring substance that stores thermal energy -- as well as volcanic minerals and fire-forged metals. Control of these resources drove much of the Drakona's imperial ambition.

## Government

Drakograd serves as the seat of the **[[Vozhdate]]**, the Drakona autocracy:

- **The [[Vozhd]]** -- the Supreme Leader who claims to be the living vessel of the [[Eternal Flame]], holding absolute authority over military, religious, and civil matters.
- **The [[Council of Scales]]** -- a nominally advisory body of twelve elder Drakona from the great bloodlines. In practice, it rubber-stamps the Vozhd's decrees.
- **The [[Zhar'kov]]** ("Flame-Watchers") -- the secret police who root out dissent and monitor Drakona abroad.

## History

Before the Skyfall, Drakograd served primarily as a mining and forging center while the true seats of power floated above in the Cloud Cities. When the **[[Earth-Anchor Ritual]]** (SA:2688) brought the Cloud Cities crashing down, Drakograd became the only intact Drakona city, and the surviving military commanders consolidated power here around a single Vozhd. What began as wartime necessity calcified into permanent autocracy.

The **[[Broken Wing Insurgency]]** (SA:2950) brought violence to the city itself, as flightless Drakona bombed Mana-Pylons and assassinated [[Magocracy]] Magisters in protest against their second-class status.

## Character

Drakograd is a city of deep paranoia and fierce pride. Temples of the [[Eternal Flame]] burn perpetually in every district, tended by the **Zharnitsy** (Flame-Priests). The [[Khor'drak]] old-blood aristocracy dominates the military hierarchy and patronizes the traditional arts -- fire-forging academies, monumental sculpture, and martial ceremonies. Beneath the surface, the [[Zhel'nov]] maintain underground networks to smuggle dissidents out and forbidden texts in.
