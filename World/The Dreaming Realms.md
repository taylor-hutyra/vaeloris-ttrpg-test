# The Dreaming Realms

**The Dreaming Realms** are a separate dimension established by [[Oneirys]] the Dreamwalker during the First Age for the safe exploration of prophetic consciousness. Those who enter may receive divine guidance through dreams, though the visions require mortal wisdom to interpret.
