# Mount Cinder

**Mount Cinder** is an active volcano on **[[Vol]]**, a [[Mirennian League]] outpost island. Its catastrophic eruption in SA:635 was one of the defining disasters of the Second Age, reshaping the political and economic landscape of [[Vaeloris]] for centuries.

## The Sundering (SA:635)

The dormant volcano erupted with cataclysmic force in SA:635. Later scholars theorize that a [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] Wind-Shaper attempted to tap into geothermal vents and failed, triggering the explosion. The eruption was not merely geological -- it carried the taint of disrupted elemental magic.

### Consequences

- **The Gloom Famine**: Volcanic ash choked the skies across the region for three years, devastating agriculture and causing widespread starvation.
- **The Tsunami of Tears**: Massive waves generated by the eruption destroyed 40% of the [[Mirennian League]]'s fleet and harbor infrastructure, crippling their naval and economic power.
- **Economic Collapse**: The combined destruction of fleet, harbors, and agricultural output left the Mirennian League vulnerable for the first time, directly enabling the [[Varnathi]] Republic to launch the Salt War (SA:654).

## Connection to the Bronrûn Exodus

Though separated by centuries, Mount Cinder's eruption set in motion geological instabilities that contributed to the **Earthwake of [[Kharnzarak]]** (SA:1200). The resulting disaster triggered the **[[Bronrûn]] Exodus**, when the Growth/Reformist clan was blamed and fled the mountain, eventually migrating to [[Novaterra]] through the [[Wandering Kin]] network.

## Current Status

Mount Cinder remains active. The volcano continues to vent sulfurous gases and occasional lava flows. The surrounding landscape is a blasted wasteland of hardened lava fields and geothermal springs. Despite the danger, the area is rich in volcanic minerals and geothermal energy, attracting bold prospectors and researchers.
