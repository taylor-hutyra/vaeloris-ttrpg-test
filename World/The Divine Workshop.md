# The Divine Workshop

**The Divine Workshop** was founded by [[Illysia]] the Craftmother during the First Age as the center of artistic excellence. Here, all races learned divine craftsmanship, and the [[Artisan's Codex]] establishing the principles of perfect craft was created.
