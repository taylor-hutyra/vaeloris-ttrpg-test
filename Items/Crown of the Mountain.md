# Crown of the Mountain

The Crown of the Mountain is the supreme regalia of the Dwarven High King of [[Kharnzarak]], a masterwork of metallurgy and symbolism that has rested upon the brow of every ruler since the kingdom's unification in SA:91.

The Crown is forged from seven distinct metals, each representing one of the seven founding clans that were united under the first High King, Thane [[Korin Barazkor]]. At its center lies a band of [[Star-Metal]] — the impossibly rare meteorite iron found only in the highest peaks of the Dragon's Tooth range. This central band is both the Crown's most precious component and its most remarkable feature: it is said to heat up if the wearer speaks a lie while seated on the [[Adamant Throne]], binding the authority of kingship to the accountability of truth.

The Crown's construction reflects the deepest values of Dwarven culture — the unity of craft and clan, the endurance of stone and metal, and the sacred weight of honest leadership. Each of the seven metals was contributed by a founding clan as a symbol of their submission to the unified kingdom, making the Crown itself a physical embodiment of the pact that ended the inter-clan Tunnel Wars.

Since the sealing of Kharnzarak in SA:1400, the Crown of the Mountain has been hidden from the outside world along with the kingdom it represents. To the surface races, it is a legend — a symbol of the ancient Dwarven power that lies locked away beneath the mountains, waiting in silence.
