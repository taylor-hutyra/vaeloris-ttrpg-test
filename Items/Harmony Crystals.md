# Harmony Crystals

The **Harmony Crystals** are divine artifacts created by [[Korthak]] the Stonefather at [[The First Forge]] during the First Age to maintain cosmic balance throughout [[Vaeloris]]. They were distributed across the world to ensure that the equilibrium between divine domains would persist even after the [[The Great Withdrawal|Great Withdrawal]].
