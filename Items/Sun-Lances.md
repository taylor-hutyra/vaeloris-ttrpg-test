# Sun-Lances

Sun-Lances are devastating magical bombardment weapons developed by the [[Drakona]] Empire, deployed during the opening phase of the Drakona War (Epoch VI, SA:2341-2700). These weapons projected focused beams of solar magic from the hulls of Drakona cloud-ships, capable of obliterating major fortifications and military installations from high altitude.

The Sun-Lances were the terror weapon of their age. On the first day of spring in SA:2341, thousands of Drakona dragon-kin warriors descended from their hidden cloud-cities accompanied by a systematic aerial bombardment that used Sun-Lances to shatter the defenses of the northern [[Varnathi Democracy|Varnathi]] provinces. Within three months, thirty percent of the Varnathi Empire was occupied, and millions of refugees fled south in the greatest humanitarian crisis in the history of [[Vaeloris]].

The surface nations had no answer to the Sun-Lances in the early years of the war, as their armies were designed for ground combat and lacked any means of reaching the Drakona's high-altitude fleet. It was only after twenty years of frantic innovation — culminating in the launch of the Allied Air Fleet in SA:2380 — that the surface powers could contest the skies. The Sun-Lances became less decisive as Allied ships were armored with "Star-Steel" alloy and air combat tactics evolved, but they remained a feared weapon throughout the conflict.

Following the Skyfall (SA:2688) and the destruction of the Drakona cloud-cities, the Sun-Lances were lost along with most Drakona military technology. Whether any surviving examples exist in the crash-site ruins remains a matter of speculation and treasure-hunter ambition.
