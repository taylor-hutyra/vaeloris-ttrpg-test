# Fungril

The **Fungril** are the quiet mystics and historians of the [[Waldkyn]], embodying the balance of **Growth** ([[Virandros]]) and **Refinement** ([[Myrasael]]). They possess an innate understanding of cycles — of decay feeding new life, of endings seeding beginnings. Their characteristic phrase, *"The rot is tomorrow's soil,"* expresses their deep appreciation for the transformative power of dissolution and renewal.

## Role Among the Waldkyn

Fungril serve as the memory and spiritual depth of their communities. They are the historians who preserve the oldest Tellings, the mystics who divine meaning from natural patterns, and the herbalists whose knowledge of decomposition and growth gives them unmatched skill with medicines and poisons alike. Where other variants look outward, the Fungril look inward and downward — into the hidden networks of root and mycelium that connect all living things.

## Temperament and Culture

Introspective and often enigmatic, the Fungril speak in riddles and metaphors even by Waldkyn standards. They are comfortable with silence and with the long, dark processes that other folk find unsettling — rot, fermentation, the slow breakdown of the old to nourish the new. This gives them a reputation for morbidity among outsiders, but among the Waldkyn they are valued as those who understand the parts of the Synthesis that others would prefer to ignore.

Fungril are drawn to liminal spaces — the boundary between forest and clearing, the edge of a swamp, the moment between sleep and waking. They believe that truth is found not in bright certainty but in the fertile darkness where old forms dissolve and new ones have not yet taken shape.

## The Murk-Tenders

Among the **Murk-Tenders of [[the Fens]]**, Fungril and [[Ribbet]] form the dominant population. The Fungril hold particular influence through the Shamanic Circle, and many **Bog-Seers** throughout history have been Fungril mystics. Their ancient knowledge of herbal remedies, poisons, and the secret properties of marsh plants is found nowhere else in Vaeloris. The Murk-Tenders are deeply territorial and distrustful of outsiders, but those who earn their trust gain access to wisdom that predates the written word.

## Among the Kith

In the [[Whispering Vales]], Fungril often serve as advisors to the Root-Speaker, their understanding of history and cycles making them natural keepers of law and precedent. Among the [[Aerinórë Kith]], they are the most spiritually attuned of an already contemplative people. The [[Chartered Kith]] of [[Nexus]] have fewer Fungril than other variants — the bright, ordered city sits uneasily with their preference for shadow and ambiguity — though those who do settle there often become researchers in bio-decomposition and waste reclamation, finding harmony in the city's hidden cycles.
