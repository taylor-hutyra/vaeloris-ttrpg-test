# Faun

The **Faun** are the joyful celebrants, musicians, and guides of the [[Waldkyn]], embodying the balance of **Growth** ([[Virandros]]) and **Inspiration** ([[Elaraieth]]). While they share the same Voice influences as the [[Faerie]], the emphasis is reversed — where Faerie are driven by Inspiration's curiosity, Faun are driven by Growth's exuberance. Their characteristic phrase, *"Dance the worry out!"* reflects their belief that joy itself is a form of balance.

## Role Among the Waldkyn

Faun serve as the emotional heart of their communities. They are the musicians who play at Tellings, the guides who lead travelers through unfamiliar terrain with song and story, and the celebrants who ensure that the rituals of the Waldkyn calendar are performed with genuine feeling rather than hollow routine. In the [[Whispering Vales]], the **Faun Horn-Speaker** represents their people on the [[Quiet Vales Great Council]], bringing a voice of passion and emotional truth to the council's deliberations.

## Temperament and Culture

Faun possess an infectious energy that can lift the spirits of even the most burdened soul. They are natural empaths, reading the emotional currents of a group the way a river-warden reads the flow of water. This makes them gifted mediators in their own way — not through the patient deliberation of the [[Galapa]], but through the power of shared laughter, music, and the simple reminder that life is meant to be celebrated.

Their hedonistic streak is not mere indulgence. The Faun believe deeply that joy is the purest expression of balance — that when a community can still celebrate, it is still whole. When the celebrations stop, something has gone profoundly wrong. This philosophy makes them sensitive barometers of communal well-being, and a village where the Faun have gone quiet is a village in trouble.

## Among the Kith

In the [[Chartered Kith]] of [[Nexus]], Faun have become renowned performers and entertainers, weaving traditional Waldkyn Tellings with new urban art forms. Some traditionalists of the [[Quiet Vales Kith]] see this as a dilution of sacred practice, but the Faun of Nexus argue that the Synthesis must be felt, not merely preserved. On [[Aerinórë]], Faun serve as the keepers of communal gatherings, ensuring that the contemplative [[Aerinórë Kith]] do not lose themselves entirely in quiet introspection.
