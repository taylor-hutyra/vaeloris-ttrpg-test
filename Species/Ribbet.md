# Ribbet

The **Ribbet** are the adaptable healers and river-wardens of the [[Waldkyn]], embodying the balance of **Growth** ([[Virandros]]) and **Flowing Change**. They are unique among the variants in that their second Voice influence is not attributed to a single creator but to the principle of change itself — the movement of water, the turning of seasons, the endless adaptation of life to circumstance. Their characteristic phrase, *"We all sing in the same rain,"* captures their deep belief in communal solidarity and shared experience.

## Role Among the Waldkyn

Ribbet serve as healers, herbalists, and wardens of the waterways that sustain their communities. In the [[Whispering Vales]], they tend the rivers, streams, and wetlands, ensuring that the waters run clean and that the delicate ecosystems along the banks remain in balance. Their natural affinity for water and their understanding of the healing properties of aquatic plants make them the foremost healers among the Waldkyn — the ones called upon when illness or injury threatens the community's harmony.

## Temperament and Culture

The Ribbet are the most gregarious of the Waldkyn variants, thriving in large groups and finding their strength in community. They are adaptable to an almost remarkable degree, able to adjust their habits, their methods, and even their temperament to suit changing circumstances. This flexibility is their greatest gift and, occasionally, their greatest challenge — a Ribbet without a community to anchor them can become aimless, flowing without direction like water without banks.

The traditional Telling of *The River and the Rock* features the Ribbet prominently. A Ribbet tribe, swift and eager, tried to cross a raging river through sheer speed and failed. It was only when a [[Galapa]] elder showed them the calm path behind the stone — where patience met current — that they crossed with ease. The lesson resonates deeply: adaptability without patience is merely chaos.

## The Murk-Tenders

Among the **Murk-Tenders of [[the Fens]]**, the Ribbet share dominance with the [[Fungril]]. Where the Fungril provide mystical insight and historical memory, the Ribbet provide practical healing skill and communal organization. Together they form the backbone of a reclusive culture that possesses ancient knowledge of herbal remedies and poisons found nowhere else in Vaeloris. Ribbet Murk-Tenders are the most likely to make cautious contact with outsiders, their natural adaptability making them the best suited to navigate the boundary between their secretive homeland and the wider world.

## Among the Kith

In the [[Chartered Kith]] of [[Nexus]], Ribbet have become sought-after physicians and water-engineers, their understanding of flow and adaptation translating naturally into the management of the city's complex water systems. Among the [[Aerinórë Kith]], Ribbet healers are valued companions on the long journeys between nomadic camps, their adaptability making them comfortable in any terrain and their communal instincts ensuring that no one in the traveling group is forgotten or left behind.
