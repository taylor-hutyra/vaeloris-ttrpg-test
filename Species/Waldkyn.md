# Waldkyn

The Waldkyn do not have a creation myth; they have a creation **memory**. They believe they were the last great collaborative act of the **Four Voices** — their name for the four creator First Hands. Recognizing that their previous creations ([[Dwarves]], [[Elves]]) were powerful but incomplete expressions of singular concepts, the Four Voices wove their essences together to create a people who would be a living embodiment of balance. This core belief is called **The Synthesis**.

## Origin

The Waldkyn believe the world's beginning was a violent argument between divine forces, a cacophony that nearly shattered existence. Their own origin was the resolution — the final, deliberate chord of harmony played by the exhausted creators: **[[Tham'dran]]** (Stability), **[[Virandros]]** (Growth), **[[Myrasael]]** (Refinement), and **[[Elaraieth]]** (Inspiration).

The great cataclysm (the Paroxysm) is known to them as the **Fifth Silence** — a time when a dissonant, selfish note tried to overwrite the symphony of creation. They understand [[Kael'Zorai]] not as a villain, but as the embodiment of imbalance — a voice that refused to listen. Their very existence is the world's answer to that silence.

The Waldkyn do not worship the Four Voices as gods, but honor them as parental aspects of their own being. They see the other races — the **Single-Forms** — not with malice, but with gentle pity, as beings who can only hear one part of the world's song.

## Physical Traits

The Waldkyn are not one people, but **eight harmonious expressions** of a single idea, each embodying a different balance of the Four Voices:

| Variant | Balance | Role |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Faerie** | Inspiration & Growth | Swift, curious messengers and scouts |
| **Faun** | Growth & Inspiration | Joyful celebrants, musicians, and guides |
| **Firbolg** | Stability & Growth | Gentle nurturers, farmers, and builders |
| **Fungril** | Growth & Refinement | Quiet mystics and historians |
| **Galapa** | Stability & Refinement | Patient judges, lore-keepers, and mediators |
| **Katari** | Refinement & Inspiration | Graceful, pragmatic protectors and hunters |
| **Ribbet** | Growth & Flowing Change | Adaptable healers and river-wardens |
| **Simiah** | Inspiration & Stability | Clever problem-solvers and tool-makers |

## Culture

Waldkyn lore is preserved in oral traditions called **Tellings** — stories and parables that change slightly with each teller and season, embodying the principle of flowing truth. They speak a dialect of Common known as **Valespeech**, rich with metaphor drawn from the natural world.

Key philosophical terms:
- **The Synthesis** — Core belief in balance and collaborative harmony
- **Kith-Weave** — The sacred bond between all Waldkyn
- **Song-Wrong** — An action or situation fundamentally out of balance
- **Still-Growth** — Patient, deliberate progress

Variant-specific temperaments:
- *Faerie:* "See the small turning."
- *Firbolg:* "A steady hand holds the heaviest pail."
- *Galapa:* "A slow truth is a strong truth."
- *Katari:* "Keep one eye open, even in the sun."
- *Fungril:* "The rot is tomorrow's soil."
- *Ribbet:* "We all sing in the same rain."
- *Simiah:* "A better branch can always be built."
- *Faun:* "Dance the worry out!"

Common greetings: *"Your path is balanced?"* (Response: *"The path holds."*) Farewell: *"May you find shade and water."*

## Sub-Groups

### Quiet Vales Kith (Mainland Traditionalists)

The [[Quiet Vales Kith]] remained in the ancestral [[Whispering Vales]], keepers of the original Synthesis. Their government is a **Triumvirate of Seasons** — the Root-Speaker (past/law), the Trunk-Speaker (present/strength), and the Branch-Speaker (future/diplomacy) — overseeing the **Great Council** of eight Speakers, one from each variant. Their core philosophy is **Naturalism**: technology and large-scale civilization are inherently a "Song-Wrong." They host the annual **Council of the Vales** for all Waldkyn.

Internal factions include the **Stillwood Circle** (isolationists), the **Flowing River School** (interventionists), and the dangerous **Kyn-Clans** (militant purifiers who seek to impose balance by force).

### Chartered Kith (Novaterran Progressives)

The [[Chartered Kith]] believe The Synthesis is not a static state but an active process. Centered in the magitech city of [[Nexus]], they see technology, magic, and urban life as new notes to be woven into the world's song. Governed by the **Weaver's Synod** led by the **First Weaver**, their city is a marvel of living wood integrated with technomantic architecture. Their philosophy is **The Great Weaving** — finding balance between the natural and the artificial.

Key factions: the **Gear-Song Harmonists** (pro-technomancy) and the **Green-Root Accord** (pro-bio-technology, allied with [[Gnomes]]).

### Aerin're Kith (Sanctuary Harmonists)

The [[Aerin're Kith]] rejected formal government entirely, living on the pristine continent of [[Aerin'r']] in small nomadic communities alongside peaceful [[Orkin]] and [[Mirennians]]. Their philosophy is **Stewardship** — protecting what remains of the world's original, untainted harmony. Decisions are reached through pure consensus, sometimes taking weeks of discussion. They are profoundly peaceful but politically inert.

## Other Enclaves

- **The Murk-Tenders of the Fens** — A reclusive and superstitious culture of Waldkyn (primarily Ribbet and Fungril) inhabiting the great swamps of the mainland. Governed by a Shamanic Circle led by a single Bog-Seer who divines the future by reading patterns in mist and marsh bubbles. Deeply territorial and distrustful of outsiders, but possessing ancient knowledge of herbal remedies and poisons found nowhere else.
- **The Crag-Striders of the Grey Peaks** — An isolated enclave of primarily Firbolg and Galapa Waldkyn living in high, inaccessible mountain valleys. Governed by a Council of Elders where leadership is based purely on age and lived experience. Their politics are defined by *Still-Growth* — patient, deliberate progress. Slow to anger and slower to act, but once their course is set, they are as unmovable as the mountains themselves.

## Conceptual Words in Valespeech

- **Greentide** — The overwhelming, chaotic life of a forest (can describe a bustling city)
- **Stone-Still** — A state of deep, patient meditation
- **Flow-State** — The ideal way of being, moving with purpose but without resistance
- **Root-Doubt** — A deep, fundamental uncertainty shaking one's core beliefs
- **Sky-Hunger** — The restless urge to explore
- **Hearth-Warmth** — The feeling of safety, community, and contentment
- **Gear-Song** — The hum of a perfectly functioning city or machine (Chartered Kith term)
- **Pattern-Blind** — A term for someone (usually a Single-Form) who cannot see the interconnectedness of the world
- **Still-Water Dread** — The unsettling feeling of a place where natural cycles have ceased; their word for the [[Silent Blight]]
