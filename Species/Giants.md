# Giants

The **Giants** are a mysterious and possibly extinct species of colossal beings who once inhabited the highest peaks of the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]]. Almost nothing is known of their origins, language, or culture. They were observed only twice in recorded history — once at the dawn of the Second Age, and once at the moment of their disappearance. Their existence remains one of the great unsolved mysteries of [[Vaeloris]].

## Physical Traits

Giants were described by the [[Dwarves|Dwarven]] mining expedition of SA:75 as beings of staggering size — capable of crushing a supply cart with a single finger gesture. Their exact proportions are debated, but contemporary accounts suggest they stood many times the height of a [[Humans|Human]]. Beyond their immense scale, little physical detail survives. No Giant body has ever been recovered.

## First Contact (SA:75)

The only documented encounter with living Giants occurred when a Dwarven mining expedition in the northern peaks broke into a high-altitude valley. The expedition recorded colossal beings tending to **cyclopean ruins** and herding massive woolly beasts. The Giants appeared indifferent to the Dwarves' presence — until a prospector struck a rune-carved stone, prompting a Giant to casually destroy the expedition's supply cart.

The incident prompted the Dwarven High King to issue the decree of **"Wide Berth"**, marking Giant territory as forbidden ground. Scholars theorized that the Giants were the "gardeners" of the [[The First Hands|First Hands']] abandoned mountain sanctuaries — caretakers of a divine age, still performing duties whose purpose had long since passed.

## The Passing of the Giants (SA:1775)

Nearly seventeen centuries after the first sighting, a [[Novaterra|Gearhaven]] survey team ascended the highest peak of the Dragon's Tooth range to install a weather station. They found the massive cyclopean fortress **completely abandoned**. Meals were left on tables. Forges were cold but unweathered. Every sign pointed to sudden, simultaneous departure — but no bodies were found.

The Giants had simply ceased to exist on the material plane.

This event, known as **The Passing of the Giants**, triggered the **Scavenger Rush** — a frenzied scramble by adventurers and nations to loot the Giant strongholds for **Titan-Steel** and ancient artifacts. The ruins yielded remarkable materials but no explanation for the disappearance.

## Theories

Scholars across [[Vaeloris]] have proposed many explanations for the Giants' vanishing:

- **The Recall Theory**: The Giants were summoned back to whatever divine plane created them, their guardianship complete.
- **The Dissolution Theory**: They were never truly physical beings, but echoes of the First Age that finally faded.
- **The Migration Theory**: They departed through some unknown means to a place beyond mortal reach.

No theory has been proven. The cyclopean ruins remain, silent and enormous, awaiting answers that may never come.

## Legacy

The Giants left behind **Titan-Steel** — a metal of extraordinary strength and lightness that became highly sought after during the Age of Flight. Their ruins dot the highest peaks, too large for any mortal species to comfortably inhabit, serving as eerie monuments to a people the world barely knew.
