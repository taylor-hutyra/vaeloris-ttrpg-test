# Gear-Song Harmonists

The dominant political faction within the [[Waldkyn]] city of [[Nexus]], the Gear-Song Harmonists are fascinated by the pure logic and efficiency of [[Gearhaven]]'s Clanks. They advocate for deeper integration with technomancy, believing that the "perfect, predictable rhythm" of machines is a beautiful new note to add to the song of creation.

## Purpose & Goals

The Harmonists champion the [[Chartered Kith]]'s philosophy of [[The Great Weaving]] taken to its fullest expression. They see technology not as a corruption of [[The Synthesis]] but as its evolution—proof that balance can encompass the artificial as well as the natural. Their vision for [[Nexus]] is a city where living wood and technomantic architecture sing in perfect mechanical harmony.

## Structure

The Gear-Song Harmonists hold the majority of seats on the [[Weaver's Synod]], the ruling council of [[Nexus]]. Positions on the Synod are earned through masterworks of synthesis—new forms of magitech, integrated architectural designs, or revolutionary social policies. The faction's Arch-Weavers guide the city's decades-long "Loom-Patterns."

## History

As [[Nexus]] grew into a marvel of living wood integrated with technomantic architecture, the Waldkyn who most embraced this fusion naturally coalesced into the Gear-Song Harmonists. Their term "[[Gear-Song]]"—the hum of a perfectly functioning city or machine—became a defining concept of Chartered Kith culture.
