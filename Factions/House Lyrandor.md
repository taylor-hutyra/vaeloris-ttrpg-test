# House Lyrandor

**House Lyrandor**, known simply as the Wardens, is the traditional house of the **Silent Sentinels** -- the warrior caste who guard the borders of the concealed Elven forest of [[Hannanórë]]. They are loyal to the Verdant Throne and the [[Talanvarë Council]], but their duty is to the forest's physical safety above all else.

House Lyrandor occupies a pragmatic middle ground in Hannanórë's politics. They generally align with [[House Valerion]]'s isolationist stance -- after all, they are the ones who enforce it by patrolling the misty borders. However, their focus on practical defense sometimes puts them at odds with the Council's rigid magical doctrines. When the [[Silent Blight]] threatens, House Lyrandor cares less about philosophical purity and more about whether the border wards still hold.

The Silent Sentinels under House Lyrandor's command are among the finest warriors in [[Vaeloris]], though few outside the forest have ever seen them fight. They are masters of guerrilla warfare within the ancient woodlands, using the terrain and the Mist-Lanterns to devastating effect against any who breach the boundary uninvited.

As pragmatists at heart, House Lyrandor represents the most approachable face of [[Cirhannim]] power for outsiders -- not because they welcome strangers, but because they can be reasoned with when the forest's survival demands it.
