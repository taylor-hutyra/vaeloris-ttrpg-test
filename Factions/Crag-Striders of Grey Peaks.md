# Crag-Striders of Grey Peaks

An isolated enclave of primarily [[Firbolg]] and [[Galapa]] [[Waldkyn]] who live in high, inaccessible mountain valleys of the [[Grey Peaks]]. They have adapted their philosophy of balance to the unforgiving nature of stone, embodying the concept of [[Still-Growth]]—patient, deliberate progress.

## Purpose & Goals

The Crag-Striders seek to live in harmony with the mountains, practicing a philosophy of endurance and patience. They value the wisdom that comes from long life and careful observation, believing that the mountains themselves teach the truest lessons of [[The Synthesis]]: that strength and stillness are not opposites but partners.

## Structure

Governed by a Council of Elders, leadership among the Crag-Striders is based purely on age and lived experience. There are no elections, no contests of skill—only the slow accumulation of years and the wisdom they bring. Their politics are defined by [[Still-Growth]]: slow to anger and slower to act, but once their course is set, they are as unmovable as the mountains themselves.

## History

The Crag-Striders descended from [[Waldkyn]] who sought the quiet solitude of the [[Grey Peaks]], finding in the mountain environment a purer expression of balance between endurance and growth. Over generations, their [[Firbolg]] patience and [[Galapa]] deliberation merged into a culture that moves at the pace of stone—imperceptibly, but with absolute certainty.

## Notable Members

- The Council of Elders — governing body of the eldest and wisest Crag-Striders
