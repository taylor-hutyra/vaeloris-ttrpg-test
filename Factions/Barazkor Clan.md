# Barazkor Clan

The **Barazkor Clan**, known as the **Iron-Gavels**, is the royal clan of [[Kharnzarak]] and the most powerful political entity in the [[N'om-Khazal'on]] (Stone-Sworn) Dwarven hegemony. They rule from the [[Adamant Throne]], their authority absolute but bound by the sacred laws carved into the [[D'om-G'ol]].

## History

The clan's ascendancy began in SA:91 when Thane Korin Barazkor was declared the first High King of a unified [[Kharnzarak]]. He achieved this not through strength alone but by controlling the supply of "Star-Metal" (meteorite iron) found in the upper peaks. The clan has held the throne ever since, their lineage unbroken through millennia of tradition, isolation, and ruthless enforcement of the [[Stone-Lays]].

## Leadership

The current ruler is [[High King Thorgar VII]], who governs with the absolute authority of the [[Adamant Throne]]. He is advised by the [[Stone-Lay Council]], nine clan-lords whose unanimous opposition can delay a royal decree -- forcing the king to justify his decision before the ancestral tombs. The Barazkor Clan's most trusted enforcer is [[Lord Durin Stone-Fist]], a ranking member who carries out the king's will in matters of law and punishment.

## Culture

The Iron-Gavels are fiercely traditionalist and militant, serving as the wardens of [[Kharnzarak]]'s military and its laws. They view any deviation from the [[Stone-Lays]] as a threat to their very existence. The clan embodies the Dwarven value of **Thral'ik** (Endurance) -- the ability to maintain cultural and structural integrity against the pressures of time and change.

## Political Stance

The Barazkor view the [[Thulkor'on]] (Broken-Crafted) as criminals who broke their oaths, a source of shame. They view the [[Elm-korin]] (Flame-Struck) with contempt, as dangerous heretics who abandoned the purity of the stone. The [[Bronr'n Legacy]] reformists are considered a treasonous threat to be rooted out and destroyed.

## Rivals

The [[Farraz'ul Clan]] are their primary internal rivals, using wealth to challenge Barazkor military authority. The [[Gulgrym Clan]] represents a potential populist threat from below. The [[Bronr'n Legacy]] operates as a clandestine danger from within.
