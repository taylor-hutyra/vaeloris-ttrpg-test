# Magocracy

The dominant global political power of the late Second Age and into the Third Age—a mage-ruled authority that emerged from the Coalition of Mages after the Skyfall. The Magocracy wielded the power that pulled down the [[Drakona]] from the sky and positioned themselves as the only force capable of containing the [[Grey Rot]], trading security for liberty across the known world.

## Purpose & Goals

The Magocracy exists to enforce order through arcane supremacy. They retain exclusive jurisdiction over all magical crimes and the regulation of magical education. Their greatest secret is that the [[Grey Rot]] was never truly cured—only caged behind the Pylons of Warding. They maintain power through a combination of genuine capability, manufactured fear, and the suppression of inconvenient truths.

## Structure

Governed by a Ruling Council of Archmages, the Magocracy enforces its will through Inquisitors, magical law, and the threat of weapons like the Arcane Nullifier—which destroyed the fortress-city of [[Vrak'Thul]] as an example to all who would defy them. Their First Decree—"War is inefficient. Order is paramount"—defines their philosophy: peace through absolute control.

## History

After the Skyfall grounded the [[Drakona]], the Coalition of Mages leveraged the resulting [[Grey Rot]] crisis to seize global authority. They declared the Rot contained, took credit for saving the world, and built an authoritarian order enforced by arcane law. The [[Laws of Accord]] codified their power, fixing borders, declaring [[Aerin'r']] a sanctuary, and guaranteeing their jurisdiction over all things magical.
