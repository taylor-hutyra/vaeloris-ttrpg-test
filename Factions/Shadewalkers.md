# Shadewalkers

The **Shadewalkers** are the oldest and most feared espionage guild in [[Vaeloris]], founded in SA:710 by veterans of the [[Salt War]], disgraced diplomats, and spies who realized that information was more profitable than warfare. Operating from the shadows of [[Port Farrick]], they established the legendary Whisper-Market where secrets are bought and sold like grain.

Throughout the centuries, the Shadewalkers have served no nation but themselves. They sold state secrets to both the [[Varnathi]] Republic and the [[Mirennian League]], ensuring neither side gained a decisive advantage. By SA:880, they had infiltrated the Varnathi high courts, and by SA:1920, their Grandmaster Varia the Whisper effectively ruled the Republic from the shadows. Their most devastating act was engineering the Great Betrayal of SA:1950, leaking forged documents that shattered the Republic into the Succession Wars.

The Guild played both sides during the [[Drakona War]], selling Allied military blueprints to the enemy -- a betrayal that triggered the Paranoia Purge and drove them deep underground. After the war, they thrived again during the War of Whispers, when the [[Magocracy]]'s ban on standing armies made espionage the primary tool of diplomacy. Their boldest stroke came in SA:3220 with the Red-Ink Crisis, a deliberate manipulation of Mana-Crystal exchange rates that nearly collapsed the Magocracy's economy. Rather than destroy them, the Magocracy was forced to hire the Shadewalkers to stabilize the banks -- making the espionage guild effectively untouchable as the operators of the global banking system.

In the current era (TA:412), the Shadewalkers remain a shadowy but pervasive force. They are simultaneously criminals, bankers, and kingmakers -- feared by every government yet indispensable to all of them.
