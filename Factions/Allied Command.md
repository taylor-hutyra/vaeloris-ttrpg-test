# Allied Command

The **Allied Command** was the unified military coalition formed in SA:2345 to fight the [[Drakona]] invasion, the greatest existential threat [[Vaeloris]] had ever faced. Born from the Treaty of Survival signed at [[Luminas]], it united the [[Varnathi]] Empire, [[Mirennian League]], [[Federation of City-States]], and the Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]] -- powers that had spent centuries trying to destroy each other.

The early years were desperate. The Drakona's aerial superiority was absolute, and the Alliance suffered catastrophic losses including the Sacking of Mirenn, which destroyed the entire Mirennian fleet. The turning point came when [[Archmage Elaruniel]]'s volatile Mana-Combustion Drive enabled the construction of heavy airships, and the Mirennians -- rebuilding their capital as a sky-port -- became the premier pilots of the war. By SA:2380, the First Air Fleet of 500 ships launched from [[Gearhaven]].

The war ground on for centuries through betrayal, innovation, and stalemate. The [[Shadewalkers]] sold Allied blueprints to the enemy; the [[Cloudborn Corsairs]] raided both sides until pardoned as privateers. Ultimately, the [[Coalition of Mages]] designed the Earth-Anchor Ritual -- the Skyfall -- which grounded all flight magic and sent the Drakona Cloud Cities crashing to earth. The collateral cost was immense: every Allied airship also fell, ending the Age of Flight.

The Allied Command dissolved after the Treaty of the Broken Peaks in SA:2700, but its legacy reshaped the world. The Coalition of Mages, having wielded the power to pull down the sky, transformed into the [[Magocracy]] -- the global authority that would dominate the coming age.
