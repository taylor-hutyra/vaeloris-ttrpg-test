# Cloudborn Corsairs

The **Cloudborn Corsairs** were a renegade squadron of sky pirates who operated during the [[Drakona War]], raiding both the [[Allied Command]] and the [[Drakona]] for supplies and profit. They were founded in SA:2480 when Captain [[Kaelen Vance]], a celebrated [[Mirennian League]] ace known as "Skysplitter," grew disillusioned with the Allied Command's willingness to sacrifice entire crews in hopeless engagements. He led a mutiny, taking his squadron and vanishing into the cloud banks.

The Corsairs established hidden bases on floating sky-islands -- chunks of land ripped skyward by raw magic during the [[The Paroxysm]]. They became the "Third Faction" of the war, beholden to neither side. At the [[Floating Market of Zure]], a massive chunk of airborne land that served as the war's neutral zone, Corsair captains traded alongside Allied spies, Drakona defectors, and [[Shadewalkers]] agents.

In SA:2595, the desperate [[Allied Command]] offered pardons to the Corsairs under the Privateer Accords. Granted legal status in exchange for military service, the sky-privateers proved devastatingly effective at hit-and-run raids against Drakona supply lines. Their freedom-loving culture and daring tactics made them legends of the air war.

The Cloudborn Corsairs met their end in SA:2688 when the Earth-Anchor Ritual -- the Skyfall -- grounded all flight magic. Every airborne vessel, Allied and Corsair alike, plummeted from the sky. The Floating Market of Zure crashed into the sea, and with it vanished the Corsairs' world. Their legacy survives in the romantic tales of sky-pirates told in taverns across [[Vaeloris]], and in rumors of Zure's sunken treasury.
