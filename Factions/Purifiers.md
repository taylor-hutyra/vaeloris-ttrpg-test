# Purifiers

The Purifiers, also known as the **Scions of Unbroken Pattern**, are a zealous minority faction within [[Forest Gnomes|Forest Gnome]] society at [[Mycohollow]]. They represent the most extreme position in the Great Philosophical Divide, believing the world's pattern is not merely frayed but actively corrupted.

## Purpose & Goals

The Purifiers believe the [[Syn-Koinos]] (Shared Pattern) has been torn and tainted by the chaotic actions of the Loud-Walkers---the larger races whose wars, magic, and reckless expansion have introduced jagged disruptions into the fabric of reality. Their sacred duty is to **excise** these flaws and restore the original, unbroken pattern. They view dangerous anomalies not as evil, but as elements that must be surgically eliminated for the good of the world.

## Structure

The Purifiers operate as a secretive sect within [[Mycohollow]]'s broader political structure. Though they participate in the [[Mycelial Council]]'s consensus-based governance, their internal hierarchy is more rigid and militant than typical Gnomish organization. Members undergo rituals of "pattern-cleansing" and are bound by oaths to the faction's cause.

## History

The Purifiers emerged from growing frustration with the [[Balancers]]' moderate approach. As external threats mounted and the wider world's conflicts increasingly disturbed the mycelial network, a faction of [[Forest Gnomes]] concluded that gentle tending was insufficient. The corrupted threads had to be cut away entirely. Their philosophy has made them increasingly dangerous, willing to hunt those they deem anomalies.
