# Appledown Family

The Appledowns are a massive [[Halflings|Halfling]] clan that controls the most fertile farmlands in the [[Burrows]]. They see themselves as the true soul of the Halfling people---deeply traditional, isolationist, and suspicious of outside influence. To an Appledown, a well-tended field and a full pantry are proof of a life well-lived.

## Purpose & Goals

The Appledowns believe Halfling-kind should be entirely self-sufficient. They view the [[Thistlewick Family]]'s trade networks and "foreign ways" as unnecessary at best and corrupting at worst. Their goal is to keep the [[Burrows]] rooted in the land: growing their own food, brewing their own ale, and minding their own business. The Tallfolk and their "Big Scramble" are someone else's problem.

## Structure

The family is organized around land and lineage. The eldest Appledowns---those who control the largest and most productive farms---hold the most sway. Family elders regularly serve on town councils and are frequently elected Mayor, a role they accept as a burdensome civic duty. Their influence over the Commonwealth's food supply gives them enormous practical power.

## History

The Appledowns have been farming since the founding of the first [[Burrows]]. Their roots go as deep as any in Halfling society, and they never let anyone forget it. As the [[Thistlewick Family]] grew wealthy through trade and the [[Rumblkin Family]] expanded into mining, the Appledowns doubled down on their agrarian identity. They represent the conservative backbone of the [[Hearthfolk Commonwealth]]---resistant to change but utterly dependable.
