# Wandering Kin

The **Wandering Kin** are a loose confederation of exiled [[Thulkorûn]] and [[Elm-korin]] Dwarves who banded together in SA:855 to survive outside the sealed halls of [[Kharnzarak]]. United by the sacred Wagon-Vow -- an oath of mutual aid that supersedes local laws -- they formed a "State without Borders," operating as caravans that crisscross the mainland and beyond.

The Thulkorûn, exiled criminals and oath-breakers, provided muscle and protection, while the Elm-korin, visionary magic-smiths banished for their arcane experiments, provided goods and repairs of a quality no human artisan could match. When the [[Bronrûn Legacy]] were blamed for the Earthwake of SA:1200 and fled Kharnzarak, thousands used the Wandering Kin network to reach the surface and eventually migrate to [[Novaterra]].

During the Mage-Purge Wars, the Wandering Kin became indispensable intermediaries for hidden mages. They developed "Rune-Boxes" -- lead-lined containers that could conceal magical artifacts from the Republic's [[Legio Null]] Inquisitors -- and served as the primary smuggling network for arcane contraband on the mainland. After the legalization of magic in SA:1715, the Kin began openly trading runic items, but their underground connections remained invaluable.

In the current era, the Wandering Kin remain the only visible face of Dwarvenkind for much of the world. They serve as traders, craftspeople, and quiet go-betweens, their caravans carrying not only goods but the living memory of a people denied their mountain home.
