# Spicers' Guild

The Spicers' Guild is the closest thing [[Halflings|Halfling]] Wayfinders have to a government. On the surface, it operates as a legitimate merchant guild controlling the import of exotic spices and luxury goods in cities like [[Aurumport]] and [[M'ren'r']]. In reality, it is a vast intelligence network, information brokerage, and mutual aid society for the Wayfinder community.

## Purpose & Goals

The Guild serves two purposes. Publicly, it is a successful trading house dealing in spices and fine goods. Privately, it is the safety net and information backbone of every [[Halflings|Halfling]] Wayfinder in the Tallfolk world. Need a secret message delivered without a trace? Need to know which noble is about to fall from favor? The Spicers' Guild can arrange it---for a price. Their services are expensive, but their discretion is legendary.

## Structure

The Guild maintains a dual leadership:

- **The Guildmaster** --- the public face, a wealthy and respectable merchant who manages legitimate trade operations
- **The Whisper-Master** --- the true leader, a shadowy figure who controls the flow of information, arranges "extra-legal" contracts, and ensures the safety of the Wayfinder community

Operatives are embedded in taverns, kitchens, and market stalls across major cities, using their natural Halfling stealth and disarming charm to gather intelligence.

## History

The Spicers' Guild began as a genuine trading cooperative, born from Wayfinder Halflings pooling resources to survive in Tallfolk cities. Over time, the network's reach and the Halflings' talent for going unnoticed transformed it into something far more powerful. Today, the Guild acts as a neutral third party in the "Big Scramble" of Tallfolk politics---essential information brokers whose neutrality is their greatest asset.
