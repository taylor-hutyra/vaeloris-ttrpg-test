# Azure Hand

The **Azure Hand** is a secretive and influential [[Merchant House]] within the [[Mirennian League]] that profits from smuggling, espionage, and the "gray market" trade of magical artifacts. Operating from the shadows of Mirennian territory, they are the hidden hand that shapes events the [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] cannot see coming.

## Operations

The Azure Hand specializes in enterprises that skirt or openly defy the law. Their agents traffic in smuggled goods, stolen intelligence, and magical contraband -- items whose trade is restricted by the [[Magocracy]] or the [[Admiralty Council]]. They maintain a network of informants in every major port, from the docks of [[Port Farrick]] to the hidden coves of the [[Fractured Isles]].

## Relationship with the Pirates

The Azure Hand maintains a complex, often collaborative relationship with the [[Pirate Confederacy]]. They use the pirate lords to destabilize their [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] rivals, funding raids on Syndicate shipping while keeping their own vessels safe through backroom agreements. This arrangement gives them plausible deniability while reaping the profits of chaos.

## History

The Azure Hand rose to prominence during the [[Guild Wars]] of SA:1100, when they exposed evidence of embezzlement within the [[Golden Compass Syndicate]], shattering its monopoly and plunging the [[Mirennian League]] into fifty years of assassinations, sabotage, and street battles between private house militias. The chaos served the Azure Hand's interests perfectly, allowing them to expand their smuggling networks while their rival burned.

## Intelligence Network

Beyond simple smuggling, the Azure Hand operates one of the most effective espionage networks on the mainland. Their agents gather intelligence on trade routes, political dealings, and military movements, selling secrets to the highest bidder. Contacts within this faction are invaluable for anyone navigating the dangerous waters of Mirennian politics.

## Rivals

The [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] views the Azure Hand as a criminal parasite. The [[Magocracy]] would suppress their magical artifact trade if they could find it. The [[Sea-Whisperers' Guild]] distrusts their willingness to sell [[Wind-Shaping]] secrets to outsiders.
