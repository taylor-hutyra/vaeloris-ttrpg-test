# Kyn-Clans

A dangerous, extremist faction of [[Waldkyn]] living on the fringes of the [[Whispering Vales]]. The Kyn-Clans believe that balance must be imposed by force—that the world's dissonance has grown so severe that only violent pruning can restore harmony. They are hostile to expanding human nations like the [[Varnathi]] and view infiltrated Waldkyn mystics not as victims but as a corruption that must be purged by fire.

## Purpose & Goals

Where the [[Stillwood Circle]] seeks to wall off the Vales and the [[Flowing River School]] seeks to heal the world through engagement, the Kyn-Clans reject both paths as weak. They seek to "prune the dissonant branches" of the world through militant action. Their targets include encroaching [[Single-Forms]], corrupted Waldkyn, and anything they deem a Song-Wrong beyond redemption.

## Structure

The Kyn-Clans operate as loosely organized war-bands on the fringes of Vales society, outside the formal authority of the [[Great Council of the Vales]]. They lack a single leader, instead following charismatic war-chiefs who prove themselves in raids and skirmishes. Their decentralized structure makes them difficult to suppress.

## History

Born from frustration with both isolationism and diplomacy, the Kyn-Clans represent the Waldkyn's shadow—the violent answer to a world that refuses to find balance peacefully. They are a dangerous third party in Vales politics, capable of complicating any conflict between the mainstream factions or with outside visitors.
