# Brinepipers of Salty Coast

The Brinepipers are a hardy community of [[Halflings]] who have adapted to coastal life on [[Novaterra]], becoming renowned fishers, salt-miners, and brewers of a famously potent seaweed grog. They represent the Halfling talent for making any environment feel like home---even a windswept, salt-crusted shoreline.

## Purpose & Goals

The Brinepipers are a self-sufficient coastal community whose goals are refreshingly simple: catch fish, mine salt, brew grog, and keep the sea from washing away their homes. Their practical, results-oriented culture extends even to leadership selection. They maintain a tense but profitable relationship with the [[Pirate Confederacy]], supplying food in exchange for protection from the [[Mirennian League]]'s taxes.

## Structure

The Brinepipers are governed by a simple **Mayoralty** with a uniquely Halfling twist: the Mayor is not chosen by vote, but by whoever brings in the **largest fish** during the annual **Brine-Tide Festival**. This practical, results-based approach to leadership perfectly encapsulates their culture---authority belongs to the one who can demonstrably provide for the community.

## History

When [[Halflings]] first arrived on [[Novaterra]]'s shores, most moved inland toward familiar farmland. The Brinepipers' ancestors were the stubborn few who looked at the crashing waves and saw opportunity rather than hardship. They learned to fish the [[Novaterra]] waters, discovered rich salt deposits along the coast, and turned seaweed into a grog that could strip paint from a hull. Their relationship with the [[Pirate Confederacy]]---food for protection---has made them a small but strategically important community on the coast.
