# Open Palm Movement

The **Open Palm Movement**, known as **The Reformists**, is the progressive faction within the [[Pentarchy of Vernath]]. Though smaller than either the [[Crimson Banner]] or the [[Marble Hand]], they serve as a persistent political conscience, typically aligned with the [[Consul of the People]].

## Membership

The Open Palm draws support from civic leaders, community organizers, educators, and sympathetic members of the [[Citizenry]] who interact daily with the [[Assimilated]] populations. Some younger members of the [[Pillar Houses]], disillusioned with their elders' rigidity, quietly support the movement.

## Ideology

The Reformists are idealists who argue that [[Vernath]]'s true strength lies in its unity, not its military might. They advocate for genuine equality for the [[Assimilated]] -- primarily [[Orkin]] citizens who hold legal rights but face systemic prejudice and confinement to specific quarters in major cities. They believe the Varnathi caste system is a relic that weakens the nation from within.

## Political Position

The Open Palm is a political thorn in the side of both the Hawks and the Traditionalists. They use their alignment with the [[Consul of the People]] to champion domestic spending, civic works, and food distribution over military budgets and aristocratic privilege. Their populist message resonates with the common [[Citizenry]], though the faction lacks the institutional power of its rivals.

## Cultural Roots

The movement draws inspiration from the Varnathi creation myth's emphasis on "fragments of divine will" shared by all mortals, arguing that this sacred potential is not limited to the Varnathi alone. They echo the human core belief that "identity is shaped, not given" and that "the world is still becoming, and so are we."

## Rivals

Both the [[Crimson Banner]] and the [[Marble Hand]] view the Open Palm as naive at best and treasonous at worst. The Hawks see integration as diluting Varnathi strength, while the Traditionalists see it as upending the social order that has sustained [[Vernath]] for centuries.
