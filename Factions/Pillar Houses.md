# Pillar Houses

The **Pillar Houses** are the old aristocratic families of [[Varnathi]] society, tracing their lineage back to the founding of the [[Varnathi Empire]] in SA:381. Named for the Consul of Pillars -- the Pentarchy position that represents their interests -- these families have dominated the Senate and shaped Varnathi politics for over two thousand years.

The Pillar Houses embody the Varnathi values of honor, legacy, and self-discipline. They view themselves as the custodians of the First Compact, the original constitution laid down by [[Vardas the Elder]], and as the philosophical guardians of a society built on duty and order. Their caste sits at the top of the Varnathi social hierarchy, above the [[Crimson Guard]] military class, the Citizenry of common merchants and farmers, and the Assimilated non-human populations.

Politically, the Pillar Houses form the core of the [[Marble Hand]] faction -- the dominant traditionalist bloc in the Senate that controls the Consulships of Pillars and Scrolls. They believe stability and strict adherence to the [[Magocracy]]'s peace are paramount, and they use their influence to block the military expansionism of the [[Crimson Banner]] hawks and the social reforms of the [[Open Palm Movement]]. Their conservatism is both their strength and their vulnerability: it has preserved Varnathi institutions through centuries of crisis, but it also keeps the state rigid and resistant to change.

As of TA:412, the Pillar Houses find themselves caught between General Valerius's popular militarism and the growing demands of the common people for genuine representation. Their grip on the Senate remains firm, but the deadlock within the Pentarchy threatens to crack the very order they have spent millennia preserving.
