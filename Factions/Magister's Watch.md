# Magister's Watch

The **Magister's Watch** is the global police force of the [[Magocracy]], established in SA:2866 by the First Decree -- "War is inefficient. Order is paramount." Composed of trained battle-mages answering directly to the Ruling Council, the Watch enforces the [[Laws of Accord]] across all nations, policing magical crimes, preventing the formation of illegal standing armies, and enforcing the ban on Aeromancy (flight magic) that was imposed after the [[Skyfall]].

The Watch's authority is vast and their methods are feared. Practitioners of forbidden magic face "Stilling" -- a lobotomy of the magical center of the brain -- and the Watch has the power to operate within any sovereign nation without local permission. During the War of Whispers (SA:2900), the Watch's Inquisitors fought a decades-long shadow war against the [[Shadewalkers]], who had entered a golden age of espionage in the vacuum left by the ban on standing armies.

In a grim irony, the Watch serves a similar function to the [[Legio Null]] of centuries past -- using force to regulate who may practice magic and how. Where the Legio Null sought to eliminate all mages, the Watch seeks to ensure only Magocracy-sanctioned mages operate freely. The methods differ, but the underlying principle -- that unchecked magical power is an existential threat -- remains the same.

As of TA:412, the Magister's Watch remains the most visible arm of the Magocracy's authority. Their patrols and inspections are a constant reminder that the peace of the Third Age is maintained not by goodwill, but by the threat of overwhelming arcane force.
