# Hollowkin Council

The joint governing body of the [[Hollowkin Settlement]] on [[Aerin'r']], established through the historic Declaration of Hollowkin Rights. This treaty between [[Halflings]] and [[Gnomes]] created a shared society of "Burrows and Hollows" that famously baffled human tax collectors with its complete lack of formal hierarchy.

## Purpose & Goals

The Hollowkin Council exists to manage the affairs of a community that defies conventional political categories. Decisions are made through consensus between [[Halflings|Halfling]] and [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] representatives, with no single leader or ruling class. The community's goals are simple: live well, share freely, and confuse anyone who tries to impose external order on their way of life.

## Structure

The Council has no permanent officers or fixed positions. Representatives are chosen informally based on expertise relevant to the matter at hand—a [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] herbalist might lead discussions on agriculture while a [[Halflings|Halfling]] elder mediates property disputes. This fluid, egalitarian structure is both the Council's greatest strength and its most bewildering feature to outsiders accustomed to hierarchy.

## History

In SA:980, [[Varnathi|Human]] expansion pushed into the fertile western river valleys, driving [[Halflings|Halfling]] clans into [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] territories. Rather than fight, the [[Forest Gnomes]] revealed themselves, and the two peoples forged the Declaration of Hollowkin Rights—a treaty of shared habitation that has endured for over two millennia.
