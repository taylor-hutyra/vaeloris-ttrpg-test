# Marble Hand

The **Marble Hand**, known as **The Traditionalists**, is the dominant conservative faction within the [[Senate]] of [[Vernath]]. They control the [[Consul of Pillars]] and the [[Consul of Scrolls]], giving them significant leverage over internal affairs and foreign relations within the [[Pentarchy]].

## Membership

The Marble Hand draws its strength from the old aristocratic families of the [[Pillar Houses]], the diplomatic corps, and the established bureaucracy of [[Vernath]]. Their members are the keepers of tradition, tracing their lineage and authority back through centuries of Varnathi governance.

## Ideology

The Traditionalists believe that stability and strict adherence to the [[Magocracy]]'s peace are paramount. They view the [[Laws of Accord]] not as chains but as the foundation upon which Varnathi prosperity was built. Their philosophy echoes the old Varnathi saying: "A crooked vault falls silent" -- dishonor and reckless ambition bring only ruin.

## Political Position

The Marble Hand is the old guard of [[Vernath]], wielding their control of the Consulships of Pillars and Scrolls to block the [[Crimson Banner]]'s militaristic agenda. They are the primary obstacle to [[General Valerius]]'s push for increased military funding and aggressive expansion. The [[Consul of Coin]] and [[Consul of the People]] are often caught between the Marble Hand and the Hawks.

## Rivals

The Marble Hand views the [[Crimson Banner]] as reckless warmongers who would provoke the [[Magocracy]] into punishing [[Vernath]]. They consider the [[Open Palm Movement]] a well-meaning but destabilizing force that threatens the social order of the [[Pillar Houses]] and the caste system that underpins Varnathi society.
