# Coalition of Mages

The **Coalition of Mages** was a secret organization of the world's most powerful Archmages, formed in SA:2305 at the Luminas Conclave to prepare for the inevitable [[Drakona]] invasion. While the national governments were paralyzed by internal politics, mages from the [[Grand University]], [[Gearhaven]], and the [[Federation of City-States]] united across borders to stockpile mana crystals and research anti-aerial combat.

The Coalition operated as a "Deep State" for decades before the war began, recruiting brilliant but controversial scholars like [[Archmage Elaruniel]], who had been censured for her aggressive theories about breaking reality. When the Drakona invasion struck in SA:2341, the Coalition's preparations proved invaluable. They became the arcane arm of the [[Allied Command]], providing the magical firepower that conventional armies could not.

Their most consequential act was authorizing Operation Icarus in SA:2680 -- a suicide reconnaissance mission into the Drakona Cloud City of Aethelgard. The intelligence gathered revealed that the cities were held aloft by continuous aeromancy rituals. This knowledge led to the Coalition's design of the Earth-Anchor Ritual, executed in SA:2688, which grounded all flight magic and sent the Cloud Cities crashing to earth. The moral weight of this act -- effectively a genocide of Drakona civilians -- haunted many of its members.

After the war's end in SA:2700, the Coalition leveraged its unmatched power into political authority. Having wielded the force that pulled down the sky, they rebranded as the [[Magocracy]] in SA:2701 -- transitioning from a military alliance into the global government that would dominate the world for centuries to come.
