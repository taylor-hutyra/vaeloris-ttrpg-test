# House Silvanus

**House Silvanus**, known as the Sunken-Leaf Faction, is a younger and less influential noble house within the concealed Elven forest of [[Hannanórë]]. They represent the quiet dissent within [[Cirhannim]] society -- those who secretly believe that Hannanórë's perfect isolation has become a slow, silent decay rather than the preservation it claims to be.

Unlike the dominant [[House Valerion]], House Silvanus argues that the [[Silent Blight]] threatening the forest is a dissonance that cannot be ignored by simply sealing the borders tighter. They believe new knowledge is needed to fight it -- knowledge that can only come from engaging with the outside world. To this end, they maintain forbidden contact with scholars in [[Mórenórë]], the free city of intellectual collaboration, making them secret allies of the [[Aen'valar]] progressive philosophy.

House Silvanus operates carefully within the rigid political structures of Hannanórë. Open defiance of the [[Talanvarë Council]] would result in exile or worse, so their dissent takes the form of whispered conversations, hidden correspondence, and the quiet cultivation of sympathizers among the younger generations of Cirhannim who have begun to question whether tradition alone can save their dying forest.

For outsiders seeking aid within Hannanórë, House Silvanus represents a potential lifeline -- the one faction willing to consider that the answers to the forest's troubles might lie beyond the mist.
