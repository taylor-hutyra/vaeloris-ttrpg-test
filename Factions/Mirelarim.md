# Mirelarim

The Mirelarim are an ancient offshoot of [[Elves]] who followed the Harmony of [[Mirelaris]] (Gem/Depth) into the ocean's abyss. They are a reclusive, bio-luminescent people living in magnificent cities of magically-sculpted coral deep beneath the waves of the [[Azure Deeps]]. They are both a distinct sub-species and a sovereign political entity.

## Purpose & Goals

The Mirelarim are fiercely territorial and view the surface world with deep suspicion, especially the ship-faring races. Their primary purpose is the preservation of their undersea domain and the ancient knowledge it contains---knowledge of the world as it was before [[The Paroxysm]], which could prove vital to scholars studying the current age.

## Structure

The Mirelarim are governed as a **Coral Monarchy**, led by a ruler who can commune with the deep ocean's "song." This monarch serves as both spiritual and political authority, interpreting the resonance of the depths to guide their people. Their society is insular and hierarchical, built around the symbiotic relationship between the Mirelarim and the living coral cities they inhabit.

## History

In the earliest ages, a group of [[Elves]] felt the call of [[Mirelaris]]---the Harmony of hidden beauty and inner worth---more strongly than any other tone. They descended into the ocean, adapting over millennia into the bio-luminescent deep-dwellers known today. Their isolation has preserved knowledge lost to every surface civilization.
