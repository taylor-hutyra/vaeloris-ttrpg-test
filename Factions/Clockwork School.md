# Clockwork School

The Clockwork School is the dominant orthodox philosophy of the [[Rock Gnomes]] in [[Lithos]], the great subterranean metropolis within the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]]. They see the universe as a vast, intricate timepiece and dedicate themselves to the perfection of pure, silent mechanics.

## Purpose & Goals

The Clockwork School believes that the world is a grand machine of stone and energy, and that their sacred duty is to listen for the grinding of the gears and keep the machine running smoothly. They pursue the perfection of clockwork mechanisms, gem-faceting, metallurgy, and geothermal engineering with obsessive precision. A gear that does not turn smoothly is a flaw to be corrected or replaced.

## Structure

The school operates through [[Lithos]]'s **Technocratic Guild-Conclave**, a council of nine masters where each member is the undisputed expert of their respective craft (Master Gem-Facer, Chief Chronometrist, etc.). Seats are held for life or until a peer demonstrably surpasses the master's skill. The **Deep Warden** leads the Conclave, listening to the grinding of tectonic plates and the hum of leylines to direct their efforts.

## History

The Clockwork School represents the original and orthodox approach to [[Rock Gnomes|Rock Gnome]] craft. Since the founding of [[Lithos]], the dominant philosophy has been one of precision and silent perfection. They respect the [[N'nm-Khazal'n]] [[Dwarves]] for their mastery of structure but find their methods loud and imprecise. The emergence of the [[Resonance School]]---which incorporates harmonic and quasi-magical elements---is viewed as a dangerous deviation from pure engineering.
