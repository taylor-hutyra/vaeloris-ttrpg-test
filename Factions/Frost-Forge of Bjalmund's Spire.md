# Frost-Forge of Bjalmund's Spire

The **Frost-Forge of Bjalmund's Spire**, known as the **Zelg'ul-Kharn** (The Echo-Clan), is a fortress-monastery of [[N'om-Khazal'on]] Dwarves isolated high in the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]]. Cut off from [[Kharnzarak]] centuries ago, they have developed a unique culture that blends traditional Dwarven ancestor-worship with a deep, pragmatic reverence for the fury of the mountain.

## The Spire

[[Bjalmund's Spire]] is named for [[Bjalmund]] the Shieldbearer, the minor deity whose avatar sacrificed itself to trap the [[Echo-Lord]]'s essence during the battle at the Scar in SA:380. The fortress-monastery was built at the site where the mountain groaned loudest in response to Bjalmund's sacrifice -- a place the Dwarves believe still resonates with divine echo.

## Leadership

The Frost-Forge is led by a **Runespeaker**, a shamanic leader who interprets the will of the peaks through the cracking of ice, the groaning of stone, and the patterns of frost on the high walls. This spiritual authority is unlike anything found in [[Kharnzarak]]'s rigid political hierarchy -- it is earned through sensitivity to the mountain's voice, not through birthright or craft mastery.

## Culture

The Echo-Clan lives an ascetic existence defined by endurance, meditation, and communion with the mountain. They practice ancient Dwarven traditions that [[Kharnzarak]] has formalized into rigid law, keeping them alive as living spiritual practices. Their forges burn cold -- using the mountain's own geothermal vents rather than conventional fuel -- and their smithing techniques produce metal tempered by ice and pressure rather than flame.

## Isolation

Separated from [[Kharnzarak]] by impassable terrain and centuries of silence, the Zelg'ul-Kharn have evolved independently. They know nothing of the [[Sealing of Kharnzarak]] or the political machinations of the [[Barazkor Clan]]. To the mountain kingdoms, the Echo-Clan is a legend -- Dwarves who chose the wind and ice over the deep halls.

## Strategic Significance

The Frost-Forge guards ancient mountain sanctuaries that may contain relics from the [[First Age]] and secrets of the [[Four Pillars of Craft]]. Their knowledge of the high peaks and their unique frost-smithing techniques could make them vital allies -- or formidable obstacles -- for anyone seeking the treasures of the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]].
