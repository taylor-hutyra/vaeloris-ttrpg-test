# Bloodied Fist Clans

The majority faction among the [[Varr-Darg]]—the traditional [[Orkin]] war-clans of the [[Barren Hills]]. The Bloodied Fist Clans believe the only path to reclamation of lost honor and land is through war. They are aggressive, expansionist, and hold a deep-seated hatred for the [[Varnathi]] who drove them into the wastelands.

## Purpose & Goals

The Bloodied Fist seek nothing less than the reconquest of ancestral [[Orkin]] territory. They are the clans most susceptible to [[Kael'Zorai]]'s call, interpreting the [[Silent Blight]] as the return of [[Grom-Kain]]—their creator come to lead them to righteous victory. Their concept of [[Trak]] (endurance) is expressed through relentless aggression and refusal to submit.

## Structure

The Bloodied Fist Clans follow the traditional Varr-Darg diarchy: each clan is led by a [[Gral-Kor]] (Stone-Hand, the warlord, always a [[Gral]]) and a [[Skorn-Kli]] (Sharp-Eye, the strategist, usually a [[Skorn]]). The clans are fiercely independent but unite under the shared banner of grievance and the promise of war. The clan is everything—an individual's worth is measured by their contribution to survival.

## History

Centuries of conflict with expanding [[Varnathi]] nations forged the Varr-Darg into hardened warriors, and the Bloodied Fist rose as the natural expression of that rage. They hold the most fervent belief in the return of [[Grom-Kain]] and represent the dominant political force in [[Barren Hills]] politics, pushing always toward open war.
