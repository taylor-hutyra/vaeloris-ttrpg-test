# Iron-Grit Syndicate

The largest and most powerful [[Orkin]] Hold on [[Novaterra]], the Iron-Grit Syndicate controls vast mining operations and supplies raw materials to [[Gearhaven]] and the other [[Free Peoples]]. They are pragmatic capitalists who have largely abandoned the old traditions in favor of profit, redefining [[Trak]] (endurance) as economic dominance.

## Purpose & Goals

The Iron-Grit Syndicate exists to mine, refine, and sell. They control the extraction of ore, gems, and other mineral resources across [[Novaterra]]'s rugged interior, feeding the insatiable demand of [[Gearhaven]]'s technomantic industries. Their concept of endurance is measured in balance sheets and market share rather than battlefield victories.

## Structure

Organized as a corporate Hold rather than a traditional clan, the Syndicate is led by a Hold-Thane—a CEO-chieftain whose position is earned through business acumen, strategic alliances, and, when necessary, brutal corporate takeovers. The Hold-Thane is advised by a Board of Captains: heads of the mining division, mercenary security, and prospecting teams.

## History

Descended from [[Orkin]] who took the [[Mirennian Passage]] to [[Novaterra]], the Syndicate's founders recognized that the new continent's wealth lay beneath its surface. They transformed the traditional clan structure into a corporate hierarchy, proving that [[Orkin]] discipline and toughness could be as effective in commerce as in combat.

## Notable Members

- The Hold-Thane — CEO-chieftain of the Syndicate
- Various Captains of the Board overseeing mining, security, and prospecting divisions
