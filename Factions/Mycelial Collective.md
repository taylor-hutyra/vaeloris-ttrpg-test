# Mycelial Collective

The **Mycelial Collective** is the governing body and intelligence network of the [[Forest Gnomes]] of the mainland, centered in [[Mycohollow]] -- a city woven into a gargantuan, semi-sentient fungal grove. In SA:2100, the Forest Gnomes performed their most audacious feat of the Quiet Work: they modified the mycelial network of the forests to act as a continental vibration-sensor, capable of tracking troop movements anywhere there were roots.

The "Myco-Whisperers" -- the spiritual leaders who commune most deeply with the grove -- became the world's premier intelligence agency. They sold this data to the highest bidder, often the [[Lothari]] or Elves, to maintain the balance of power across [[Vaeloris]]. Their government is as organic as their city: the Mycelial Council is composed not of elected officials but of Gnomes who have achieved the deepest symbiotic link with the grove, led by twin speakers -- the Myco-Whisperer (spiritual leader) and the Geo-Weaver (lead artisan).

The Collective's finest hour came during the [[Grey Rot]] crisis after the [[Skyfall]]. In SA:2710, while Human mages faltered, Gnomes of the Collective discovered that the Rot was a fungal-magical hybrid. They developed "Spore-Masks" and alchemical sprays that could slow the spread, becoming the frontline medics of the crisis. When the [[Magocracy]] proposed burning the Dwarven city of Iron-Beach to stop an outbreak, the Collective's medics defied them and cured the plague, proving there were alternatives to the Magocracy's scorched-earth approach.

Within the Collective, three philosophical factions debate the correct approach to the Quiet Work: the [[Balancers]] (majority), who favor gentle guidance; the [[Purifiers]], who seek to excise corruption from the world's pattern; and the [[Integrators]], who believe the pattern must evolve to incorporate new elements.
