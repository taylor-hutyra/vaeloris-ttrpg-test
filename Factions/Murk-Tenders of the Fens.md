# Murk-Tenders of the Fens

A reclusive and highly superstitious culture of [[Waldkyn]]—primarily [[Ribbet]] and [[Fungril]]—who inhabit the great swamps of [[The Fens]] on the mainland. They have adapted to a life of secrecy and subtle influence, guarding ancient knowledge found nowhere else in the world.

## Purpose & Goals

The Murk-Tenders serve as guardians and students of the swamplands. They possess unrivaled knowledge of herbal remedies and poisons, secrets accumulated over generations of isolation. Their [[Bog-Seer]] divines the future by reading patterns in mist and marsh bubbles, guiding the community through a form of mysticism unique among [[Waldkyn]].

## Structure

The Murk-Tenders are governed by a Shamanic Circle led by a single [[Bog-Seer]]—a powerful mystic who serves as both spiritual leader and political authority. They are deeply territorial and view all outsiders, including other [[Waldkyn]], with distrust. Their society is organized around the rhythms of the swamp: tidal patterns, seasonal floods, and the growth cycles of rare marsh plants.

## History

At some point in the distant past, a group of [[Ribbet]] and [[Fungril]] [[Waldkyn]] settled in [[The Fens]], drawn to the swamp's complex ecosystem as an expression of [[The Synthesis]] in its rawest form. Over generations, they developed their own distinct culture, becoming increasingly isolated and suspicious of the outside world while accumulating a vast pharmacological knowledge.

## Notable Members
