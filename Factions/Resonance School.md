# Resonance School

The Resonance School is a smaller, heterodox faction within [[Rock Gnomes|Rock Gnome]] society at [[Lithos]]. Considered heretics by the orthodox [[Clockwork School]], they study the harmonic properties of crystals and metals, believing that true perfection lies not in silent mechanics but in creating perfectly tuned resonances that can influence magic and reality itself.

## Purpose & Goals

The Resonance School seeks to bridge the gap between engineering and magic. They believe that crystals and metals are not merely inert materials to be shaped, but contain **living echoes**---harmonic signatures that, when properly tuned, can interact with magical forces and even alter reality. Their work aims to create instruments and devices that resonate with the fundamental frequencies of the world.

## Structure

The school operates as a secretive guild-within-a-guild inside [[Lithos]]'s broader craft structure. They hold no seats on the Guild-Conclave and meet in private workshops where their experiments with harmonic resonance can proceed without orthodox interference. Their internal hierarchy is based on the depth of one's attunement to crystal harmonics.

## History

The Resonance School was born when a faction of [[Rock Gnomes|Rock Gnome]] gem-cutters and metallurgists noticed that certain crystal configurations produced not just beautiful light refractions, but detectable magical effects. Their investigation into these phenomena was condemned by the [[Clockwork School]] as dangerously close to the "chaotic" magic of the Loud-Walkers. Undeterred, the heretics continued their research in secret, convinced that the universe's machinery is not silent at all---it sings.
