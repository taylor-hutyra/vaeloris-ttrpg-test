# Thistlewick Family

The Thistlewicks are a sprawling, influential [[Halflings|Halfling]] merchant family known for their shrewd business sense and extensive trade networks that connect the [[Burrows]] to the outside world. They are seen as worldly and a bit too ambitious by other Halflings, but their imports of spices, fine fabrics, and news are indispensable to Commonwealth life.

## Purpose & Goals

The Thistlewicks exist at the intersection of Halfling comfort and foreign commerce. They believe that the [[Burrows]] thrive best when connected to the wider world---not through politics or war, but through mutually profitable trade. Their caravans and contacts reach into [[Tallfolk]] cities across [[Vaeloris]], bringing back exotic luxuries and valuable information.

## Structure

The family operates as an extended mercantile clan, with branches in multiple [[Burrows]] and trade contacts in major cities. Family seniority and business acumen determine internal rank. The Thistlewicks maintain a veneer of respectable merchantry, though their connections to the [[Spicers' Guild]] suggest deeper involvement in information networks.

## History

The Thistlewicks rose from humble roots to become one of the three most powerful families in the [[Hearthfolk Commonwealth]]. While the [[Appledown Family]] controlled the best farmland and the [[Rumblkin Family]] held the mines, the Thistlewicks carved out their niche as the essential link to the outside world. Other Halflings may grumble about their "foreign ways," but no one turns down a Thistlewick shipment of Aethelas cinnamon.
