# Iron-Bound

The **Iron-Bound** are the elite heavy infantry corps of the [[Varnathi Empire]] and its successor states, created in SA:395 after the Standardization of Steel. Using the "Blast-Furnace" technique perfected with the aid of [[Thulkorûn]] (exiled Dwarf) consultants, Varnathi smiths achieved the mass production of standardized steel armor and weapons -- making the Iron-Bound the most heavily armed ground force on the continent.

The Iron-Bound were the spearhead of the Empire's ambitions. They shattered disorganized [[Orkin]] warbands during the Southern Conquests of SA:410, establishing the fortress-city of [[Castellum]] as their primary base. They fought in the Salt War against the [[Mirennian League]], endured the brutal Siege of Solis, and were deployed in the War of the Barren Hills -- the Empire's attempt to exterminate the Orkin for iron and coal resources.

Their rigid phalanx tactics and steel discipline made the Iron-Bound nearly unbeatable in conventional warfare for centuries. It was only the Battle of Crimson Field in SA:1695, where 3,000 battle-mages annihilated a 50,000-strong Republican army, that proved the Age of Steel was over and magic had changed warfare forever.

The Iron-Bound tradition endures in the current era as the foundation of the [[Crimson Guard]], the elite military class of the [[Varnathi]] Pentarchy. Though the name has changed, the ethos of steel discipline and duty to the state remains the heart of Varnathi martial culture.
