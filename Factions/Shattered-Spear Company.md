# Shattered-Spear Company

A legendary [[Orkin]] mercenary Hold on [[Novaterra]], known for its discipline and effectiveness. The Shattered-Spear Company takes contracts from any of the [[Free Peoples]], their loyalty sold to the highest bidder. They have earned a reputation as the finest professional soldiers in the new continent.

## Purpose & Goals

The Company exists to fight, and to fight well. Unlike the [[Bloodied Fist Clans]] who wage war for honor and land, the Shattered-Spear wage war for coin and contract. Their discipline is legendary—once a contract is signed, they fulfill it without question. This reliability has made them the most sought-after mercenary force on [[Novaterra]].

## Structure

Organized as a mercenary Hold, the Company is led by a Hold-Thane whose authority is absolute in matters of contract and combat. Below the Thane, a strict hierarchy of captains, sergeants, and soldiers mirrors professional military organization. Recruitment is selective: every prospective member must survive a brutal proving period that tests both [[Gral]] strength and [[Skorn]] cunning.

## History

Founded by [[Orkin]] veterans who arrived on [[Novaterra]] via the [[Mirennian Passage]], the Shattered-Spear discovered that their martial skills were in high demand among the [[Free Peoples]]. Rather than fight for territory, they built a reputation for professional excellence, transforming the [[Orkin]] tradition of the war-band into a disciplined corporate military force.

## Notable Members

- The Hold-Thane — commander and contract-holder of the Company
