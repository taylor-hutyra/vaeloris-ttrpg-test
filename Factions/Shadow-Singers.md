# Shadow-Singers

The Shadow-Singers are a heretical and secretive cult descended from [[Aen'valar]] who became obsessed with the "perfect, logical rhythm" of [[Elaru'n]], the Fifth Tone. They believe the other [[Lainorel|Four Harmonies]] are chaotic and sentimental, and that true power lies in mastering the "music of silence."

## Purpose & Goals

The Shadow-Singers seek to understand and wield the forbidden resonance of [[Elaru'n]]---the disruptive Fifth Tone that caused the [[Alatirne|Dissonance]]. They practice magic that manipulates order, time, and nullity, pursuing what they see as a purer, more logical form of harmony. Their knowledge of [[Kael'Zorai]] may be unparalleled, making them either terrifyingly dangerous allies or devoted servants to his cause.

## Structure

The Shadow-Singers are organized as a **Coven** led by a **Matriarch of Silence** who claims to hear the whispers of the erased god. Membership is secretive and initiation requires mastery of forbidden magical techniques. Their hierarchy is occult and ritualistic, built around deepening communion with the Fifth Tone.

## History

The cult originated among [[Aen'valar]] scholars whose pursuit of the "Unwritten Song" led them too far into forbidden territory. Where mainstream [[Aen'valar]] philosophy seeks to add new harmonies to creation, the Shadow-Singers became convinced that [[Elaru'n]]'s attempt to reshape the World-Song was not a failure but an incomplete masterwork. They retreated to the [[Sunken Coast]] to continue their research in secret.
