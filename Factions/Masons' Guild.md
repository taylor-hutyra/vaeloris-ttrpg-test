# Masons' Guild

An [[Orkin]] trade guild operating within the Orkin Quarter of [[Luminas]], controlling all construction and repair work in the community. Alongside the [[Butchers' Guild]], the Masons' Guild is one of the true economic and political power brokers of the [[Urban Orkin of Luminas|Orkin Quarter]], its leaders often holding more influence than the elders on the Neighborhood Council.

## Purpose & Goals

The Guild controls every wall, roof, and foundation within the Orkin Quarter. In a confined district where space is precious and structures require constant maintenance, this monopoly is immensely powerful. The Guild determines who gets new housing, whose buildings are repaired first, and how the Quarter's limited physical space is allocated.

## Structure

Organized along traditional craft-guild lines with masters, journeymen, and apprentices. [[Orkin]] are renowned for their physical strength and endurance, making them natural builders. The Guild Master holds a seat of influence on the Neighborhood Council, and the Guild's construction crews are the closest thing the Quarter has to an organized labor force.

## History

The [[Gral]]'s legendary strength and the [[Skorn]]'s architectural cunning made the [[Orkin]] natural builders. When confined to the Quarter, the mason families organized into a formal guild that quickly became indispensable. Their ability to build, repair, and expand the Quarter's infrastructure gave them leverage that rivaled even the [[Butchers' Guild]]'s control of food.
