# Crimson Banner

The **Crimson Banner**, commonly known as **The Hawks**, is the political arm of the [[Varnathi]] military establishment and the power base of the [[Consul of Blades]]. They are the most aggressive faction within the [[Pentarchy of Vernath]], advocating for a strong, proactive military posture and the expansion of [[Vernath]]'s influence across [[Vaeloris]].

## Membership

The Crimson Banner draws its membership from ambitious young nobles, military veterans, and industrialists who profit from military contracts. They maintain close ties to the [[Crimson Guard]], the elite Varnathi military force, and have significant support among the [[Pillar Houses]] who favor martial glory.

## Ideology

The Hawks believe the [[Laws of Accord]] imposed by the [[Magocracy]] have made [[Vernath]] weak and subservient. They champion increased military funding, aggressive "border security" measures against the [[Kyn-Clans]], and a return to the imperial ambitions of earlier ages. Their nationalism is rooted in the Varnathi cultural belief that humans carry "fragments of divine will" and are destined to impose order upon the world.

## Political Position

Within the [[Pentarchy]], the Crimson Banner consistently pushes for military expansion and is blocked by the [[Marble Hand]] faction, who fear provoking the [[Magocracy]]. This deadlock defines the current political paralysis of [[Vernath]] as of 412 T.A. Their most prominent champion, [[General Valerius]], is immensely popular with the public after his celebrated campaigns, lending the Hawks dangerous populist momentum.

## Rivals

The Crimson Banner views the [[Marble Hand]] as cowards clinging to a failed peace and the [[Open Palm Movement]] as naive traitors who would dilute Varnathi identity through integration. They regard the [[Mirennian League]] and the [[Pirate Confederacy]] as external threats requiring a military solution.
