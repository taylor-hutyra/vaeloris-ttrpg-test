# Dust-Diggers

A loose confederation of smaller, independent [[Orkin]] prospecting Holds that roam the vast deserts of [[Novaterra]]. The Dust-Diggers are constantly seeking new veins of ore and ancient ruins to plunder, living a nomadic existence on the frontier of the new continent.

## Purpose & Goals

The Dust-Diggers are driven by the promise of what lies beneath the sand. They prospect for mineral wealth, excavate ancient ruins, and trade their findings to the larger Holds and the [[Free Peoples]]. Their independence is fiercely guarded—they answer to no Hold-Thane and resist absorption by the [[Iron-Grit Syndicate]] or any other corporate power.

## Structure

The Dust-Diggers have no central leadership. Each prospecting band operates independently, led by its own captain or elder. They share information through a network of desert waypoints and seasonal gatherings where finds are traded and routes are mapped. Cooperation between bands is common when a major discovery is too large for one group to exploit alone.

## History

Not all [[Orkin]] who came to [[Novaterra]] sought the stability of a Hold. The Dust-Diggers descend from the most restless and independent-minded prospectors—those who found the corporate structure of the [[Iron-Grit Syndicate]] too confining and the military discipline of the [[Shattered-Spear Company]] too rigid. They chose the open desert and the freedom it offers.
