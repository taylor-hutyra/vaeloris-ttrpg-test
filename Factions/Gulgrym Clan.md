# Gulgrym Clan

The **Gulgrym Clan**, known as the **Deep-Miners**, is the most powerful of the Guild Clans within [[Kharnzarak]]. They represent the miners who form the backbone of the Dwarven economy, extracting the ore, gems, and stone that fuel the kingdom's industry and the [[Farraz'ul Clan]]'s wealth.

## Role in Kharnzarak

The Deep-Miners perform the most essential and most dangerous labor in [[Kharnzarak]]. They descend into the lowest shafts, breaking new tunnels into the unknown dark, facing cave-ins, gas pockets, and the remnants of ancient horrors sealed in the deep. Without their toil, the forges of the [[Barazkor Clan]] would go cold and the vaults of the [[Farraz'ul Clan]] would empty.

## Social Position

Despite their critical importance, the Gulgrym occupy a subordinate position in [[Kharnzarak]]'s rigid caste system. As a Guild Clan, they sit below the High Clans in the social hierarchy, with virtually no upward mobility. Their clan leaders hold seats on lesser councils but lack meaningful representation on the [[Stone-Lay Council]], where the High Clans make the decisions that shape their lives.

## Resentment

The Gulgrym are a downtrodden but proud people. Their clan leaders harbor deep resentments toward the High Clans, who profit from Gulgrym labor while offering little in return. The miners see the [[Barazkor Clan]]'s martial glory and the [[Farraz'ul Clan]]'s glittering wealth as monuments built on their broken backs. This simmering anger makes them a potential source of populist unrest within the mountain.

## The Gatestone Question

Below even the Gulgrym are the [[Gatestone Clans]], who perform the most menial and dangerous tasks. The Deep-Miners' relationship with the Gatestone is complex -- they share the burden of hard labor but also benefit from having a caste beneath them. Some Gulgrym leaders advocate for solidarity with the Gatestone; others fear that lifting the lowest would diminish their own precarious status.

## Potential for Change

The Gulgrym represent the greatest internal threat to [[Kharnzarak]]'s status quo. A unified miners' revolt could paralyze the kingdom overnight. The [[Bronr'n Legacy]] recognizes this and quietly courts sympathetic Gulgrym leaders, seeing the Deep-Miners as the indispensable muscle for any future reform movement.
