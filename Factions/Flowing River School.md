# Flowing River School

A minority political faction within the [[Waldkyn]] [[Great Council of the Vales]], the Flowing River School argues that [[The Synthesis]] is a duty not only to the Waldkyn themselves, but to the entire world. Led by the [[Branch-Speaker]], they believe that ignoring the [[Silent Blight]] and the suffering of other peoples is itself a failure of balance.

## Purpose & Goals

The Flowing River School holds that the Waldkyn must engage with the outside world, lending their unique gifts of harmony and balance to heal the growing dissonance. They see isolation as a betrayal of the Synthesis—a refusal to listen to the world's song. They are the only faction within the Vales likely to support foreign diplomatic missions or offer aid to the [[Free Peoples]].

## Structure

The faction is often represented by the [[Branch-Speaker]]—chief diplomat and scout-master within the Triumvirate of Seasons, responsible for observing the outside world. The Branch-Speaker represents the potential and direction of the future. The School draws members from the more outward-looking kiths, particularly [[Faerie]] scouts and [[Katari]] protectors.

## History

As the [[Stillwood Circle]] gained dominance with its isolationist platform, a counter-movement formed among Waldkyn who had witnessed the world's suffering firsthand. The Flowing River School coalesced around the principle that balance requires engagement, not withdrawal—that a tree which refuses to bend in the wind will break.
