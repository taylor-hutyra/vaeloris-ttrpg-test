# Crimson Guard

The **Crimson Guard** is the elite military force of [[Vernath]] and the armed pillar upon which the Varnathi state rests. Answering to the [[Consul of Blades]], they are tasked with defending [[Vernath]]'s borders from external threats including the [[Kyn-Clans]], pirates, and hostile nations.

## Structure

The Crimson Guard operates as both a professional army and a social caste within Varnathi society. Service in the Guard is considered the highest expression of duty to the state, and its officers form a hereditary military class that sits just below the [[Pillar Houses]] in the rigid Varnathi hierarchy. The Guard traces its lineage to the [[Iron-Bound]], the Empire's elite heavy infantry corps of earlier ages.

## Culture

While revered by the public as the embodiment of Varnathi honor and discipline, the Guard's internal culture is known by insiders to be rife with corruption and a sense of entitlement. Officers use their status to secure political favors, and promotion often depends more on family connections than merit. The saying "Measure twice, live once" is their public motto, but behind closed doors, ambition outweighs caution.

## Political Influence

The Crimson Guard is the muscle behind the [[Crimson Banner]] political faction. [[General Valerius]], the Guard's most celebrated commander, is immensely popular with the common people after his frontier campaigns. His push for increased military funding and aggressive border security has made the Guard a political force in its own right, straining the balance of the [[Pentarchy]].

## Notable Campaigns

The Guard maintains garrisons across Varnathi territory, from the walls of [[Vernath]] to the fortress-city of [[Castellum]]. Their campaigns against [[Kyn-Clan]] raiders on the northern border have earned them public adoration but also drawn the Guard away from other strategic concerns.

## Rivals

The [[Magocracy]] views the Crimson Guard with suspicion, seeing an empowered Varnathi military as a threat to the [[Laws of Accord]]. The [[Pirate Confederacy]] and the [[Mirennian League]]'s private fleets represent naval threats the Guard struggles to counter on the open sea.
