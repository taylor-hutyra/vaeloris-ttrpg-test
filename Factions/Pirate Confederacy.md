# Pirate Confederacy

The **Pirate Confederacy of the [[Fractured Isles]]** is a lawless, anarcho-syndicalist confederation of pirate crews operating out of hidden coves in the southern archipelago. United by their hatred of the [[Mirennian League]] and the [[Varnathi]] navy, they are a persistent thorn in the side of every maritime power.

## Government

The Confederacy is governed -- loosely -- by the **Captains' Council**. There is no single ruler. The most feared and respected pirate captains meet irregularly to set the "Code," resolve blood-feuds, and coordinate large-scale raids. Alliances are fleeting and betrayal is common, but the Code provides just enough structure to prevent total self-destruction.

## History

The Confederacy traces its origins to SA:1020, when Captain Vane, a renegade [[Mirennian League|Mirennian]] admiral, united the disparate pirate crews and fortified the island of [[Serpent's Tooth]]. Under his leadership, piracy transformed from a nuisance into a geopolitical power. The Confederacy's boldest action was the six-month blockade of [[Port Farrick]] in SA:1070, broken only when the [[Shadewalkers]] assassinated Vane.

## The Scaled Pact

In SA:2280, the [[Drakona]] High Command approached the pirate lords and offered "Air Cover" -- immunity from navies -- in exchange for intelligence. The pirates agreed, acting as the Drakona's eyes and ears on the surface. This treachery was eventually forgiven during the [[Dragonfall]] when the Allied Command, desperate for ships, offered the [[Privateer Accords]] -- legal status in exchange for fitting flight-cores to their vessels and fighting the Drakona.

## Culture

The Confederacy embodies a brutal meritocracy of violence and cunning. Captains rule by reputation, and crews owe loyalty only to the strongest leader. The legend of Captain Vane inspired a "Golden Age" of romanticized piracy that persists in tavern songs across [[Vaeloris]].

## Rivals

The [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] views the Confederacy as vermin to be eradicated. The [[Varnathi]] navy hunts them relentlessly. The [[Azure Hand]] maintains a complex relationship, sometimes collaborating and sometimes competing for the same illicit trade routes.
