# House Valerion

**House Valerion**, known as the Silent Weavers, is the most powerful and ancient noble house within the concealed Elven forest of [[Hannanórë]]. They are staunch isolationists who dominate the [[Talanvarë Council]] -- the true governing power of [[Cirhannim]] society -- and serve as the primary political force shaping Hannanórë's rigid, unchanging culture.

House Valerion believes that any contact with the outside world, especially the "reckless" [[Aen'valar]], will irrevocably taint the pure society of the Cirhannim. They view the world-song as a sacred, finite composition that must be preserved exactly as it was first played, and they see change as a form of corruption. Their influence over the Talanvarë Council allows them to veto any proposal that might open Hannanórë's borders or introduce foreign ideas.

Within the internal politics of Hannanórë, House Valerion stands as the primary political antagonist to reform. They oppose [[House Silvanus]]'s secret belief that the forest's perfect isolation has become a slow decay, and while they generally align with [[House Lyrandor]]'s warrior caste on matters of defense, they sometimes clash over the Wardens' pragmatic approach to threats that the Council's rigid doctrines cannot address.

As of TA:412, House Valerion's grip on Hannanórë remains firm, though the growing [[Silent Blight]] threatens to challenge their isolationist doctrine. Whether the forest can survive by looking only inward is a question the Silent Weavers refuse to ask.
