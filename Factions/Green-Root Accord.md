# Green-Root Accord

A powerful opposition faction within the [[Waldkyn]] city of [[Nexus]], the Green-Root Accord warns that over-reliance on sterile mechanics is creating an imbalanced, "soulless" song. With strong [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] ties, they champion bio-technology and symbiotic systems, arguing that true progress must always honor the living half of [[The Synthesis]].

## Purpose & Goals

The Accord holds that the [[Gear-Song Harmonists]]' fascination with machines risks turning [[Nexus]] into another [[Gearhaven]]—efficient but spiritually hollow. They advocate for [[Gnomes|Gnomish]]-style approaches: living architecture, symbiotic crop systems, and technology that grows rather than is assembled. Their vision of [[The Great Weaving]] insists that every mechanical thread be matched by an organic one.

## Structure

The Green-Root Accord holds a significant minority of seats on the [[Weaver's Synod]]. Their members tend to be bio-architects, herbalists, and those Waldkyn who work most closely with [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] artisans. The faction maintains particularly strong ties to the [[Forest Gnomes]] and their traditions of living craftsmanship.

## History

As the [[Gear-Song Harmonists]] pushed [[Nexus]] toward ever-deeper integration with technomancy, a counter-movement emerged among Waldkyn who feared the city was losing its connection to living systems. Bolstered by [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] allies who shared their philosophy, the Green-Root Accord became the voice of organic balance within Chartered Kith politics.

## Notable Members

- Various Arch-Weavers and bio-architects aligned with [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] traditions
