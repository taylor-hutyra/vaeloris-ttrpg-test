# Ashfall

A sovereign industrial barony that declared independence from the [[Federation of City-States]] in SA:3100, unveiling [[Necro-Industrialism]]—factories and armies powered by the undead. After repelling three [[Magocracy]] invasions (the dead simply rose to fight again), Ashfall won formal recognition of its sovereignty in the [[Ashfall Compact]] of SA:3250.

## Purpose & Goals

Ashfall exists as the world's dark laboratory—a nation built on the principle that no knowledge is too dangerous and no practice too unethical if it produces results. Its factories run on undead labor, its armies are composed of soldiers who cannot die twice, and its markets trade in technology and research that every other nation officially condemns but secretly purchases.

## Structure

The internal governance of Ashfall is opaque to outsiders. What is known is that the barony operates as a necro-industrial state where the line between the living and the dead is an economic distinction rather than a moral one. The living govern and innovate; the dead labor and fight. This grim pragmatism makes Ashfall both reviled and indispensable.

## History

When the industrial barony seceded from the [[Federation of City-States]] in SA:3100, the [[Magocracy]] moved to crush the rebellion. They invaded three times; each time, Ashfall's undead armies simply reconstituted. The Magocracy was forced to recognize that a war of attrition against the dead was mathematically impossible, leading to the [[Ashfall Compact]] of SA:3250. Ashfall then became the Magocracy's covert supplier of forbidden weapons to fight the [[Grey Rot]].
