# Principality of the Scale

The **Principality of the Scale** is a human political entity born from collaboration with the [[Drakona]] during the [[Dragonfall]]. When the Drakona invaded in SA:2341, they offered the northern [[Varnathi]] provinces an ultimatum: "Serve us, and live better than you did under the corrupt Senate." Many provinces accepted, forming a human nation loyal to the Dragons that fought against the [[Allied Command]].

## History

In SA:2350, the [[Drakona]] established the Principality as a protectorate in the occupied northern territories. Rather than ruling through brute force alone, they offered governance, infrastructure, and order to war-weary populations who had suffered under the corrupt and failing [[Varnathi]] Republic. For many northern commoners, Drakona rule was genuinely preferable to the exploitation of the [[Pillar Houses]] and the [[Shadewalkers]]' shadow government.

## The Permanent Divide

After the [[Treaty of the Broken Peaks]] in SA:2700, the humans who had served the Principality were branded traitors. This created a permanent "North-South" divide in Varnathi culture that persists into the current age. Northerners are called "Dragon-Thralls" by southerners, while southerners are dismissed as "Warmongers" by the north.

## Legacy

The Principality's legacy is complex. On one hand, it represents the darkest chapter of human collaboration with an invading empire. On the other, it birthed a fierce underground resistance movement in the north that persisted long after the [[Drakona]] surrender. These resistance cells evolved into anti-government militias in the [[Third Age]], making the northern provinces a hotbed of separatism and political unrest.

## Current Status

The descendants of the Principality's citizens live under a cloud of inherited shame within the [[Varnathi]] state. They face discrimination, limited political representation, and the constant suspicion of their southern compatriots. Some embrace the stigma defiantly; others work to prove their loyalty through service in the [[Crimson Guard]].

## Rivals

The [[Crimson Banner]] views the northern provinces with undisguised contempt. The [[Marble Hand]] tolerates them as a political reality but restricts their representation. Only the [[Open Palm Movement]] advocates for reconciliation and full integration of the north.
