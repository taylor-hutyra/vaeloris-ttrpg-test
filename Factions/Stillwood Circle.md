# Stillwood Circle

The dominant political faction within the [[Waldkyn]] of the [[Whispering Vales]], the Stillwood Circle advocates total isolation from the outside world. Led by the [[Root-Speaker]]—keeper of laws, history, and traditions—they believe that true harmony can only be preserved by sealing the Vales from the dissonance of [[Single-Forms]] and their ambitions.

## Purpose & Goals

The Stillwood Circle holds that the world's "Song-Wrong" is growing louder—the [[Silent Blight]], expanding human nations, and technomantic innovations all threaten to corrupt the original [[The Synthesis|Synthesis]]. Their solution is radical separation: magical wards to close the Vales permanently. They view diplomatic missions and foreign visitors with deep suspicion, seeing them as harbingers of dissonance.

## Structure

The Stillwood Circle operates within the [[Great Council of the Vales]], where the [[Root-Speaker]] holds one of three executive seats in the Triumvirate of Seasons. The Root-Speaker represents the past and ensures new decisions harmonize with ancient wisdom. The faction draws its membership from the more conservative kiths, particularly traditionalist [[Galapa]] and [[Firbolg]] communities.

## History

The faction crystallized around the central political question facing the [[Quiet Vales Kith]]: how to respond to the growing threats of the outside world. As the [[Silent Blight]] spread and [[Varnathi]] expansion pressured the mainland, the Stillwood Circle's isolationist stance gained majority support within the Council.
