# Urban Orkin of Luminas

The [[Orkin]] community of [[Luminas]], governing themselves through a Neighborhood Council within the segregated Orkin Quarter. Forced into this district by [[Varnathi]] law, they have built a nation-within-a-nation, navigating the prejudices of the outside world while maintaining their own internal structures for survival and governance.

## Purpose & Goals

The Neighborhood Council mediates internal disputes, organizes community defense, and acts as the official liaison to the human city guard. Their primary concern is survival and dignity within a system that treats them as second-class citizens. The [[Charter of Civic Unity]] granted [[Orkin]] "Subject Status," but true equality remains elusive.

## Structure

The council is composed of elders representing the various clans who settled in [[Luminas]]. However, the real power lies with the trade guilds—the [[Butchers' Guild]] (controlling the food supply) and the [[Masons' Guild]] (controlling construction and repair) are the true economic and political power brokers, their leaders often wielding more influence than the elders on the council.

## History

As the [[Magocracy]] pressured the successor states of the [[Varnathi]] Empire to integrate the [[Orkin]], communities of [[Gral]] and [[Skorn]] settled in the cities. Segregated by law and custom, the [[Luminas]] [[Orkin]] built their Quarter into a self-sustaining community, adapting their clan structures to an urban environment.
