# Crystal-Singers of Mórenórë

The Crystal-Singers are a small, wealthy, and highly respected [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] enclave within the free city of [[Mórenórë]]. They are master artisans who craft the vital focusing crystals, harmonic lenses, enchanted instruments, and arcane conduits used by [[Aen'valar]] [[Elves]] and human mages in their research.

## Purpose & Goals

The Crystal-Singers occupy a unique niche: a commercial guild with academic authority. They do not craft machines like the [[Rock Gnomes]] or tend ecosystems like the [[Forest Gnomes]], but instead produce the peerless, essential equipment that makes advanced magical research possible. Their political power derives entirely from being the **sole providers** of this irreplaceable equipment.

## Structure

The Crystal-Singers operate as a **commercial guild with academic authority** within [[Mórenórë]]'s political framework. Their guild has a unique status: neither purely mercantile nor purely scholarly, they function as a neutral, indispensable entity in the city's fractious three-way politics between the [[Aen'valar]] Archon, the Human Guild-Chancellor, and the [[Waldkyn]] Speaker.

## History

Gnomish crystal artisans were drawn to [[Mórenórë]] by its reputation as a beacon of intellectual collaboration and its proximity to both [[Aen'valar]] scholars and human mages. Over time, their unmatched skill in shaping crystals into precise magical instruments earned them an irreplaceable position. No faction in [[Mórenórë]] can afford to antagonize the Crystal-Singers, because no one else can produce what they make.
