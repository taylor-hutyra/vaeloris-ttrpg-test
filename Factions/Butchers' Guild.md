# Butchers' Guild

An [[Orkin]] trade guild operating within the Orkin Quarter of [[Luminas]], controlling the community's food supply. Alongside the [[Masons' Guild]], the Butchers' Guild is one of the true economic and political power brokers of the [[Urban Orkin of Luminas|Orkin Quarter]], its leaders often holding more influence than the elders on the Neighborhood Council.

## Purpose & Goals

The Guild controls the procurement, preparation, and distribution of food within the Orkin Quarter. In a segregated community dependent on its own resources, this monopoly translates directly into political power. The Guild's leaders leverage their control of sustenance to influence community decisions and negotiate with the [[Varnathi]] authorities.

## Structure

The Guild is organized along traditional craft-guild lines, with masters, journeymen, and apprentices. Guild membership is both a trade credential and a political affiliation. The Guild Master sits on the Neighborhood Council by custom if not by formal right, and their voice carries the weight of every family that depends on the Guild for their daily meal.

## History

When the [[Orkin]] of [[Luminas]] were confined to their Quarter, the clans that specialized in animal husbandry, butchery, and food preservation naturally consolidated into a formal guild. Their control of the most essential commodity—food—elevated them from tradespeople to power brokers within a community that could not afford to alienate them.
