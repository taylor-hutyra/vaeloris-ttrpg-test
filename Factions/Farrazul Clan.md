# Farrazul Clan

The **Farrazul Clan**, known as the **Gem-Cutters**, is the wealthiest of the High Clans within [[Kharnzarak]]. Named for [[Farrazul]], the Pillar of Depth and Gem, their power derives not from armies but from their control over the kingdom's vast gem and precious metal mines.

## Economic Power

The Gem-Cutters hold a monopoly on the extraction and refinement of gems, precious metals, and rare crystalline deposits within [[Kharnzarak]]'s deepest mines. This gives them economic leverage that rivals the military authority of the [[Barazkor Clan]] itself. Every gemstone that leaves the mountain, every piece of jewelry worn by the [[Pillar Houses]], and every refined ore that enters the forges passes through Farrazul hands.

## Political Stance

The Farrazul are outwardly traditional but inwardly opportunistic. They maintain the appearance of orthodox adherence to the [[Stone-Lays]] while quietly advocating for more pragmatic policies -- particularly limited trade with the surface world to increase their wealth. They understand that [[Kharnzarak]]'s isolation, while culturally pure, stunts the economic potential of their mines.

## Rivalry with the Barazkor

The Farrazul and the [[Barazkor Clan]] exist in a state of constant, restrained competition. The Iron-Gavels command the military and the law; the Gem-Cutters command the treasury. Neither can move without the other, creating a power dynamic that has defined [[Kharnzarak]]'s internal politics for centuries. The Farrazul are patient rivals, knowing that gold outlasts iron.

## Culture

The clan takes its name from the Dwarven Pillar of Depth and Gem, and their artisans are considered the finest gem-cutters and jewelers in all of [[Vaeloris]]. Their craft halls produce works of breathtaking beauty -- a quiet rebellion against the utilitarian austerity championed by the [[Barazkor Clan]]. The Dwarven saying "N'om zalg'ol" ("Stone does not forget") is their motto, a reminder that wealth, once accumulated, endures.

## Position in the Hierarchy

As one of the High Clans, the Farrazul sit on the [[Stone-Lay Council]] and wield significant influence over the kingdom's economic policy. They maintain cordial relations with the [[Gulgrym Clan]], whose miners extract the raw materials the Gem-Cutters refine, though this relationship is marked by the exploitation common to [[Kharnzarak]]'s rigid caste system.
