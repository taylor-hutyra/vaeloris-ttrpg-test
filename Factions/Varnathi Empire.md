# Varnathi Empire

The **Varnathi Empire** was the dominant human political entity of the early and middle Second Age, forged in SA:381 when **Caelius I** -- great-grandson of [[Vardas the Elder]], founder of [[Vernath]] -- unified the seven major highland city-states through a mix of diplomacy and the War of the Seven Spears. Standing atop the walls of Vernath, he declared the birth of the Empire and claimed a "Mandate of Order" -- that since the gods had left, it was Man's duty to impose structure upon the chaotic world.

The Empire's military strength was built on the Standardization of Steel (SA:395), which produced the [[Iron-Bound]] elite heavy infantry, and on a vast network of paved roads and fortified cities including [[Argentum]], [[Castellum]], and [[Port Farrick]]. The Empire's ambition was boundless: it crushed the [[Orkin]] in the Southern Conquests, attempted to seize Mirennian coastal trade, and later waged a war of extermination in the Barren Hills to fuel the colonial race for [[Novaterra]].

The Empire transitioned to a Republic in SA:581 when the decadent Emperor Titus II was starved of resources by the governors of Argentum, Castellum, and Port Farrick. The Republic functioned as a loose federation of city-states, each with its own identity -- Vernath for philosophy, Argentum for finance, Castellum for military discipline, Port Farrick for trade. But the Republic was increasingly hollowed out by corruption, with the [[Shadewalkers]] espionage guild effectively running the state from the shadows by SA:1920.

The Empire's final chapter came in SA:1950 when the Shadewalkers engineered the Great Betrayal -- leaking forged documents that shattered the Republic into the devastating Succession Wars. From its ashes rose the current [[Varnathi]] Imperial Democracy, the Pentarchy, which carries forward the Empire's legacy of discipline and ambition in a form still riven by internal faction and the memory of past glories.
