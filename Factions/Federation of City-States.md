# Federation of City-States

The **Federation of City-States** is a major political power on the southern mainland of [[Vaeloris]], formed in SA:1730 when the southern city-states united out of fear of both the weakened [[Varnathi]] Republic and the rising Technocracy of [[Gearhaven]]. These cities traced their fierce independence to the legacy of the Vermillion Dynasties, Sorcerer-Kings who had ruled the region centuries earlier before collapsing from magical drought and inbreeding.

The Federation's defining innovation was their "Sword and Spell" military doctrine -- integrating battle-mages directly into infantry squads rather than keeping them as separate units. This approach, born from the [[Arcane Revolution]], made their forces uniquely flexible and dangerous. During the Iron Pact of SA:1880, thirty years of alliance with the [[Varnathi]] Empire against [[Gearhaven]]'s blockade allowed Imperial discipline to mix with Federalist innovation, producing a modernized military that would eventually eclipse the Empire itself.

The Federation joined the [[Allied Command]] during the [[Drakona War]] and contributed its formidable combined-arms forces to the global coalition. After the war, the Federation was one of the sovereign nations recognized under the [[Laws of Accord]], its borders fixed by the [[Magocracy]] alongside the other major powers.

In the current era (TA:412), the Federation remains one of the world's tri-polar powers, balancing against the Varnathi Democracy to the north and the Free Peoples of [[Novaterra]] across the sea. Their military remains among the most effective on the continent, and their culture of pragmatic self-reliance makes them a counterweight to both Imperial ambition and Magocratic control.
