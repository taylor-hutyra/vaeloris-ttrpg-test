# Pirate Confederacy

A lawless, anarcho-syndicalist confederation of pirate crews operating out of hidden coves in the southern [[Fractured Isles]], centered on the fortified island of [[Serpent's Tooth]].

## Government: The Captains' Council

There is no single ruler. The most feared and respected pirate captains meet irregularly to set the "Code," resolve blood-feuds, and coordinate large-scale raids. Alliances are fleeting and betrayal is common, but the crews are united in their hatred of the [[Mirennian League]] and the [[Varnathi Pentarchy|Varnathi]] navy.

## History

### The Golden Age of Piracy
In 1020 S.A., [[Captain Vane]], a renegade [[Mirennians|Mirennian]] admiral, united the disparate pirate crews of the southern archipelago. He established the "Code of the Coast" and fortified [[Serpent's Tooth]]. His fleet grew bold enough to blockade [[Port Farrick]] for six months in 1070 S.A. The siege was broken only when the [[Shadewalkers]] assassinated Vane, fracturing the coalition back into warring crews.

### The Scaled Pact
In 2280 S.A., the [[Drakona]] High Command approached the Pirate Lords. In exchange for air cover and immunity from navies, the Pirates agreed to serve as eyes and ears on the surface. Piracy spiked uncontrollably.

### The Privateer Accords
Desperate for more ships during the [[Drakona]] wars, the Allied Command offered a pardon in 2595 S.A. Pirates who fitted their ships with flight-cores and fought for the Alliance were granted legal status. The resulting "Sky-Privateers" wreaked havoc on Drakona supply lines.

### The Pirate Revival
By 3100 S.A., the grounded Confederacy adapted with "Wave-Skimmers" — extremely fast hydrofoil ships — and early non-magical gliders launched from cliffs to scout prey, challenging the [[Magocracy]]'s trade monopolies.

## Relationship with Other Powers

The [[Azure Hand]] of the [[Mirennian League]] maintains a complex, often collaborative relationship with the Pirate Lords, using them to destabilize their [[Golden Compass Syndicate]] rivals.
