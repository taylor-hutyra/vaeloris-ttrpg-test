# Quiet Vales Great Council

The ancestral home of the [[Waldkyn]] in the [[Whispering Vales]] is governed by a complex, ancient system designed to give voice to every aspect of their diverse people. The Quiet Vales Kith are the keepers of the original Synthesis and believe that true balance comes from harmony with the natural world.

## Government: Triumvirate-led Council

- **The Great Council** — Primary legislative and spiritual body. Composed of eight Speakers, one from each [[Waldkyn]] variant (the [[Faun]] Horn-Speaker, the [[Katari]] Claw-Speaker, the [[Galapa]] Stone-Speaker, etc.). Each is chosen by their peers as the one who best embodies their people's unique "note" in the song.
- **The Triumvirate of Seasons** — Three co-equal leaders holding executive authority, symbolic of a tree's life cycle:
  - **The Root-Speaker** — Keeper of laws, history, and traditions. Represents the past.
  - **The Trunk-Speaker** — Commander of the Vale-Wardens and mediator of disputes. Represents the present.
  - **The Branch-Speaker** — Chief diplomat and scout-master. Represents the future.

## Societal Structure: The Kith-Weave

Society is organized into interwoven villages or "kiths," each typically centered around a dominant sub-race but welcoming to all. Identity is tied to one's kith, sub-race, and role within the greater harmony of the Vales.

## Major Factions

- **The Stillwood Circle (Isolationists)** — Dominant faction, led by the Root-Speaker. They believe the only way to preserve harmony is to magically seal the Vales from the outside world. They view outsiders with deep suspicion.
- **The Flowing River School (Interventionists)** — Minority faction, often represented by the Branch-Speaker. They argue that the Synthesis is a duty to the world, not just to themselves. Ignoring the [[Blight]] is a failure of balance.
- **The Kyn-Clans (Militant Purifiers)** — Dangerous extremist faction on the fringes. They believe balance must be imposed by force, seeking to "prune dissonant branches" from the world.

## Philosophy

The Quiet Vales Kith practice Naturalism — the belief that technology and large-scale civilization are inherently a "Song-Wrong" disrupting the world's harmony. They host the annual Council of the Vales, a spiritual gathering for all [[Waldkyn]], but view their progressive cousins in [[Chartered Kith Weaver's Synod|Nexus]] with concern.
