# Varnathi Pentarchy

The Pentarchy of [[Vernath]] is the governing body of the oldest human power on the mainland. In theory it is a balanced republic led by five co-equal Consuls, each representing a core pillar of Varnathi society. In practice, the system is strained to the breaking point by ambition and fear of a changing world.

## The Five Consuls

- **Consul of Pillars** — Represents the old aristocratic families and internal affairs. The traditionalist heart of the Senate, focused on preserving law, culture, and the status quo.
- **Consul of Coin** — Represents the merchant and artisan guilds. Pragmatic head of the treasury whose vote is most often swayed by economic arguments.
- **Consul of Scrolls** — Represents foreign relations and serves as liaison to the [[Magocracy]]. Chief diplomat tasked with upholding the [[Laws of Accord]].
- **Consul of the People** — Represents the citizenry, managing civic works, food distribution, and public sentiment. A populist position.
- **Consul of Blades** — Supreme commander of the [[Crimson Guard]] and all defensive forces.

## Societal Structure

Varnathi society is highly stratified by perceived "duty" to the state:

1. **The Pillar Houses** — Old aristocratic families dominating the Senate.
2. **The Crimson Guard** — Elite military class, revered publicly but internally rife with corruption.
3. **The Citizenry** — Common merchants, artisans, and farmers.
4. **The Assimilated** — Non-human citizens (primarily [[Orkin]]) granted rights but facing systemic prejudice.

## Major Factions

- **The Crimson Banner (Hawks)** — Political arm of the military and the Consul of Blades. They champion a strong, proactive military and believe the [[Laws of Accord]] have made [[Vernath]] weak. Led by the popular [[General Valerius]].
- **The Marble Hand (Traditionalists)** — Dominant Senate faction controlling the Consulships of Pillars and Scrolls. They believe strict adherence to the [[Magocracy]]'s peace is paramount.
- **The Open Palm Movement (Reformists)** — Smaller faction aligned with the Consul of the People. Advocates for true equality for the Assimilated and unity over military might.

## Current State

As of 412 T.A., the Pentarchy is in near-paralysis. [[General Valerius]], immensely popular after celebrated campaigns, pushes for increased military funding and aggressive border security. He is consistently blocked by the [[Marble Hand]], creating a bitter deadlock with the Consuls of Coin and People caught in the middle.
