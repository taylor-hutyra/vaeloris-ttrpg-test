# Novaterran Hold System

The [[Orkin]] who migrated to [[Novaterra]] have adapted their concept of *Trak* (Endurance) to the new world. Here, endurance is measured not just in battle, but in wealth and influence.

## Government: Corporate Meritocracy

The Novaterran Orkin are organized into powerful "Holds" that function more like mercenary corporations or industrial syndicates than traditional clans.

- **The Hold-Thane** — The CEO-Chieftain of each Hold. Leadership is not hereditary but is won through a ruthless combination of business acumen, strategic alliances, and, when necessary, brutal corporate takeovers (which can be literal).
- **The Board of Captains** — The Thane's council, composed of their most successful lieutenants: the captain of the mining division, the master of the mercenary company, the chief of the prospecting teams.

## Major Holds

- **The [[Iron-Grit Syndicate]]** — The largest and most powerful Hold, controlling vast mining operations and supplying raw materials to [[Gearhaven Cog-Council|Gearhaven]]. Pragmatic capitalists who have largely abandoned old traditions in favor of profit.
- **The [[Shattered-Spear Company]]** — A legendary mercenary Hold known for discipline and effectiveness. They take contracts from any of the Free Peoples, loyalty sold to the highest bidder.
- **The [[Dust-Diggers]]** — A loose confederation of smaller, independent prospecting Holds roaming the vast deserts of [[Novaterra]], seeking new veins of ore and ancient ruins to plunder.

## Philosophy

While still valuing strength and endurance, the Novaterran [[Orkin]] have expanded the definition of *Trak* to include economic power and political savvy. They work as mercenaries, prospectors, and pioneers within the orbit of [[Gearhaven Cog-Council|Gearhaven]] and the other Free Peoples.

## View of Others

They view their mainland cousins in the [[Varr-Darg Diarchy]] with a degree of pity, seeing them as trapped in an ancient, pointless war. They are utterly baffled by the [[Vorok-Varr]] pacifists of [[Aerinórë]], viewing them as a curiosity rather than kin.

## Society

The Holds have replaced the traditional Varr (clan) structure. An [[Orkin]]'s identity is tied not to blood but to their Hold's banner and their professional role within it. This creates a mobile, ambitious society where advancement is possible for any who prove their worth in the harsh economy of the frontier.
