# Chartered Kith Weaver's Synod

The progressive [[Waldkyn]] of [[Novaterra]] built their capital, [[Nexus]], as a testament to their philosophy of "The Great Weaving" — the act of finding balance between the natural and the artificial. Their government reflects this fusion of nature and artifice.

## Government: Technocratic Synod

- **The Weaver's Synod** — Ruling council composed of Arch-Weavers from each of the city's great Spires. A position is earned by creating a masterwork of synthesis — a new form of magitech, a perfectly integrated architectural design, or a revolutionary social policy.
- **The First Weaver** — Elected leader of the Synod. Sets the city's "Loom-Pattern" — the grand, decades-long projects that guide [[Nexus]]'s evolution.

## Major Factions

- **The Gear-Song Harmonists** — Dominant faction, fascinated by the pure logic and efficiency of [[Gearhaven Cog-Council|Gearhaven]]'s [[Clanks]]. They advocate for deeper integration with technomancy, believing the "perfect, predictable rhythm" of machines is a beautiful new note to add to the song.
- **The Green-Root Accord** — Powerful opposition with strong [[Gnomes|Gnomish]] ties. They warn that over-reliance on sterile mechanics creates an imbalanced, "soulless" song. They champion [[Gnomes|Gnomish]]-style bio-technology and symbiotic systems, arguing that true progress must honor the living half of the Synthesis.

## Philosophy: Integration

The Chartered Kith believe the Synthesis is not a static state but an active process. Technology, magic, and urban life are not corruptions, but new, complex notes to be woven into the world's song. Their sacred duty is the *Great Weaving* — finding balance between old and new, natural and artificial.

## The City of Nexus

[[Nexus]] is a marvel of living wood integrated with technomantic architecture. It stands as proof of concept for the Chartered Kith philosophy — that nature and artifice can enhance one another rather than exist in opposition.

## Relations

The Chartered Kith do not attend the Council of the Vales hosted by the [[Quiet Vales Great Council]], seeing it as a relic of a past they have moved beyond. The [[Quiet Vales Great Council|Quiet Vales Kith]] view them as having been seduced by the "loud," imbalanced song of the city.
