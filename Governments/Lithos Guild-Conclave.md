# Lithos Guild-Conclave

Deep within the [[Dragon's Tooth Mountains]] lies [[Lithos]], the greatest city of the [[Rock Gnomes]], a breathtaking subterranean metropolis of silent clockwork, glowing crystals, and geothermal vents.

## Government: Technocratic Guild-Conclave

- **The Guild-Conclave** — A council of nine, where each member is the undisputed master of their respective craft (e.g., the Master Gem-Facer, the Prime Harmonist, the Chief Chronometrist). A seat is held for life or until a peer demonstrably surpasses the master's skill.
- **The Deep Warden** — Leader of the Conclave, chosen from among its members. Their role is not to rule, but to "listen" to the great machine of the world — the grinding of tectonic plates, the hum of leylines — and to direct the Conclave's efforts to maintain its stability.

## Societal Structure: Meritocracy of Precision

Society is rigidly structured around guilds. An individual's worth is measured by the precision and quiet perfection of their work. There is little room for sentiment; a gear that does not turn smoothly is a flaw to be corrected or replaced.

## Internal Factions

- **The Clockwork School (Orthodox)** — Dominant philosophy focused on perfecting pure, silent mechanics. They see the universe as a vast, intricate timepiece. They respect the [[N'om-Khazal'on]] [[Dwarves]] for their mastery of structure but find their methods loud and imprecise.
- **The Resonance School (Heretics)** — Studies the harmonic properties of crystals and metals. They believe true perfection lies in creating perfectly tuned resonances that can influence magic and reality itself. Considered dangerously close to the "chaotic" magic of the Loud-Walkers.

## Philosophy

The [[Rock Gnomes]] see the world as a grand machine of stone and energy. Their sacred duty is to listen for the grinding of the gears and keep the machine running smoothly. They call this philosophy *Precision* — the belief that the finest work is the quietest.

## Relations

The [[N'om-Khazal'on]] [[Dwarves]] know the Rock Gnomes as the respected but elusive *N'om-kith* ("Stone-kin"). It was the Rock Gnomes who secretly aided in weaving the wards around [[Hannan'r']].
