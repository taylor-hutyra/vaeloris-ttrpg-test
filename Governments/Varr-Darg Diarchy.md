# Varr-Darg Diarchy

The harsh, unforgiving [[Barren Hills]] are home to the most traditional and militant [[Orkin]] clans. The Varr-Darg ("Land-Blood Clans") are the keepers of the oldest Orkin traditions, forged by centuries of conflict with expanding human nations like the [[Varnathi Pentarchy|Varnathi]].

## Government: Diarchy of Strength and Cunning

Most Varr-Darg clans are led by a diarchy reflecting the dual nature of [[Grom-Kain]]'s creation:

- **The Gral-Kor (Stone-Hand)** — The warlord of the clan, always a [[Gral]] (Orc). Ultimate authority in matters of war and single combat. Position earned and maintained by physical might and proven battlefield success.
- **The Skorn-Kli (Sharp-Eye)** — The strategic leader, almost always a [[Skorn]] (Goblin). Master tactician, scout, and spymaster responsible for planning raids, managing supplies, and identifying enemy weaknesses. While the Gral-Kor commands the strike, the Skorn-Kli chooses the target.

A clan with a weak Gral-Kor may be strong but foolish. A clan without a clever Skorn-Kli may be strong but starves. The balance is essential.

## Societal Structure: The Varr

The clan is everything. There are no other structures. An individual's worth is measured entirely by their contribution to the clan's survival. Loyalty is absolute. To be exiled is a death sentence.

## Major Factions

- **The Bloodied Fist Clans** — The majority faction. They believe the only path to reclamation is through war. Aggressive and expansionist, they hold deep hatred for the [[Varnathi Pentarchy|Varnathi]]. They are the clans most susceptible to [[Kael'Zorai]]'s call, seeing him as the returned [[Grom-Kain]] who will lead them to righteous victory.
- **The Whisper-Shamans** — Smaller but influential faction of shamans communing with spirits of the land. They argue that suffering is a test of spiritual endurance, not just physical might. Wary of the "new voice" ([[Kael'Zorai]]), sensing its silent, unnatural order is a perversion of [[Grom-Kain]]'s true will.

## Philosophy

The Varr-Darg hold the most fervent belief in the return of [[Grom-Kain]] (the Will-Forge). Their core virtue is *Trak* (Endurance) — the will to survive at any cost. They see the [[Silent Blight]] not as foreign corruption but as their maker's righteous judgment on a world that betrayed them.

### Common Saying

*"Varr trak, or varr grak."* — "Clan endures, or clan dies."
